The **Core Sentinel** is the fifth boss in Starfall. A hovering mechanical construct found at the planet's core, it combines precise laser attacks with periodic shield phases that render it temporarily invulnerable. Patience and timing are just as important as raw damage in this fight.

## How to Summon

The Core Sentinel altar spawns in the **core layer**, between Y coordinates core and the bottom of the world. Look for a purple-tinted altar structure deep in the core.

Stand near the altar and press **F** with the following ingredients:

| Ingredient    | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Carbon Plate  | 5        |
| Titanium Bar  | 4        |
| Diamond       | 3        |

All ingredients are consumed when the boss is summoned.

## Phases

### Phase 1 (100%-60% HP)
- **Speed:** 60
- **Damage:** 40
- **Attack Interval:** 2.5s
- The Sentinel hovers and fires targeted laser beams at the player. It periodically activates a shield that absorbs all damage for a short duration.

### Phase 2 (60%-25% HP)
- **Speed:** 80
- **Damage:** 50
- **Attack Interval:** 1.8s
- The Sentinel becomes more mobile and aggressive. Laser attacks come faster and shield phases become more frequent, shrinking your damage windows.

### Phase 3 (Below 25% HP)
- **Speed:** 100
- **Damage:** 60
- **Attack Interval:** 1.2s
- The Sentinel enters a critical state. It alternates rapidly between attacking and shielding, fires sweeping lasers, and moves at full speed. Precise timing is required to deal damage during brief vulnerable windows.

## Attacks

- **Focused Laser** -- A narrow beam that tracks the player's position. Telegraphed by a targeting reticle that locks on before firing. Move continuously to avoid it.
- **Sweeping Laser** -- In Phase 2 and 3, the Sentinel fires a laser and sweeps it in an arc across the arena. Jump over or duck under the beam.
- **Shield Activation** -- The Sentinel surrounds itself with an energy shield, becoming immune to all damage. The shield lasts several seconds before dropping. In Phase 3, shield cycles become shorter but more frequent.
- **EMP Burst** -- A close-range energy pulse that knocks the player back and deals moderate damage. Triggers if you stay too close for too long.

## Strategy

- **Do not attack during shield phases.** All damage is negated. Save your ammunition and cooldowns for when the shield drops.
- **Learn the shield timing.** Each phase has a rhythm: attack window, then shield, then attack window. In Phase 1, the windows are generous. By Phase 3, they are very short.
- **Dodge lasers by moving laterally.** The focused laser tracks you but has a slight delay. Constant lateral movement keeps you ahead of it.
- **Jump over sweeping lasers.** The sweep goes horizontally. A well-timed jump clears it.
- **Ranged weapons recommended.** The EMP burst punishes melee players who stay close. Ranged attacks let you deal damage from a safe distance.
- **Phase 3 is about patience.** The Sentinel shields frequently but each shield phase is shorter. Wait for openings rather than wasting resources hitting the shield.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Laser Gun, Crystal Staff, or Titanium Sword
- **Armor:** Titanium Armor (full set recommended)
- **Consumables:** 20+ Cooked Meat, Ironskin Potion, Swiftness Potion
- **Accessories:** Star Compass helps track the Sentinel's position; Gravity Belt aids with dodging

## Lore

No one built the Core Sentinel. Or if someone did, they predate every other sign of civilization on this planet by millions of years. It floats in the molten core as if the heat were nothing, its surface unmarked by the ages. Its laser systems are more advanced than anything aboard the crashed ship. Whatever it was built to guard, it has not forgotten its duty.
