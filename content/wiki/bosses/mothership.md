The **Mothership** is the sixth and final standard boss in Starfall. A massive alien vessel that hovers above the surface, it deploys swarms of combat drones and fires devastating energy beams. With four phases, 1500 HP, and the highest damage output of any non-void boss, it is the ultimate test before assembling the jetpack.

## How to Summon

The Mothership altar spawns on the **surface**, between Y coordinates surface-20 and surface+10. Look for a magenta-tinted altar structure on the overworld.

Stand near the altar and press **F** with the following ingredients:

| Ingredient    | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Carbon Plate  | 10       |
| Titanium Bar  | 5        |
| Diamond       | 5        |

All ingredients are consumed when the boss is summoned.

## Phases

### Phase 1 (100%-70% HP)
- **Speed:** 50
- **Damage:** 50
- **Attack Interval:** 3.0s
- The Mothership hovers overhead, deploying drones at regular intervals and occasionally firing a beam weapon. Relatively slow and manageable.

### Phase 2 (70%-40% HP)
- **Speed:** 60
- **Damage:** 60
- **Attack Interval:** 2.5s
- Drone deployment increases. The Mothership begins moving more actively and its beam attacks become more frequent.

### Phase 3 (40%-15% HP)
- **Speed:** 75
- **Damage:** 70
- **Attack Interval:** 1.8s
- The Mothership enters full combat mode. Drones spawn in larger groups, the beam attack sweeps wider, and the ship itself moves aggressively to position above the player.

### Phase 4 (Below 15% HP)
- **Speed:** 90
- **Damage:** 80
- **Attack Interval:** 1.2s
- Desperation mode. The Mothership fires beams almost continuously, drones flood the battlefield, and it reaches its maximum speed. A frantic final push is needed to bring it down.

## Attacks

- **Drone Deployment** -- The Mothership releases combat drones that pursue the player autonomously. Each drone fires small projectiles. If left unchecked, drones will overwhelm you through sheer numbers.
- **Energy Beam** -- A powerful vertical beam fires downward from the Mothership. Telegraphed by a bright targeting indicator on the ground. Deals massive damage on hit.
- **Beam Sweep** -- In Phase 3 and 4, the beam sweeps horizontally across the ground instead of targeting a single point.
- **Tractor Pull** -- The Mothership occasionally activates a tractor beam that slowly pulls the player upward toward it, dealing contact damage if you reach it.

## Drops

| Drop              | Details           |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| Navigation Module  | Jetpack component (ID 185) |
| XP                 | 600               |

The Navigation Module is the sixth and final jetpack component. With all six components, visit a Fusion Station to assemble the Jetpack.

## Strategy

- **Kill drones immediately.** This is the single most important rule. Every drone you leave alive adds another source of projectile damage. Prioritize clearing drones over damaging the Mothership.
- **Beam attacks are telegraphed.** Watch the ground for the bright targeting indicator and dodge sideways. The beam itself is narrow.
- **Beam sweeps require jumping.** In later phases, the sweep goes horizontally at ground level. Jump over it.
- **Spread out your healing.** With four phases, this is a long fight. Do not burn all your consumables in the first two phases.
- **Build cover.** Before summoning, build a few tall pillars or walls near the altar. You can duck behind them to break line of sight with drones and avoid beams.
- **Phase 4 is pure chaos.** At 80 damage with 1.2s attacks and constant drone spawns, you need to finish the fight quickly. Save your strongest consumables (Ironskin, Swiftness) for this phase.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Laser Gun, Titanium Sword, or Crystal Staff (AoE helps with drones)
- **Armor:** Titanium Armor or Carbon Fiber Armor (full set)
- **Consumables:** 30+ Cooked Meat, Ironskin Potion, Swiftness Potion, Night Owl Potion
- **Accessories:** Cloud Boots for dodging, Lucky Charm for damage boost
- **Tip:** An AoE weapon or summon weapon (Drone Totem, Swarm Beacon) is extremely valuable for clearing drone swarms while you focus fire on the Mothership.

## Lore

The Mothership was already in orbit when you crashed. Your ship's black box recorded a brief transmission just before impact: a single pulse on an unknown frequency, followed by the engine failure that brought you down. Coincidence? The Mothership does not care about your theories. It descends from the clouds with a mechanical scream, its hull bristling with weapons platforms, and the sky fills with drones. Whatever brought you to this planet, it intends to make sure you never leave.
