# Bosses

Starfall features six main bosses and one post-game true final boss. Each main boss guards a jetpack component needed to escape the planet. Bosses are summoned at altars found throughout the world by pressing **F** with the required ingredients in your inventory.

## Boss Progression

Bosses are designed to be fought in a specific order. Each one is found deeper in the world and requires stronger gear than the last.

## How Summoning Works

1. Explore the correct world layer to find the boss altar (altars generate during world creation).
2. Gather the required ingredients listed on each boss page.
3. Stand near the altar and press **F**.
4. The ingredients are consumed and the boss spawns nearby.

## Jetpack Assembly

After defeating the first six bosses, you will have all six jetpack components:

- Fuel Cell Casing
- Thrust Regulator
- Pressure Valve
- Energy Capacitor
- Ignition Core
- Navigation Module

Bring all six to a **Fusion Station** to assemble the **Jetpack**. Equip it and hold **Space** while airborne to fly. Reach the top of the world to trigger the ending and escape the planet.

## The True Final Boss

After escaping and returning to the planet, craft a **Super Portal** at a Fusion Station to access the **Void Dimension**. Explore the void, gather materials, and craft a **Void Lord Summon Token** at the **Void Forge**. Return to the overworld and use the token to summon [The Void Lord](/wiki/bosses/void-lord), the true final boss of Starfall.
