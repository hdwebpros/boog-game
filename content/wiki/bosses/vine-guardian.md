The **Vine Guardian** is the first boss in Starfall. A massive, animated tangle of roots and vines, it has claimed dominion over a patch of the surface world and fiercely attacks anything that approaches its altar. Though it is the weakest boss, careless players will find themselves overwhelmed by its vine lashes and slug minions.

## How to Summon

The Vine Guardian altar spawns on the **surface layer**, between Y coordinates surface-20 and surface+30. Look for a green-tinted altar structure while exploring above ground.

Stand near the altar and press **F** with the following ingredients in your inventory:

| Ingredient   | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Wood         | 10       |
| Leaves       | 20       |
| Plant Fiber  | 5        |

All ingredients are consumed when the boss is summoned.

## Phases

### Phase 1 (100%-50% HP)
- **Speed:** 60
- **Damage:** 15
- **Attack Interval:** 2.0s
- The Vine Guardian patrols along the ground, lashing out with vine attacks and periodically summoning Space Slugs to aid it.

### Phase 2 (Below 50% HP)
- **Speed:** 80
- **Damage:** 20
- **Attack Interval:** 1.5s
- The Guardian becomes enraged. It moves faster, hits harder, and attacks more frequently. Slug summons continue.

## Attacks

- **Vine Lash** -- A sweeping vine strike that hits players at close range. Telegraphed by a brief wind-up animation.
- **Slug Summon** -- The Guardian periodically spawns Space Slugs that crawl toward the player. These can pile up if not dealt with.
- **Ground Pound** -- In Phase 2, the Guardian occasionally slams the ground, sending out a short-range shockwave.

## Drops

| Drop              | Details           |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| Fuel Cell Casing   | Jetpack component (ID 180) |
| XP                 | 100               |

The Fuel Cell Casing is the first of six jetpack components needed to assemble the Jetpack at a Fusion Station.

## Strategy

- **Stay mobile.** The Vine Guardian is slow in Phase 1, so kiting it is straightforward. Circle around it and attack between its vine lashes.
- **Clear slugs immediately.** If you ignore the summoned Space Slugs, they will surround you and make dodging the Guardian's attacks much harder.
- **Phase 2 warning.** When the Guardian drops below 50% HP, back off briefly to adjust to its increased speed before re-engaging.
- **Melee works well.** A Wood or Stone Sword is sufficient. Time your swings between its 2-second attack windows.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Stone Sword or better
- **Armor:** Wood Armor (full set recommended)
- **Consumables:** 5-10 Healing Herbs
- **Tip:** Craft a few extra Healing Herbs before the fight. The boss is not hard, but slug chip damage adds up.

## Lore

Deep within the tangled overgrowth of the planet's surface, the Vine Guardian stirs. It is said to be the forest's immune response -- a living amalgamation of roots and thorns that rises when the land senses an intruder. Crash-landed astronauts are exactly the kind of foreign body it was made to reject.
