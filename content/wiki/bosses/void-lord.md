**The Void Lord** is the true final boss of Starfall. A towering 128x192 pixel entity of pure void energy, it dwarfs every other creature in the game. With 5000 HP, four brutal phases, and an arsenal of reality-warping attacks -- void beams, dimensional rifts, minion summoning, and teleportation -- the Void Lord is the ultimate challenge. Defeating it triggers the True Ending.

## How to Summon

The Void Lord cannot be fought until you have completed the following:

1. **Beat the game** by assembling the Jetpack and escaping the planet.
2. **Craft a Super Portal** at a Fusion Station (99 Obsidian, 40 Arcane Dust, 30 Titanium Bars).
3. **Visit the Void Dimension** through the Super Portal.
4. **Craft a Void Lord Summon Token** (ID 370) at a **Void Forge** in the Void Dimension:

| Material            | Quantity |
|---------------------|----------|
| Chaos Shard         | 10       |
| Dimensional Fabric  | 3        |
| Abyssal Ingot       | 5        |

5. **Return to the overworld** and use the Summon Token. The Void Lord spawns in the original world.

## Phases

### Phase 1 -- Awakening (100%-75% HP)
- **Speed:** 100
- **Damage:** 80
- **Attack Interval:** 2.0s
- The Void Lord materializes and attacks with void beams and basic melee strikes. It teleports periodically to reposition. This is the most forgiving phase, but 80 base damage is still punishing.

### Phase 2 -- Dominion (75%-50% HP)
- **Speed:** 120
- **Damage:** 100
- **Attack Interval:** 1.6s
- The Void Lord opens dimensional rifts that spawn void minions. Void beams become wider and teleportation is more frequent. Managing minions while dodging beams is the central challenge.

### Phase 3 -- Fury (50%-25% HP)
- **Speed:** 140
- **Damage:** 120
- **Attack Interval:** 1.2s
- All attacks intensify. Dimensional rifts spawn faster, void beams track the player more aggressively, and the Void Lord moves at 140 speed. The damage output at this stage can kill an unprepared player in two or three hits.

### Phase 4 -- Enrage (Below 25% HP)
- **Speed:** 160
- **Damage:** 150
- **Attack Interval:** 0.8s
- The Void Lord enrages. It attacks nearly every second for 150 damage, teleports constantly, and floods the arena with minions and void beams simultaneously. This is the hardest phase of any boss in the game. Pure endurance and preparation determine survival.

## Attacks

- **Void Beam** -- A massive beam of void energy fired in a line toward the player. In Phase 1 it is narrow and telegraphed. By Phase 3 it is wider and tracks the player's movement.
- **Dimensional Rift** -- The Void Lord tears open a rift in reality that spawns void minions (Void Wraiths, Shadow Stalkers). Active rifts persist for several seconds, continuously spawning enemies.
- **Minion Summon** -- Directly calls forth void creatures to aid it. In Phase 4, the Void Lord can have many minions active simultaneously.
- **Teleportation** -- The Void Lord vanishes and reappears at a new position, often directly beside or behind the player. Frequency increases with each phase.
- **Void Slam** -- A devastating close-range attack where the Void Lord strikes the ground, creating a shockwave of void energy in all directions.
- **Reality Distortion** -- In Phase 4, the Void Lord warps the space around it, pulling the player inward while dealing continuous damage.

## Strategy

- **Full void armor is non-negotiable.** Without Void Armor, the Void Lord's attacks will kill you in one or two hits even in Phase 1.
- **Bring your best void weapons.** The Abyssal Scythe, Chaos Edge, or Soul Reaver are ideal. You need maximum damage output to push through 5000 HP before you run out of healing.
- **Stock up on potions.** Bring Wrath Potions (damage boost), Forcefield Potions (damage reduction), Swiftness Potions (movement speed), and as many healing items as you can carry.
- **Kill minions selectively.** In Phase 2 and 3, do not try to kill every minion. Focus on the Void Lord and only clear minions that are directly threatening you.
- **Phase 4 is a DPS check.** At 150 damage with 0.8s attacks and 160 speed, you cannot survive Phase 4 for long. Your goal is to have the Void Lord as close to 25% HP as possible before Phase 4 begins, then burn it down with everything you have.
- **Teleportation counter.** When the Void Lord teleports, it often appears behind you. Keep moving and do not stand still after it vanishes.
- **Use artifacts.** If you have upgraded artifacts from the Sky Merchant, equip the best ones. Every stat advantage matters in this fight.
- **Build an arena.** Before summoning, build a flat open area with walls on the sides. This gives you room to dodge beams and prevents minions from approaching from all angles.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Chaos Edge, Abyssal Scythe, Soul Reaver, or Void Staff (best available void weapons)
- **Armor:** Void Armor (full set, mandatory)
- **Consumables:** Wrath Potion, Forcefield Potion, Swiftness Potion, 30+ best healing items
- **Accessories:** Celestial Cape, upgraded Artifacts (Tier 3 if possible)
- **Tip:** This fight will likely take multiple attempts. Learn the Phase 1 and 2 patterns before focusing on survival in Phase 3 and 4.

## Lore

The Void Lord is not from this world, nor from any world. It exists in the spaces between dimensions, a consciousness older than the stars that burn above the planet's sky. When you opened the Super Portal, you did not simply travel to the Void Dimension -- you announced your presence to its ruler. The Void Lord does not invade. It does not conquer. It simply is, and everything else is permitted to exist only so long as it does not draw attention. You drew attention. Now the Void Lord has crossed into your reality, a towering monument of absolute power, and it intends to unmake the intruder who dared walk its domain. There is no negotiation. There is no retreat. There is only the fight.
