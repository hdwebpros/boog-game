The **Crystal Golem** is the third boss in Starfall. An enormous construct of living stone and crystal, it guards the underground caverns with devastating slam attacks and showers of razor-sharp crystal shards. With three phases and 800 HP, it represents a significant step up in difficulty from the first two bosses.

## How to Summon

The Crystal Golem altar spawns in the **underground layer**, between Y coordinates underground and deep underground. Look for a cyan-tinted altar structure in the caverns.

Stand near the altar and press **F** with the following ingredients:

| Ingredient   | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Iron Bar     | 6        |
| Diamond Ore  | 4        |
| Glass        | 3        |

All ingredients are consumed when the boss is summoned.

## Phases

### Phase 1 (100%-60% HP)
- **Speed:** 40
- **Damage:** 30
- **Attack Interval:** 3.0s
- The Golem lumbers slowly, slamming the ground and occasionally summoning a rain of crystals from above. Its slow speed makes it easy to kite, but each hit is punishing.

### Phase 2 (60%-30% HP)
- **Speed:** 55
- **Damage:** 40
- **Attack Interval:** 2.2s
- The Golem sheds its outer layer, revealing a more agile inner form. Attack frequency increases and crystal rain becomes more dense.

### Phase 3 (Below 30% HP)
- **Speed:** 70
- **Damage:** 50
- **Attack Interval:** 1.5s
- The Golem enters a frenzy. It moves nearly twice as fast as Phase 1, slams constantly, and crystal rain covers a wide area. This is the most dangerous phase.

## Attacks

- **Ground Slam** -- The Golem raises both arms and smashes the ground, dealing heavy damage in a radius around it. Telegraphed by a slow wind-up.
- **Crystal Rain** -- Crystals fall from the ceiling above the player's position. The affected area is marked briefly before impact. In later phases, more crystals fall over a wider area.
- **Charge** -- In Phase 2 and 3, the Golem occasionally charges forward a short distance, dealing damage on contact.
- **Crystal Spray** -- In Phase 3, the Golem fires a fan of crystal shards in front of it at close range.

## Strategy

- **Dodge crystal rain by moving sideways.** The crystals target your position when they spawn, so a quick sidestep avoids them entirely.
- **Phase 1 is your damage window.** The Golem is extremely slow at 40 speed. Get in as much damage as possible before Phase 2 speeds things up.
- **Phase 3 is dangerous.** At 70 speed with 50 damage and 1.5s attacks, the Golem becomes relentless. Save your best consumables for this phase.
- **Use the environment.** Underground tunnels can funnel the Golem, making its charges predictable. Just make sure you have room to dodge crystal rain.
- **Ironskin Potion** is highly recommended for Phase 3 to survive the increased damage output.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Iron Sword or Diamond Sword (melee), or Apprentice Staff (magic)
- **Armor:** Iron Armor (full set)
- **Consumables:** 15+ Cooked Meat or Healing Herbs, Ironskin Potion
- **Accessories:** Cloud Boots help with dodging crystal rain

## Lore

The underground caverns are not natural. Long ago, something carved them out -- or grew them. The Crystal Golem is the oldest inhabitant of the deep, a primordial fusion of mineral and intent. Its crystalline body hums with a low frequency that makes the walls shiver. It does not think, not exactly, but it remembers. And it remembers that this territory is its own.
