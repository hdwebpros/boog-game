The **Magma Wyrm** is the fourth boss in Starfall. A flying serpentine creature wreathed in flame, it weaves through the deep underground at terrifying speeds, strafing players with fire breath and diving from above. Its aerial mobility makes it one of the most challenging fights up to this point.

## How to Summon

The Magma Wyrm altar spawns in the **deep underground layer**, between Y coordinates deep underground and core. Look for an orange-red altar structure near lava formations.

Stand near the altar and press **F** with the following ingredients:

| Ingredient    | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Diamond       | 5        |
| Titanium Bar  | 3        |
| Iron Bar      | 4        |

All ingredients are consumed when the boss is summoned.

## Phases

### Phase 1 (100%-50% HP)
- **Speed:** 80
- **Damage:** 35
- **Attack Interval:** 2.0s
- The Wyrm flies in sweeping patterns, periodically breathing fire at the player and performing dive attacks.

### Phase 2 (50%-20% HP)
- **Speed:** 110
- **Damage:** 45
- **Attack Interval:** 1.5s
- The Wyrm accelerates dramatically. Fire breath attacks become longer and dives are more frequent. The speed increase from 80 to 110 is immediately noticeable.

### Phase 3 (Below 20% HP)
- **Speed:** 140
- **Damage:** 55
- **Attack Interval:** 1.0s
- The Wyrm reaches its maximum speed of 140 and attacks every second. Fire breath is nearly continuous. This phase is a frantic race to finish the boss before it overwhelms you.

## Attacks

- **Fire Breath** -- The Wyrm breathes a stream of fire in a cone toward the player. The flame lingers briefly on the ground. In later phases the cone is wider and lasts longer.
- **Dive Attack** -- The Wyrm ascends, pauses briefly, then dives at the player's position at high speed. Deals heavy contact damage.
- **Tail Whip** -- If the player is directly behind or beside the Wyrm, it lashes with its tail for moderate damage.
- **Lava Trail** -- In Phase 3, the Wyrm leaves a short-lived trail of fire along its flight path.

## Strategy

- **Ranged and magic weapons are essential.** The Wyrm flies, making melee extremely difficult. A bow or staff lets you hit it reliably.
- **Watch for dive telegraphs.** The Wyrm pauses in the air before diving. Move sideways immediately when you see it hover.
- **Fire breath has a pattern.** The Wyrm faces you before breathing fire. If it turns toward you and slows down, move perpendicular to its facing.
- **Phase 3 is a DPS race.** At 140 speed with 1-second attacks, you cannot dodge forever. Burn through the last 20% HP as fast as possible.
- **Clear the arena.** Before summoning, mine out a large open area near the altar. The Wyrm's flight patterns need space, and tight tunnels make dodging dives nearly impossible.

## Recommended Gear

- **Weapon:** Iron Bow, Crystal Staff, or Diamond Sword (ranged/magic strongly preferred)
- **Armor:** Diamond Armor (full set)
- **Consumables:** 20+ Cooked Meat, Ironskin Potion, Swiftness Potion
- **Tip:** A Swiftness Potion is almost mandatory for Phase 3. The speed boost helps you keep up with the Wyrm's 140 speed.

## Lore

The deep underground was once a network of magma tubes, carved by rivers of molten rock over millennia. The Magma Wyrm is the creature that carved them. Born from the planet's molten heart, it has circled these tunnels for ages, a perpetual engine of flame and fury. Its scales glow white-hot, and the air itself ignites in its wake.
