# Skills

Starfall features a skill tree system that lets you customize your character as you level up. Earn XP by killing enemies, spend skill points to unlock passive abilities across five base branches, three super trees, and an ascension branch.

## How It Works

1. **Earn XP** by defeating enemies. Each enemy type gives different XP.
2. **Level up** when your XP reaches the threshold: **50 x N^2** (where N is the next level). Level 2 needs 200 XP, level 3 needs 450 XP, and so on.
3. **Spend skill points** -- each level grants 1 skill point.
4. Press **TAB** to open the skill tree. Click a skill to unlock it if you have enough points and meet the prerequisites.

## Skill Tree Structure

```
              Combat ──────┐
                           ├──► Warlord (super tree)
              Survival ────┘

              Combat ──────┐
                           ├──► Spellblade (super tree)
              Magic ───────┘

              Mining ──────┐
                           ├──► Trailblazer (super tree)
              Mobility ────┘

         2+ super trees completed
                  │
                  ▼
             Ascension
                  │
                  ▼
         Paragon (infinite)
```

## Branches Overview

| Branch | Color | Theme | Skills | Max Points |
|--------|-------|-------|--------|------------|
| [Combat](/wiki/skills/combat) | Red | Melee damage, attack speed, crits | 5 | 9 |
| [Survival](/wiki/skills/survival) | Green | Max HP, defense, healing | 4 | 6 |
| [Mining](/wiki/skills/mining) | Orange | Mine speed, drops, range | 4 | 6 |
| [Magic](/wiki/skills/magic) | Blue | Mana, magic damage, mana shield | 4 | 6 |
| [Mobility](/wiki/skills/mobility) | Yellow | Move speed, double jump, jetpack | 4 | 6 |

## Super Trees

Super trees unlock when **both parent branches are fully maxed**. They contain the most powerful skills in the game.

| Super Tree | Parents | Color | Theme |
|------------|---------|-------|-------|
| [Warlord](/wiki/skills/super-trees) | Combat + Survival | Orange | Lifesteal, death prevention |
| [Spellblade](/wiki/skills/super-trees) | Combat + Magic | Purple | Melee shockwaves, mana burst |
| [Trailblazer](/wiki/skills/super-trees) | Mining + Mobility | Gold | 3x3 mining, flash movement |

## Ascension

The [Ascension](/wiki/skills/ascension) branch unlocks after completing at least **2 of the 3 super trees**. It contains 6 skills costing 5 to 15 points each, designed for void dimension content.

## Paragon

The [Paragon](/wiki/skills/paragon) system activates after all skills are unlocked. Every skill point earned becomes a paragon point, providing infinite scaling across 5 stat categories.

## Tips

- You will not have enough skill points to max everything early on. Pick one or two branches that match your playstyle and focus on those first.
- Super trees are gated behind maxing both parent branches. Plan ahead if you want a specific super tree.
- The Ascension branch is post-game content. Do not worry about it until you have cleared the Void Dimension.
- Paragon points provide marginal but unlimited scaling. They matter most in extended endgame play.
