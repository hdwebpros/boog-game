# Ascension Skills

The **Ascension** branch is the final skill tier in Starfall. It unlocks after completing at least **2 of the 3 super trees** (Warlord, Spellblade, Trailblazer). These skills are designed for the Void Dimension and the true final boss, with extremely high point costs and godlike effects.

## Unlock Requirement

Complete any 2 of the following super trees:
- Warlord (Combat + Survival, 7 points)
- Spellblade (Combat + Magic, 7 points)
- Trailblazer (Mining + Mobility, 7 points)

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Void Strike | 5 | 5 | +100% damage against void enemies | 2 super trees completed |
| Soul Harvest | 5 | 5 | Kills heal 5% of max HP | 2 super trees completed |
| Dimensional Shift | 5 | 5 | 20% chance to dodge any attack | 2 super trees completed |
| Chaos Mastery | 5 | 8 | +50% all damage | Void Strike + Soul Harvest |
| Void Armor | 5 | 8 | -30% all damage taken | Dimensional Shift |
| Ascendant | 5 | 15 | +25% to all stats | Chaos Mastery + Void Armor |

**Total points to max: 46**

## Skill Details

### Void Strike (Tier 5, 5 pts)
Doubles your damage against all enemies flagged as void-type. This includes all 8 void dimension enemies and the Void Lord boss. Essential for making void content manageable.

### Soul Harvest (Tier 5, 5 pts)
Every enemy kill restores 5% of your maximum HP. Combined with the large groups of enemies in the void dimension, this provides strong sustain without potions.

### Dimensional Shift (Tier 5, 5 pts)
Gives a flat 20% chance to completely avoid any incoming attack. Dodged attacks deal zero damage. This is a separate calculation from defense and stacks with all other defensive skills.

### Chaos Mastery (Tier 5, 8 pts)
A massive +50% bonus to all damage types (melee, ranged, magic, summon). This stacks multiplicatively with other damage bonuses, making your total damage output enormous.

### Void Armor (Tier 5, 8 pts)
Reduces all incoming damage by 30%. This is calculated after defense, so it stacks well with Iron Body and armor. Combined with Dimensional Shift's dodge chance, you become extremely hard to kill.

### Ascendant (Tier 5, 15 pts)
The capstone skill of the entire skill tree. Grants +25% to all stats: damage, defense, HP, mana, speed, mining speed. This is the single most expensive skill in the game and the ultimate power boost.

## Skill Progression Path

```
Void Strike ────┐
                ├──► Chaos Mastery ──┐
Soul Harvest ───┘                    ├──► Ascendant
                                     │
Dimensional Shift ──► Void Armor ────┘
```

The three entry skills (Void Strike, Soul Harvest, Dimensional Shift) can be taken in any order. Chaos Mastery and Void Armor each require their respective prerequisites. Ascendant requires both Chaos Mastery and Void Armor.

## Tips

- **Void Strike** should be your first Ascension skill if you are heading into the Void Dimension. Doubling your damage against void enemies is transformative.
- The total cost of the Ascension branch is 46 points. You will need to be a high level to afford all of these on top of the base branches and super trees.
- **Ascendant** costs 15 points on its own. Only invest in it after you have everything else you need. It is a luxury, not a necessity.
- **Soul Harvest** stacks with Second Wind and Blood Oath for triple on-kill healing. Combined, you heal a massive percentage of your HP with every kill.
- **Dimensional Shift** is especially powerful against the Void Lord's high-damage attacks. A 20% dodge chance can save your life multiple times during the fight.
