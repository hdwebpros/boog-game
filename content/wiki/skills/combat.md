# Combat Skills

The **Combat** branch (red) is the offensive melee skill line. It boosts raw damage output, attack speed, and adds critical hit chance. Combat is one of the two parent branches for the **Warlord** and **Spellblade** super trees, making it the most versatile branch to invest in.

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Sharp Edge | 1 | 1 | +20% melee damage | None |
| Quick Draw | 1 | 1 | +20% attack speed | None |
| Power Strike | 2 | 2 | +30% melee damage | Sharp Edge |
| Critical Eye | 2 | 2 | 15% critical hit chance | Quick Draw |
| Berserker | 3 | 3 | +50% all damage when below 30% HP | Power Strike |

**Total points to max: 9**

## Skill Details

### Sharp Edge (Tier 1)
A straightforward 20% boost to melee damage. This applies to all melee weapons including swords and scythes. Pick this up first if you are using melee as your primary weapon style.

### Quick Draw (Tier 1)
Increases attack speed by 20%, meaning faster swings and more hits per second. This affects all weapon types, not just melee. A strong first pick for any build.

### Power Strike (Tier 2)
Stacks with Sharp Edge for a total of +50% melee damage. At this point, melee weapons start dealing serious damage even against tougher underground enemies.

### Critical Eye (Tier 2)
Adds a 15% chance for any attack to deal double damage. Critical hits are shown as larger damage numbers. This applies to all weapon styles, making it valuable even for ranged or magic builds.

### Berserker (Tier 3)
A risk-reward skill that gives +50% damage to all attacks when your HP drops below 30%. Extremely powerful in boss fights when combined with lifesteal from the Warlord super tree.

## Recommended Builds

- **Pure melee**: Sharp Edge -> Power Strike -> Berserker, then Quick Draw -> Critical Eye. Maximizes melee damage as fast as possible.
- **Hybrid damage**: Quick Draw -> Critical Eye first. The attack speed and crit chance benefit all weapon styles, not just melee.
- **Rush Warlord**: Max all 5 skills as fast as possible to unlock the Warlord super tree (also requires maxed Survival).

## Synergies

- **Survival**: Maxing both unlocks the **Warlord** super tree. Berserker's low-HP bonus pairs well with Survival's defensive skills to survive the danger zone.
- **Magic**: Maxing both unlocks the **Spellblade** super tree. Critical Eye benefits magic damage, and Spellblade adds melee shockwaves.
- **Mobility**: Swift Feet keeps you mobile in combat. Double Jump helps dodge boss attacks while staying in melee range.
