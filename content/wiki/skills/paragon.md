# Paragon System

The **Paragon** system is Starfall's infinite progression mechanic. Once every skill in all branches (base, super, and ascension) has been unlocked, each subsequent skill point earned from leveling up becomes a **paragon point**. Paragon points can be spent across five stat categories for unlimited incremental power growth.

## How It Works

1. Unlock every skill in the game (all 5 base branches, all 3 super trees, and the full Ascension branch).
2. Continue killing enemies and earning XP to level up.
3. Each level still grants 1 skill point, but it is now a **paragon point**.
4. Open the skill tree (press **TAB**) and allocate paragon points to any of the five categories.

## Paragon Categories

| Category | Effect per Point | Best For |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| Vitality | +5 HP, +1 defense | Tanks, survival |
| Power | +1% all damage | All damage builds |
| Industry | +2% mining speed, +3 mana | Miners, mages |
| Agility | +0.5% movement speed | Explorers, speedrunners |
| Evasion | +1% dodge chance | Dodge-focused builds |

## Scaling

Paragon points have no cap. You can invest as many as you earn into any category. The bonuses are small per point but stack infinitely:

- 10 Vitality points = +50 HP, +10 defense
- 10 Power points = +10% all damage
- 10 Industry points = +20% mining speed, +30 mana
- 10 Agility points = +5% movement speed
- 10 Evasion points = +10% dodge chance

## Tips

- **Power** is the most universally useful category. Every build benefits from more damage.
- **Vitality** is the safest choice if you are struggling with endgame content. The defense and HP add up quickly.
- **Evasion** has diminishing psychological returns but the math is linear -- going from 20% to 30% dodge is just as valuable as going from 0% to 10%.
- **Industry** is a split category. If you are a pure mage, the extra mana is valuable. If you are done mining, the mining speed is wasted. Choose accordingly.
- **Agility** gives the smallest per-point bonus. Only invest here if you already have Flash Step and want maximum movement speed.
- There is no way to respec paragon points, so choose carefully. However, the individual per-point impact is small enough that no single allocation decision is catastrophic.
