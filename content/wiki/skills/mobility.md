# Mobility Skills

The **Mobility** branch (yellow) is the movement skill line. It makes you faster, reduces fall damage, grants a double jump, and improves jetpack fuel efficiency. Mobility is a parent branch for the **Trailblazer** super tree alongside Mining.

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Swift Feet | 1 | 1 | +20% movement speed | None |
| Feather Fall | 1 | 1 | -60% fall damage | None |
| Double Jump | 2 | 2 | Perform a second jump mid-air | Swift Feet |
| Fuel Saver | 2 | 2 | -40% jetpack fuel consumption | Feather Fall |

**Total points to max: 6**

## Skill Details

### Swift Feet (Tier 1)
A 20% boost to horizontal movement speed. You walk and run faster, which makes everything from exploration to combat repositioning smoother. One of the best quality-of-life skills in the game.

### Feather Fall (Tier 1)
Reduces fall damage by 60%. This is a lifesaver when exploring vertical cave systems. Long drops that would kill you instead leave you with most of your health.

### Double Jump (Tier 2)
Grants a second jump while airborne. Press **Space** or **W** again after your initial jump to perform a mid-air jump. This dramatically improves your vertical mobility and makes platforming sections trivial. Also useful for dodging boss attacks.

### Fuel Saver (Tier 2)
Reduces jetpack fuel consumption by 40%. Once you have the Jetpack assembled, this skill lets you fly much further on a single fuel bar. Essential for reaching the top of the world and for extended flight in the overworld.

## Recommended Builds

- **Explorer**: Swift Feet -> Double Jump first. Maximizes your ability to navigate the world quickly and reach difficult areas.
- **Jetpack focus**: Feather Fall -> Fuel Saver. Prioritizes flight efficiency for late-game content where the jetpack is your primary movement tool.
- **Rush Trailblazer**: Max all 4 skills to unlock the Trailblazer super tree (also requires maxed Mining).

## Synergies

- **Mining**: Maxing both unlocks the **Trailblazer** super tree. Swift Feet and Double Jump let you navigate cave systems efficiently while mining.
- **Combat**: Double Jump is excellent for dodging boss attacks while staying in melee range. Swift Feet helps with kiting ranged enemies.
- **Survival**: Feather Fall combined with Tough Skin means falls are almost never dangerous. Good for aggressive cave exploration.
