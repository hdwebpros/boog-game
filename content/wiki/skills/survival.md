# Survival Skills

The **Survival** branch (green) is the defensive skill line. It increases your maximum HP, adds passive regeneration, boosts defense, and provides on-kill healing. Survival is a parent branch for the **Warlord** super tree alongside Combat.

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Tough Skin | 1 | 1 | +25 max HP | None |
| Recovery | 1 | 1 | Regenerate 1 HP per second | None |
| Iron Body | 2 | 2 | +4 defense | Tough Skin |
| Second Wind | 2 | 2 | Heal 15% max HP on kill | Recovery |

**Total points to max: 6**

## Skill Details

### Tough Skin (Tier 1)
A flat +25 to your maximum HP. This is significant in the early game when your base HP is low. More HP means more room for error against tough enemies.

### Recovery (Tier 1)
Passive regeneration of 1 HP per second. This does not sound like much, but it adds up quickly during exploration. You will spend less healing items just walking between encounters.

### Iron Body (Tier 2)
Adds 4 flat defense, which reduces all incoming damage. Defense stacks with armor, making you substantially tankier with good gear equipped.

### Second Wind (Tier 2)
Heals 15% of your max HP every time you kill an enemy. This is extremely powerful when fighting groups of weaker enemies and keeps you topped off without using potions.

## Recommended Builds

- **Tank build**: Tough Skin -> Iron Body first, then Recovery -> Second Wind. Maximizes survivability as quickly as possible.
- **Sustain build**: Recovery -> Second Wind first. Focuses on passive healing to reduce potion consumption during exploration.
- **Rush Warlord**: Max all 4 skills quickly to unlock the Warlord super tree (also requires maxed Combat).

## Synergies

- **Combat**: Maxing both unlocks the **Warlord** super tree. The Berserker skill's low-HP bonus is safer with Survival's defensive buffs keeping you alive.
- **Mining**: Tough Skin and Recovery help you survive deep underground where enemies hit harder. Second Wind keeps you healed while mining through enemy-filled caverns.
- **Mobility**: Feather Fall reduces fall damage, and Survival skills ensure you survive the occasional long drop.
