# Mining Skills

The **Mining** branch (orange) is the resource-gathering skill line. It makes you mine faster, find more drops, and reach further with your pickaxe. Mining is a parent branch for the **Trailblazer** super tree alongside Mobility.

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Efficient Mining | 1 | 1 | +30% mining speed | None |
| Treasure Hunter | 1 | 1 | 20% chance for double drops | None |
| Deep Miner | 2 | 2 | +50% mining speed | Efficient Mining |
| Prospector | 2 | 2 | +1.5 tile mining range | Treasure Hunter |

**Total points to max: 6**

## Skill Details

### Efficient Mining (Tier 1)
A 30% boost to mining speed. Blocks break noticeably faster, which adds up enormously when digging tunnels or mining ore veins. Essential for any player who spends time underground.

### Treasure Hunter (Tier 1)
Gives a 20% chance to receive double drops when mining a block. Over time, this significantly increases your material income. Especially valuable for rare ores like diamond and titanium.

### Deep Miner (Tier 2)
Stacks with Efficient Mining for a total of +80% mining speed. At this level, even hard blocks like obsidian break quickly. A must-have for serious miners.

### Prospector (Tier 2)
Extends your mining reach by 1.5 tiles. This lets you mine blocks that are further away without repositioning, which speeds up tunnel digging and makes mining in dangerous areas safer (you can stay further from lava and enemies).

## Recommended Builds

- **Speed miner**: Efficient Mining -> Deep Miner first. Maximizes how fast you break blocks, which is the biggest quality-of-life improvement.
- **Resource farmer**: Treasure Hunter -> Prospector first. Maximizes the value of each mining session, especially useful when farming rare ores.
- **Rush Trailblazer**: Max all 4 skills to unlock the Trailblazer super tree (also requires maxed Mobility).

## Synergies

- **Mobility**: Maxing both unlocks the **Trailblazer** super tree. Swift Feet helps you move through mines faster, and Double Jump lets you navigate vertical shafts.
- **Survival**: Tough Skin and Recovery help you survive deep underground where enemies are stronger. Second Wind heals you as you fight through caves.
- **Combat**: Not a natural pairing, but Quick Draw's attack speed boost helps clear enemies that interrupt your mining.
