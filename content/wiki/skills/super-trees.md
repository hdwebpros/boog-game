# Super Trees

Super trees are advanced skill branches that unlock once you have fully maxed **both parent branches**. They contain the most powerful skills in the base game, with high point costs and transformative effects.

There are three super trees, each combining two base branches.

## Warlord (Combat + Survival)

**Color:** Orange (#ff8844)

The Warlord tree turns you into an unstoppable melee fighter with lifesteal and death prevention.

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Blood Oath | 4 | 3 | 15% lifesteal (heal for 15% of damage dealt) | Max Combat + Max Survival |
| Undying Rage | 4 | 4 | Survive a lethal hit with 1 HP + 3 seconds of invulnerability (60s cooldown) | Blood Oath |

**Total points: 7**

### Blood Oath
Every attack you land heals you for 15% of the damage dealt. With high-damage weapons, this provides substantial sustain in combat. Pairs exceptionally well with the Berserker skill -- you deal massive damage at low HP and heal it right back.

### Undying Rage
When you would take a killing blow, you instead survive at 1 HP and become invulnerable for 3 seconds. This has a 60-second cooldown. During the invulnerability window, you can heal back up through Blood Oath or potions. This is the single most powerful defensive skill in the game.

## Spellblade (Combat + Magic)

**Color:** Purple (#cc44ff)

The Spellblade tree merges melee and magic into a hybrid damage powerhouse.

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Arcane Strikes | 4 | 3 | Melee attacks release a shockwave dealing 50% bonus magic damage | Max Combat + Max Magic |
| Mana Overload | 4 | 4 | When mana is above 75%, deal +100% damage but rapidly drain mana | Arcane Strikes |

**Total points: 7**

### Arcane Strikes
Every melee swing releases a ranged shockwave that deals 50% of the hit's damage as magic damage. This effectively gives melee weapons ranged capability and boosts total damage output by 50% per swing.

### Mana Overload
While your mana is above 75%, all your attacks deal double damage. However, mana drains rapidly while the effect is active. This creates a burst-damage playstyle: fill your mana, activate the buff, deal massive damage, then wait for mana to regenerate. Arcane Flow from the Magic branch helps recover mana faster between bursts.

## Trailblazer (Mining + Mobility)

**Color:** Gold (#ffcc22)

The Trailblazer tree turns mining into a speed-focused, large-scale operation.

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Tunnel Bore | 4 | 3 | Mine in a 3x3 area instead of single blocks | Max Mining + Max Mobility |
| Flash Step | 4 | 4 | +50% movement speed + complete fall damage immunity | Tunnel Bore |

**Total points: 7**

### Tunnel Bore
Your pickaxe breaks a 3x3 grid of blocks with each swing. This turns mining from a block-by-block process into rapid tunnel carving. Combined with Deep Miner's speed boost, you can excavate entire underground areas in seconds.

### Flash Step
A massive 50% movement speed boost (stacking with Swift Feet for a total of +70%) and complete immunity to fall damage. You become a blur of speed, able to traverse the entire world in minutes. Fall damage immunity means you can drop from any height without worry.

## Unlocking Super Trees

To unlock a super tree, you must spend every skill point required to max both parent branches:

| Super Tree | Branch 1 (cost) | Branch 2 (cost) | Total prereq points |
|------------|------------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Warlord | Combat (9) | Survival (6) | 15 |
| Spellblade | Combat (9) | Magic (6) | 15 |
| Trailblazer | Mining (6) | Mobility (6) | 12 |

## Tips

- Combat is a parent for two of the three super trees (Warlord and Spellblade). If you are unsure which super tree to aim for, maxing Combat first keeps both options open.
- The **Trailblazer** tree has the lowest prereq cost (12 points vs 15) and is the fastest to unlock. It is also the best quality-of-life tree for exploration.
- You need to complete **2 of 3 super trees** to unlock the Ascension branch. Plan your skill point allocation accordingly.
- Warlord is the strongest super tree for boss fights. Blood Oath + Undying Rage makes you nearly unkillable in extended combat.
- Spellblade is the highest burst-damage tree. Mana Overload + Berserker at low HP is devastating but risky.
