# Magic Skills

The **Magic** branch (blue) is the spellcasting skill line. It expands your mana pool, speeds up mana regeneration, boosts magic damage, and adds a defensive mana shield. Magic is a parent branch for the **Spellblade** super tree alongside Combat.

## Skills

| Skill | Tier | Cost | Effect | Prerequisite |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Mana Well | 1 | 1 | +30 max mana | None |
| Arcane Flow | 1 | 1 | +50% mana regeneration | None |
| Spell Power | 2 | 2 | +30% magic damage | Mana Well |
| Mana Shield | 2 | 2 | 25% of incoming damage drains mana instead of HP | Arcane Flow |

**Total points to max: 6**

## Skill Details

### Mana Well (Tier 1)
Adds 30 to your maximum mana pool. More mana means more spell casts before you run dry. Essential if you are using magic weapons as your primary damage source.

### Arcane Flow (Tier 1)
Increases mana regeneration by 50%. This means less downtime waiting for mana to refill between fights. Combined with Mana Well, you can sustain magic weapons through longer encounters.

### Spell Power (Tier 2)
A flat 30% increase to all magic damage. This applies to staves, magic weapons, and any ability that deals magic damage. A significant boost that makes magic weapons competitive with higher-tier melee options.

### Mana Shield (Tier 2)
Redirects 25% of incoming damage to your mana pool instead of HP. This effectively gives you a second health bar as long as you have mana. Extremely powerful when combined with a large mana pool from Mana Well.

## Recommended Builds

- **Pure mage**: Mana Well -> Spell Power for maximum magic damage output. Follow with Arcane Flow -> Mana Shield for sustain.
- **Defensive mage**: Arcane Flow -> Mana Shield first. Prioritizes survivability through the mana shield while relying on weapon base damage.
- **Rush Spellblade**: Max all 4 skills to unlock the Spellblade super tree (also requires maxed Combat).

## Synergies

- **Combat**: Maxing both unlocks the **Spellblade** super tree. Critical Eye's crit chance applies to magic attacks, and Spellblade adds melee shockwaves that scale with magic damage.
- **Survival**: Mana Shield combined with Tough Skin and Iron Body creates an extremely durable character. Recovery's passive healing keeps you alive while mana absorbs burst damage.
- **Mobility**: Swift Feet helps you kite enemies while casting spells. Feather Fall gives you time to cast while falling.
