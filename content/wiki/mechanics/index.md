# Mechanics

Starfall has several interconnected systems that govern how you interact with the world. Understanding these mechanics is key to progressing through the game and taking on its toughest challenges.

## Mechanic Guides

### [Combat](/wiki/mechanics/combat)
The combat system covers 4 weapon styles (melee, ranged, magic, summon), damage calculation, defense, invincibility frames, and enemy AI patterns. Learn how each weapon type works and how to maximize your damage output.

### [Jetpack](/wiki/mechanics/jetpack)
The jetpack is assembled from 6 components dropped by the main bosses. Once built at a Fusion Station, it grants flight by holding Space while airborne. Fuel depletes during flight and recharges when standing on solid ground.

### [Multiplayer](/wiki/mechanics/multiplayer)
Starfall supports Valheim-style player-hosted multiplayer. One player hosts and acts as the authoritative server, while others connect via WebSocket. Explore, build, and fight bosses together.

### [Potions & Buffs](/wiki/mechanics/potions-and-buffs)
16 potions provide timed buffs that boost your stats and grant special abilities. Learn what each potion does, how to craft them, and the best combinations for different situations.

### [Enchanting](/wiki/mechanics/enchanting)
Enhance your weapons and armor with powerful enchantments. This system adds another layer of customization to your loadout and can make the difference in tough boss fights.
