# Enchanting

The enchanting system allows you to infuse weapons with elemental power, adding bonus damage and special effects. It is built around **elemental shards**, **Arcane Dust**, and the **Arcane Anvil** crafting station.

## How It Works

The enchanting pipeline has 4 stages:

1. **Mine elemental crystal blocks** underground to receive elemental shards
2. **Craft the Arcane Anvil** station (at a regular Anvil)
3. **Combine shards + Arcane Dust** into Chant Orbs (at the Arcane Anvil)
4. **Apply a Chant Orb** to a weapon to enchant it

## Elemental Shards

Five types of elemental crystal blocks generate naturally underground. Mining them drops the corresponding shard:

| Crystal Block | Shard | Depth Range |
|---------------|-------|-------------|
| Ember Crystal | Ember Shard | Underground to Deep |
| Frost Crystal | Frost Shard | Underground to Deep |
| Storm Crystal | Storm Shard | Underground to Deep |
| Void Crystal | Void Shard | Deep Underground and below |
| Life Crystal | Life Shard | Underground (shallowest) |

**Arcane Dust** also drops from crystal blocks and rare underground formations. It is required for every chant orb recipe.

## The Arcane Anvil

The Arcane Anvil is the enchanting station. Craft it at a regular **Anvil**:

- 8 Iron Bar + 4 Diamond + 5 Arcane Dust

Place it in your base and press **C** nearby to access enchanting recipes.

## Chant Orbs

Each chant orb costs **5 shards + 3 Arcane Dust** at the Arcane Anvil:

| Chant Orb | Shards | Enchantment | Effect |
|-----------|--------|-------------|--------|
| Inferno Chant | 5 Ember | Inferno | Fire DoT on hit |
| Glacial Chant | 5 Frost | Glacial | Slows enemy movement |
| Tempest Chant | 5 Storm | Tempest | Bonus lightning damage |
| Abyssal Chant | 5 Void | Abyssal | Void damage (strong vs void enemies) |
| Verdant Chant | 5 Life | Verdant | Life steal on hit |

Chant orbs stack to 10.

## Applying Enchantments

Use a Chant Orb on a weapon to apply the enchantment. The weapon gains:

- **Bonus elemental damage** matching the enchantment type
- **A special on-hit effect** (burn, slow, lightning, void damage, or life steal)
- **A colored glow** on the weapon sprite

Only one enchantment can be active on a weapon at a time. Applying a new chant replaces the old one.

## The Eternal Chant

The **Eternal Chant** is the ultimate enchantment, combining all 5 elements. Craft it at the Arcane Anvil:

- 1 Inferno Chant + 1 Glacial Chant + 1 Tempest Chant + 1 Abyssal Chant + 1 Verdant Chant

An Eternal-enchanted weapon applies all 5 elemental effects simultaneously and glows gold.

**Total materials for one Eternal Chant:**
- 5 of each shard type (25 total)
- 15 Arcane Dust (3 per sub-chant)

## Utility: Mystical Compass

Craft at the Arcane Anvil (8 Arcane Dust + 3 Diamond + 5 Iron Bar). When held, it points toward the nearest shard deposit underground.

After accessing the Void Dimension, element-specific **Shard Compasses** become available at the Void Forge.

## Key Interactions

- **Spelunker Potion** highlights crystal blocks through walls, making shard farming much easier.
- **Star Compass** accessory reveals ores on the minimap, including crystal deposits.
- **Miner's Lantern** accessory increases mining speed, speeding up crystal harvesting.
- Enchantments work with all 4 weapon styles (melee, ranged, magic, summon).
