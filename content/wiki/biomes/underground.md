The Underground is where Starfall transitions from a survival game into a spelunking adventure. Stretching from y=180 to y=640, this layer is a labyrinth of tunnels, caverns, and ore veins. The enemy roster expands dramatically compared to the surface, and the resources here -- iron ore, diamond ore, and crystal formations -- are essential for progressing through the game's material tiers. Bring torches, bring weapons, and bring your nerve.

## Terrain & Generation

The Underground generates as a dense network of stone tunnels and open caverns. Cave systems wind through the rock in organic patterns, sometimes opening into large chambers. Crystal caverns appear as special formations with glittering walls. Clay pockets and moss-covered areas add variety to the stone landscape. The terrain is dark by default, requiring light sources to navigate safely.

## Enemies

- [Cave Bat](/wiki/enemies/cave-bat) -- Quick flyers that dive at players in tight tunnels. Fast but fragile.
- [Rock Golem](/wiki/enemies/rock-golem) -- Heavily armored stone creatures that charge at intruders. Tough to take down.
- [Fungal Shambler](/wiki/enemies/fungal-shambler) -- Mushroom creatures that split into Sporelings on death.
- [Sporeling](/wiki/enemies/sporeling) -- Tiny spore creatures spawned by dying Fungal Shamblers.
- [Mimic](/wiki/enemies/mimic) -- Deceptive creatures disguised as treasure chests. Attacks when you get close.
- [Shock Beetle](/wiki/enemies/shock-beetle) -- Armored beetles that retaliate with electric projectiles when struck.
- [Phantom Wraith](/wiki/enemies/phantom-wraith) -- Spectral entities that phase between tangible and intangible states.
- [Gloom Moth](/wiki/enemies/gloom-moth) -- Winged insects that are passive during daylight but turn aggressive in the dark.

The Underground has the highest enemy variety of any layer. Multiple threats can appear simultaneously in larger caverns, so stay alert.

## Tips

- Always carry torches. The Underground is pitch black, and Gloom Moths become aggressive in darkness.
- Mimics look like treasure chests. Approach any chest cautiously and be ready to fight.
- Shock Beetles punish reckless melee attacks with electric retaliation. Use ranged weapons or time your strikes carefully.
- Rock Golems are tanky. If you are undergeared, consider running past them rather than fighting.
- Crystal caverns are worth exploring thoroughly. The crystals are valuable and the caverns often connect to deeper areas.
- Mine iron ore as your top priority here. Iron tools and weapons are a massive upgrade from stone.
- Phantom Wraiths cannot be hit during their intangible phase. Wait for them to solidify before attacking.

## Notable Structures

Crystal caverns are the most distinctive underground formations. They appear as open chambers with crystal-encrusted walls that sparkle in torchlight. Clay pockets and moss-covered sections break up the monotony of stone and can signal proximity to larger cave systems.
