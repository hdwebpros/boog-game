The Hellfire Caverns are a network of tunnels and chambers deep within the Void Dimension, flooded with rivers and lakes of lava. The walls glow orange from the heat, and hellfire ore -- a powerful void material -- veins through the stone. Nether Golems lumber through the passages, nearly immovable walls of void stone given terrible life. The Hellfire Imps from the Ashen Wastes are here too, more numerous and just as aggressive.

## Terrain & Generation

The Hellfire Caverns generate as an enclosed tunnel system within the Void Dimension. Lava lakes fill the lower sections of the caverns, and lava flows cut channels through the void stone. Hellfire ore deposits glow with an inner heat, embedded in the cavern walls. The passages vary between tight corridors and open chambers, with lava as a constant hazard. Void crystal formations appear in some of the deeper chambers.

## Enemies

- [Hellfire Imp](/wiki/enemies/hellfire-imp) -- More numerous here than in the Ashen Wastes. Their ranged fire attacks are especially dangerous near lava where dodging room is limited.
- [Nether Golem](/wiki/enemies/nether-golem) -- An immense construct of void stone. Incredibly durable and hits like a freight train. Slow but nearly impossible to stop once it starts moving.

The combination of tight spaces, lava hazards, and Nether Golems makes the Hellfire Caverns one of the most dangerous zones in the game. Nether Golems can block entire passages, forcing you to fight or find another way around.

## Tips

- Nether Golems are tanks. Unless you have void-tier weapons, consider going around them rather than through them.
- The lava here is just as deadly as overworld lava. Bridge carefully and never rush through passages.
- Hellfire ore is a key material for void-tier crafting at the Void Forge. Mine every vein you find.
- Hellfire Imps are more dangerous in the caverns than in the open Ashen Wastes because you have less room to dodge their projectiles.
- Void Crystals are rare and valuable. Check every large chamber thoroughly.
- Bring plenty of blocks for bridging over lava. You will need them constantly.

## Notable Structures

Lava lakes and lava channels are the defining features of the Hellfire Caverns. Some chambers contain natural void crystal formations that are worth the risk to reach. The caverns themselves form a winding network that can be disorienting without markers.
