The Deep Underground is a punishing expanse of massive caverns, lava pools, and rare ore deposits. Starting at y=640 and extending to y=1320, this layer dwarfs the Underground above it in both scale and danger. Lava pools cast an orange glow across the stone, Lava Serpents erupt from the molten rock, and titanium ore -- one of the highest-tier materials -- hides in the walls. Only well-equipped players should venture this deep.

## Terrain & Generation

The Deep Underground generates with significantly larger caverns than the layer above. Open chambers can span dozens of tiles in every direction. Lava pools are a defining feature, filling low points and creating hazardous rivers of molten rock. Obsidian forms where water meets lava. The stone here is darker, and the overall atmosphere is oppressive. Titanium ore veins appear embedded in the walls, rarer than iron or diamond but immensely valuable.

## Enemies

- [Lava Serpent](/wiki/enemies/lava-serpent) -- A fiery snake that emerges from lava pools to strike at nearby players. Deals heavy fire damage.
- [Phantom Wraith](/wiki/enemies/phantom-wraith) -- The same phasing spectral entity found in the upper Underground, but more frequently encountered here.

Enemy variety is lower than the Underground, but the threats are individually much more dangerous. Lava Serpents are especially hazardous near the lava pools you need to navigate around.

## Tips

- Lava is instant death if you fall in without protection. Build bridges over lava pools rather than trying to jump across.
- Lava Serpents emerge from lava, so do not stand at the edge of lava pools. Keep your distance and use ranged weapons.
- Titanium ore is rare. When you find a vein, mine all of it. You need substantial quantities for a full set of titanium gear.
- Obsidian is incredibly hard to mine and requires high-tier pickaxes. It is needed in large quantities for the Super Portal (99 obsidian), so start collecting early.
- Phantom Wraiths are more common here. Learn their phase timing from the upper Underground before coming this deep.
- Always have an escape route planned. The large caverns can be disorienting, and getting lost near lava is dangerous.

## Notable Structures

Lava lakes are the dominant feature of the Deep Underground. Some caverns contain natural obsidian formations where ancient lava flows met underground water sources. These obsidian deposits are the most reliable source of this crucial endgame material.
