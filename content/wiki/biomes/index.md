# Biomes

The world of Starfall spans 6000x1600 tiles across multiple depth layers and surface biomes. From the floating Cloud City in the sky to the molten Core deep underground, each region presents unique terrain, resources, and dangers. Beyond the main world lies the Void Dimension, an enclosed hellscape accessible only through the Super Portal.

## Surface Biomes

The surface layer sits at roughly y=100 and stretches across eight distinct biomes. This is where you crash-land and begin your journey.

- [Plains](/wiki/biomes/plains) -- Flat grasslands ideal for new players to get their bearings.
- [Forest](/wiki/biomes/forest) -- Dense woodland with abundant timber for early crafting.
- [Desert](/wiki/biomes/desert) -- Scorching sands with cacti, sandstone, and dangerous scorpions.
- [Mountains](/wiki/biomes/mountains) -- Elevated rocky terrain with exposed iron ore deposits.
- [Lake](/wiki/biomes/lake) -- Bodies of water with coral reefs and aquatic life.
- [Snow](/wiki/biomes/snow) -- Frozen tundra patrolled by wolves and wisps.
- [Jungle](/wiki/biomes/jungle) -- Thick tropical vegetation crawling with spiders and stranglers.
- [Mushroom](/wiki/biomes/mushroom) -- Surreal fungal landscape tucked between other biomes.

## Depth Layers

The world is divided into six depth layers, each progressively more dangerous and resource-rich.

| Layer | Y Range | Key Features |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Sky | y < 60 | Cloud City, open air |
| Surface | y ~ 100 | 8 biomes, starter area |
| Ocean | Surface level | Water bodies, coral reefs |
| Underground | 180 -- 640 | Cave systems, iron and diamond ore |
| Deep Underground | 640 -- 1320 | Large caverns, titanium ore, lava pools |
| Core | 1320+ | Carbon fiber deposits, extreme danger |

- [Sky & Cloud City](/wiki/biomes/cloud-city) -- A floating island city high above the world.
- [Underground](/wiki/biomes/underground) -- Winding cave systems beneath the surface.
- [Deep Underground](/wiki/biomes/deep-underground) -- Vast caverns with rare ores and lava.
- [Core](/wiki/biomes/core) -- The planet's molten heart, home to the rarest materials.

## Void Dimension

A separate world (3000x800 tiles) accessed through the Super Portal after beating the game. Far more dangerous than anything in the main world.

- [Ashen Wastes](/wiki/biomes/ashen-wastes) -- The entry zone of the Void Dimension.
- [Hellfire Caverns](/wiki/biomes/hellfire-caverns) -- Lava-filled tunnels rich with hellfire ore.
- [Void Forest](/wiki/biomes/void-forest) -- A dark woodland of twisted void trees.
- [Dark Citadel](/wiki/biomes/dark-citadel) -- Nether brick fortress, the most dangerous zone in the game.
