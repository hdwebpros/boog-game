Cloud City is a floating island suspended high above the world at y=60, near the horizontal center of the map. This is not a hostile zone -- it is a sanctuary in the sky, home to the Sky Merchant NPC who sells powerful accessories and upgradeable artifacts. Reaching Cloud City is a milestone in any playthrough, and the items available here can dramatically improve your capabilities.

## Terrain & Generation

Cloud City generates as a floating island platform near the world's center x-coordinate at y=60 tiles. The island is constructed from cloud blocks, cloud brick, and cloud pillars, giving it an ethereal, weightless appearance. A small building on the island houses the Sky Merchant NPC. The island is large enough to walk around on but compact enough to explore in moments.

## Enemies

Cloud City has no enemy spawns. It is a safe zone where you can browse the shop and plan your next move without interruption.

## Tips

- Getting to Cloud City requires height. You can build a tall pillar of blocks, use a Featherfall Potion, or fly up with the jetpack once you have it.
- The Sky Merchant sells six accessories (Cloud Boots, Star Compass, Gravity Belt, Miner's Lantern, Lucky Charm, Celestial Cape) for Silver Coins.
- After visiting the Void Dimension, the Sky Merchant also offers upgradeable Artifacts with three tiers each.
- Silver Coins are the currency here. You earn them by selling items to the Sky Merchant or finding them as drops.
- Press F near the Sky Merchant to open the shop interface with buy and sell tabs.
- Cloud City is a great landmark for navigation. If you can see it from the surface, you know roughly where the world center is.

## Notable Structures

The Sky Merchant's shop building is the primary structure on Cloud City. It is a small enclosed building with the NPC shopkeeper inside. The island itself is the only floating terrain in the overworld, making it visible from a great distance.
