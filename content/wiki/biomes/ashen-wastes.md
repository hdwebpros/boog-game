The Ashen Wastes are the first thing you see when you step through the Super Portal into the Void Dimension. A gray, lifeless expanse of ash and brimstone stretches before you under a sky that is not quite a sky. This is the entry zone -- the least dangerous part of the Void Dimension, which still means it is far more dangerous than most of the overworld. Void Wraiths phase through the terrain and Hellfire Imps hurl fire from a distance. Welcome to the void.

## Terrain & Generation

The Ashen Wastes generate as a relatively open zone within the Void Dimension (3000x800 tiles). The ground is composed of ash blocks and void dirt, with brimstone formations rising from the terrain. Void stone makes up the deeper layers. The landscape is flat and barren compared to the other void zones, giving you clear sightlines but also leaving you exposed. The Super Portal deposits you here, making it the staging area for deeper void exploration.

## Enemies

- [Void Wraith](/wiki/enemies/void-wraith) -- A spectral terror that phases through solid matter. Similar to the Phantom Wraith but significantly more powerful.
- [Hellfire Imp](/wiki/enemies/hellfire-imp) -- A swift demonic creature that hurls fire from a distance. Fast and aggressive.

Even the "easy" zone of the Void Dimension is a serious threat. Both enemies here hit much harder than any overworld creature. Void-tier or at minimum titanium/carbon fiber gear is strongly recommended.

## Tips

- This is your base of operations in the Void Dimension. Consider building a small shelter near the portal before venturing deeper.
- Void Wraiths phase through walls. A shelter will not protect you from them -- stay armed at all times.
- Hellfire Imps are ranged attackers. Close the distance or fight back with your own ranged weapons.
- The open terrain here is a double-edged sword. You can see threats coming, but they can see you too.
- Gather void materials here for crafting at the Void Forge. Even the basic materials have uses.
- Do not underestimate the Void Dimension. The enemies here are on par with overworld bosses in terms of danger.

## Notable Structures

The Super Portal arrival point is the most notable landmark in the Ashen Wastes. Brimstone spires rise from the ash in scattered formations, serving as natural landmarks for navigation in the otherwise featureless landscape.
