The Dark Citadel is the final and most dangerous zone of the Void Dimension. Massive structures of nether brick rise from the void stone floor -- walls, towers, and corridors that suggest an ancient, terrible civilization once thrived here. The Dark Knight patrols these halls, the most dangerous regular enemy in the entire game. Chaos Elementals attack from range with unpredictable energy, and Void Serpents coil through the larger chambers. If you have reached the Dark Citadel, you are nearing the end of what Starfall has to offer -- and the game knows it.

## Terrain & Generation

The Dark Citadel generates as a zone dominated by nether brick structures. Walls, floors, and pillars of nether brick form corridors and chambers that feel constructed rather than natural. The architecture is imposing and labyrinthine, with multiple levels connected by stairways and drops. Void stone fills the gaps between structures, and void crystal deposits appear in the deepest chambers. The structures create a fortress-like environment with chokepoints and ambush corners.

## Enemies

- [Dark Knight](/wiki/enemies/dark-knight) -- An armored warrior of the void and the most dangerous regular enemy in the game. High HP, high damage, aggressive AI.
- [Chaos Elemental](/wiki/enemies/chaos-elemental) -- A being of pure chaotic energy that attacks from range with unpredictable projectile patterns.
- [Void Serpent](/wiki/enemies/void-serpent) -- A colossal serpent that emerges from the depths. Massive hitbox and devastating attacks.

The Dark Citadel has the most dangerous enemy roster in the game. Dark Knights can trade blows with players in full void gear and win. Chaos Elementals add ranged pressure that forces constant movement. Void Serpents are rare but terrifying when they appear.

## Tips

- Do not fight Dark Knights without void-tier weapons and armor. They are the most dangerous regular enemies in the game and will punish undergeared players.
- Chaos Elementals fire unpredictable projectiles. Keep moving and use the nether brick structures as cover.
- Void Serpents are rare but lethal. If you see one, decide quickly whether to fight or flee.
- The citadel structures provide cover and chokepoints. Use the architecture to your advantage by funneling enemies into narrow corridors.
- Nether brick is a strong building material worth collecting for use back in the overworld.
- Void Crystals found here are essential for the highest-tier void crafting recipes.
- This is the endgame of the endgame. If you can survive the Dark Citadel, you are ready for anything -- including the Void Lord.

## Notable Structures

The nether brick fortress structures are the most impressive generated architecture in Starfall. Towers, corridors, chambers, and stairways form an interconnected citadel that spans the entire zone. The structures suggest intelligent design, hinting at the lore of whatever civilization once existed in the Void Dimension.
