The Plains is the default surface biome in Starfall and the most likely place you will find yourself after crash-landing. Rolling fields of grass stretch out in every direction, broken only by the occasional tree or gentle hill. The terrain is forgiving, the enemies are slow, and the resources are plentiful enough to get you on your feet. If you are new to the game, this is where you want to be.

## Terrain & Generation

The Plains biome generates with mostly flat terrain at around y=100 with minor elevation changes. The ground is composed of grass blocks on the surface with dirt underneath. Scattered trees provide wood, and the open landscape makes it easy to spot enemies and plan your next move. The flatness also makes this biome ideal for building a base.

## Enemies

- [Space Slug](/wiki/enemies/space-slug) -- The only enemy that regularly spawns in the Plains. These slow, patrol-pattern creatures are easy to defeat and drop Plant Fiber.

The Plains is the safest surface biome. Space Slugs are passive wanderers that only deal contact damage, making them manageable even with no equipment.

## Resources

| Resource | Source | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Wood | Trees | Chop down trees for early crafting |
| Plant Fiber | Ground drops, Space Slugs | Used in basic recipes |
| Dirt | Ground blocks | Abundant, useful for bridging |
| Grass | Surface blocks | Decorative, converts dirt to grass |

## Tips

- The Plains is the best place to build your first base thanks to the flat terrain and low enemy density.
- Farm Space Slugs early for Plant Fiber, which you need for basic tool and armor recipes.
- Dig straight down from the Plains to quickly reach the Underground layer and start mining ores.
- Trees here are spaced out but reliable. Chop every tree you see early on to stockpile wood.

## Notable Structures

The Plains biome does not contain any special structures or altars. Its value lies in being a safe, open environment for establishing your foothold in the world.
