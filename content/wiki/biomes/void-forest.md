The Void Forest is a grotesque parody of the overworld's woodlands. Twisted trees of void wood rise from soul sand, their dark leaves blocking what little light exists in the Void Dimension. Shadow Stalkers lurk in the darkness between the trunks -- massive ambush predators that strike from the shadows. Soul Eaters drift through the canopy above, feeding on the life force of anything that enters their domain. This is not a forest you walk through. It is a forest that walks through you.

## Terrain & Generation

The Void Forest generates as a zone within the Void Dimension characterized by dense void tree coverage. The ground is soul sand and void grass, with void wood trunks rising to a canopy of void leaves. The terrain is relatively flat but cluttered with undergrowth and root structures. The darkness here is near-total, making visibility a serious concern. The trees are spaced closely enough to create natural corridors and dead ends.

## Enemies

- [Shadow Stalker](/wiki/enemies/shadow-stalker) -- A massive ambush predator lurking in void shadows. Deals devastating damage on its first strike and is difficult to detect before it attacks.
- [Soul Eater](/wiki/enemies/soul-eater) -- A flying void creature that feeds on life force. Drains health at range and is hard to reach in the canopy.

The Void Forest's enemies are built around ambush and attrition. Shadow Stalkers punish careless movement, while Soul Eaters drain your health from positions you cannot easily counter. Together they make the Void Forest one of the most stressful zones to traverse.

## Tips

- Move slowly and deliberately. Shadow Stalkers ambush players who rush through the forest without looking.
- Soul Eaters are aerial threats. A ranged weapon is essential for dealing with them in the canopy.
- Cutting down void trees improves visibility and reduces ambush opportunities for Shadow Stalkers.
- Light sources help but do not eliminate the danger. The darkness in the Void Forest is deeper than normal.
- Soul Sand has a distinct texture. If you see it underfoot, you are in the Void Forest and should be on high alert.
- The Void Forest is arguably the most psychologically intense zone in the game. Take breaks if you need to.

## Notable Structures

The void trees themselves are the primary structures, forming a dense, oppressive canopy. Some areas contain clusters of soul sand that form natural clearings between the trees. These clearings can be fortified as temporary safe points.
