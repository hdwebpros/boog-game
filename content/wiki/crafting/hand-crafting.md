**Hand Crafting** is the starting crafting method in Starfall. It requires no station at all -- just open the crafting menu anywhere by pressing **C**. Hand crafting recipes are always available regardless of what station you are standing near.

## How to Obtain

Hand crafting is available from the start of the game. No station is needed.

## How to Use

Press **C** anywhere in the world to open the crafting menu. Hand crafting recipes appear by default.

## Available Recipes

| Item | Materials | Qty | Category |
|------|-----------|-----|----------|
| Torch | 1 Wood + 1 Plant Fiber | 4 | Utility |
| Workbench | 10 Wood | 1 | Station |

## Crafting Progression

Hand Crafting is the starting point for all crafting in Starfall.

```
► Hand Crafting ──► Workbench
```

## Tips

- Craft a **Workbench** as your first priority after gathering 10 wood. It unlocks the entire crafting tree.
- Torches are cheap and stack in groups of 4. Keep a supply on you for exploring dark underground areas.
- Plant Fiber can be crafted from leaves at the Workbench, but for your first torches you will need to find it from surface plants.
