The **Arcane Anvil** is a side-branch crafting station focused on magic. It produces the five elemental **Chant Orbs**, the powerful **Eternal Chant** that combines all five, and the **Mystical Compass** utility item. While not required for the main crafting progression, the Arcane Anvil is essential for magic-focused builds.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material   | Quantity |
|------------|----------|
| Iron Bar   | 8        |
| Diamond    | 4        |
| Arcane Dust| 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place the Arcane Anvil in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Available Recipes

### Chant Orbs

Each chant orb channels a different elemental power. Collect all five to craft the Eternal Chant.

| Item | Materials | Output |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Ember Chant | 5 Ember Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | 1 |
| Frost Chant | 5 Frost Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | 1 |
| Storm Chant | 5 Storm Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | 1 |
| Void Chant | 5 Void Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | 1 |
| Life Chant | 5 Life Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | 1 |

### Combined Chant

| Item | Materials | Output |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Eternal Chant | Ember Chant + Frost Chant + Storm Chant + Void Chant + Life Chant | 1 |

### Utility

| Item | Materials | Output |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Mystical Compass | 8 Arcane Dust + 3 Diamond + 5 Iron Bar | 1 |

## Crafting Progression

```
Anvil ──► Arcane Anvil (side branch)
```

The Arcane Anvil is a side branch off the main crafting tree. It does not lead to any further stations.

## Tips

- **Elemental shards** drop from enemies in specific biomes and depths. Farm the appropriate areas to collect them.
- The **Eternal Chant** is extremely powerful but requires all five individual chants. It is a long-term goal, not something you rush.
- **Arcane Dust** is the bottleneck material. It drops from deeper enemies and can be found in crystal caverns underground.
- The **Mystical Compass** helps locate rare ores and points of interest. Worth crafting if you are struggling to find titanium or diamond veins.
- If you are not running a magic build, you can skip the Arcane Anvil entirely. It has no impact on the main crafting progression.
