The **Fusion Station** is the pinnacle of the main crafting progression. It handles carbon fiber processing, titanium-tier weapons and armor, the all-important **Jetpack** assembly, and the **Super Portal** to the Void Dimension. This is where endgame gear is made.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Tech Bench**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Titanium Bar | 10       |
| Diamond      | 5        |
| Glass        | 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place the Fusion Station in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Tech Bench ──► Fusion Station ──► Void Forge
```

The Fusion Station is the last station in the main progression. After assembling the Jetpack and beating the game, you can craft the **Super Portal** to access the Void Dimension and the **Void Forge** for the ultimate tier of equipment.

## Tips

- The **Jetpack** is the primary goal of the game. You cannot escape the planet without it. Each of the six bosses drops one component.
- The **Laser Gun** is one of the strongest ranged weapons in the base game. It fires a continuous beam that deals rapid damage.
- The **Swarm Beacon** is the top-tier summon weapon. It spawns multiple attack drones that swarm enemies.
- The **Super Portal** is only unlocked after beating the game (escaping the planet). The recipe is expensive -- start stockpiling obsidian and arcane dust before your first escape.
- Build the **Void Forge** inside or near the Void Dimension for convenience, since its recipe materials (brimstone and hellfire cores) come from void enemies and void ore.
