# Crafting Stations

Starfall uses a tiered crafting system. You start with basic hand crafting and work your way up through increasingly powerful stations, each unlocking new recipes for tools, weapons, armor, and materials.

## Crafting Progression

The stations form a linear progression with two side branches. Each station is built at the previous one.

```
Hand Crafting (tier 0)
  └─► Workbench (tier 0)
        └─► Workbench Mk2 (tier 1)
              ├─► Furnace (tier 1)
              │     └─► Anvil (tier 2)
              │           ├─► Tech Bench (tier 3)
              │           │     └─► Fusion Station (tier 4)
              │           │           └─► Void Forge (tier 5)
              │           └─► Arcane Anvil (tier 2)
              └─► Brewing Stand (tier 1)
```

## Station Overview

| Station | Tier | Built At | Key Materials | Primary Purpose |

| [Hand Crafting](/wiki/crafting/hand-crafting) | 0 | -- | -- | Torches, Workbench |
| [Workbench](/wiki/crafting/workbench) | 0 | Hand | 10 wood | Wood gear, basic items |
| [Workbench Mk2](/wiki/crafting/workbench-mk2) | 1 | Workbench | 15 wood + 10 stone | Stone gear, unlocks Furnace |
| [Furnace](/wiki/crafting/furnace) | 1 | Workbench Mk2 | 20 stone | Smelting ores into bars |
| [Anvil](/wiki/crafting/anvil) | 2 | Furnace | 10 iron bar | Iron gear, advanced items |
| [Tech Bench](/wiki/crafting/tech-bench) | 3 | Anvil | 8 diamond + 5 iron bar + 3 glass | Diamond gear, titanium smelting |
| [Fusion Station](/wiki/crafting/fusion-station) | 4 | Tech Bench | 10 titanium bar + 5 diamond + 5 glass | Titanium/carbon gear, Jetpack |
| [Arcane Anvil](/wiki/crafting/arcane-anvil) | 2 | Anvil | 8 iron bar + 4 diamond + 5 arcane dust | Chant orbs, magic items |

## How Crafting Works

1. **Build** the crafting station from the required materials at the previous-tier station.
2. **Place** the station in the world using **RMB** (right mouse button).
3. **Stand near** the placed station (within a few tiles).
4. **Press C** to open the crafting menu. Only recipes for your current station (and hand crafting) are shown.
5. **Select a recipe** and click **Craft**. The required materials are consumed from your inventory and the crafted item is added.

## Tips

- You can pick up and re-place stations freely. Carry your key stations with you as you explore deeper.
- The Workbench Mk2 is easy to overlook but is required to unlock the Furnace. Build it early.
- The Arcane Anvil and Brewing Stand are side branches. You do not need them for the main crafting progression, but they provide powerful combat advantages.
- In the Void Dimension, bring materials back to the overworld to use the Void Forge.
