The **Brewing Stand** is a side-branch crafting station dedicated entirely to potions. It produces all 16 potions in the game, ranging from simple healing boosts to powerful combat buffs. Potions provide temporary effects that can make the difference between life and death in boss fights.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench Mk2**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Stone    | 15       |
| Iron Bar | 3        |
| Glass    | 2        |

Note: Iron bars and glass require a Furnace to produce, so you will need to build the Furnace before you can craft the Brewing Stand.

## How to Use

1. Place the Brewing Stand in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Workbench Mk2 ──► Brewing Stand (side branch)
```

The Brewing Stand is a side branch. It does not lead to any further stations.

## Tips

- Stock up on potions before every boss fight. A well-timed Ironskin + Regeneration combo can carry you through tough encounters.
- **Spelunker Potion** is extremely useful for finding rare ores. Use one when exploring new underground areas.
- Higher-tier potions require rarer materials. The tier 3 potions need diamonds and titanium bars, so save them for the hardest content.
- The **Rage Potion** is high risk, high reward -- extra damage at the cost of lower defense. Best paired with the Mana Shield skill or Forcefield Potion.
- Keep a Brewing Stand at your base and brew in bulk before long expeditions.
