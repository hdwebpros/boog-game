The **Void Forge** is the final crafting station in Starfall, unlocked after reaching the Void Dimension. It processes void materials into the most powerful equipment in the game and crafts the **Void Lord Summon Token** needed to face the true final boss.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Brimstone     | 20       |
| Hellfire Core | 10       |

Both materials are found exclusively in the Void Dimension.

## How to Use

1. Place the Void Forge in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Fusion Station ──► Void Forge (endgame)
```

The Void Forge is the end of the crafting progression. There are no further stations to build.

## Tips

- Place the Void Forge in the overworld near your base. You will be going back and forth between the Void Dimension and the overworld to gather materials and craft.
- **Abyssal Ingots** and **Dimensional Fabric** are the two key refined materials. Stockpile hellfire ore, void crystals, and brimstone in the Void Dimension before returning to craft.
- The **Void Lord Summon Token** must be used in the overworld, not the Void Dimension. Craft it and return home before summoning the true final boss.
- Void weapons cover all four weapon styles (melee, ranged, magic, summon). Pick the ones that match your skill build.
- Full Void Armor is the strongest defensive set in the game. Prioritize crafting it before fighting the Void Lord.
- **Shard Compasses** point toward deposits of each elemental shard type, making it easier to farm materials for the Arcane Anvil's chant recipes.
