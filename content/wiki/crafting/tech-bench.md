The **Tech Bench** represents the shift from medieval-style crafting to advanced technology. It unlocks diamond-tier equipment, titanium smelting, and the recipes for two critical late-game stations: the **Fusion Station** and the **Portal**.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Diamond  | 8        |
| Iron Bar | 5        |
| Glass    | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place the Tech Bench in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Anvil ──► Tech Bench ──► Fusion Station
```

The Tech Bench leads directly to the **Fusion Station**, the second-to-last crafting station in the main progression. You will need to smelt titanium ore here before you can build it.

## Tips

- **Titanium ore** is found in the Deep Underground layer (below depth 640). It is relatively rare, so mine every vein you find.
- The **Diamond Pickaxe** mines significantly faster than iron and can break the hardest blocks in the game. Prioritize crafting it.
- The **Crystal Staff** is a powerful mid-tier magic weapon. If you are running a magic build, this is a major upgrade over the Apprentice Staff.
- Start smelting titanium bars as soon as you build the Tech Bench. You need 10 for the Fusion Station, which means 40 titanium ore.
- The **Portal** allows fast travel between placed portal pairs. Very useful for connecting your base to deep mining areas.
