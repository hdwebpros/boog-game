The **Sleep Chamber** is a placeable crafting station that allows you to set a custom respawn point. When placed under a solid roof, interacting with it will set your spawn location so you respawn there instead of the default world spawn.

## Recipe

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 5 |
| Glass | 3 |
| Wood | 10 |

Crafted at a **Workbench Mk2**.

## How to Use

1. **Craft** the Sleep Chamber at a Workbench Mk2.
2. **Place** it in the world using RMB.
3. **Build a roof** above it — the chamber requires a solid block (not dirt, leaves, grass, sand, or snow) within 6 tiles above it.
4. **Stand near** the chamber and press **F** to set your spawn point.
5. When you die, you will respawn at the Sleep Chamber instead of the world spawn.

## Requirements

The Sleep Chamber must have a valid roof to function. Invalid roof materials include:
- Air, Dirt, Leaves, Grass, Snow, Sand
- Void Dirt, Void Grass, Void Leaves, Jungle Grass

Use **Stone**, **Wood**, **Iron**, or any other solid building material for the roof.

## Tips

- Place a Sleep Chamber near boss arenas so you can quickly return after dying.
- Build a small shelter with stone walls and roof to create a valid spawn point anywhere in the world.
- Your custom spawn point is saved with your world data.
- You can move the Sleep Chamber and set a new spawn point at any time.
