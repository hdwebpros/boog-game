The **Anvil** is a major milestone in Starfall's crafting progression. It unlocks the full iron-tier equipment set, the first magic and summon weapons, and branches into both the **Tech Bench** (main progression) and the **Arcane Anvil** (magic side branch).

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Furnace**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 10       |

## How to Use

1. Place the Anvil in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Furnace ──► Anvil ──┬──► Tech Bench
                    └──► Arcane Anvil
```

The Anvil is another critical junction. The **Tech Bench** continues the main crafting chain toward diamond and titanium gear. The **Arcane Anvil** is a side branch for magic-focused items and chant orbs.

## Tips

- The **Apprentice Staff** is your first magic weapon. It costs mana to use but deals strong damage at range. Well worth crafting if you have invested in the Magic skill branch.
- The **Drone Totem** is the first summon weapon. It spawns a drone minion that fights alongside you -- great for extra damage without changing your playstyle.
- Stockpile iron bars. You will need large amounts for iron armor, the Tech Bench recipe, and the Arcane Anvil.
- The **Rebreather** lets you breathe underwater, making ocean exploration much easier. Craft one before hunting the Deep Sea Leviathan boss.
