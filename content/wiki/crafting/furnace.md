The **Furnace** is the first smelting station in Starfall. It converts raw ores mined from underground into usable bars and materials. Without a Furnace, ore is just dead weight in your inventory. Building one unlocks the path to iron-tier equipment and beyond.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench Mk2**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Stone    | 20       |

## How to Use

1. Place the Furnace in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Available Recipes

### Refined Materials

| Item | Materials | Output |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Iron Bar | 3 Iron Ore | 1 |
| Diamond | 3 Diamond Ore | 1 |
| Glass | 4 Sand | 1 |

### Stations

| Item | Materials | Output |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Anvil | 10 Iron Bar | 1 |

## Crafting Progression

```
Workbench Mk2 ──► Furnace ──► Anvil
```

The Furnace is a stepping stone to the **Anvil**, which requires 10 iron bars to build. You will need to smelt at least 30 iron ore before you can progress.

## Tips

- Iron ore is found in the Underground layer (below depth 180). Look for darker-colored ore veins in cave walls.
- **Glass** is often overlooked but is needed for several advanced station recipes (Tech Bench, Fusion Station, Brewing Stand). Smelt sand early and stockpile it.
- Diamond ore appears deeper underground and is rarer. You will need diamonds for the Tech Bench, so mine them whenever you find them.
- The Furnace itself has a small recipe list, but every recipe here feeds into dozens of downstream crafts at higher-tier stations.
