The **Workbench Mk2** is the upgraded version of the basic Workbench. It unlocks stone-tier gear and, more importantly, provides access to the **Furnace** and **Brewing Stand** -- two critical stations for mid-game progression.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood     | 15       |
| Stone    | 10       |

## How to Use

1. Place the Workbench Mk2 in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Workbench ──► Workbench Mk2 ──┬──► Furnace
                               └──► Brewing Stand
```

The Workbench Mk2 is a critical junction. It branches into the **Furnace** (which leads to the main crafting chain) and the **Brewing Stand** (for potions).

## Tips

- The **Brewing Stand** requires iron bars and glass, which you need a Furnace to make. Build the Furnace first, smelt some iron and sand, then come back for the Brewing Stand.
- Stone tools and armor are a solid upgrade from wood. The Stone Pickaxe mines noticeably faster and can break harder blocks.
- Keep this station around even after progressing further. You may need to craft replacement stone gear if you die and lose equipment.
