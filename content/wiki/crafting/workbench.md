The **Workbench** is the first proper crafting station in Starfall. It unlocks the full range of wood-tier equipment and several essential utility items. Building one should be your top priority after landing.

## How to Obtain

Craft by hand (press **C** anywhere):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood     | 10       |

## How to Use

1. Place the Workbench in the world with **RMB**.
2. Stand within a few tiles of it.
3. Press **C** to open the crafting menu.
4. Select a recipe and click **Craft**.

## Crafting Progression

```
Hand Crafting ──► Workbench ──► Workbench Mk2
```

The Workbench sits at the start of the progression. Building a **Workbench Mk2** here unlocks the Furnace, Brewing Stand, and the rest of the crafting tree.

## Tips

- The **Healing Herb** is your earliest healing item. Craft several before exploring caves.
- **Vine Rope** is useful for descending into deep caverns safely. The recipe yields 8, so one craft goes a long way.
- Do not skip the **Workbench Mk2**. It is easy to miss, but it is required to progress to stone-tier gear and beyond.
- A **Chest** lets you store excess materials at your base. Place a few near your crafting area.
