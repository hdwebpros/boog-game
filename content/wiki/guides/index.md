# Guides

Whether you just crash-landed or you are gearing up for the Void Lord, these guides will help you through every stage of Starfall.

## Available Guides

### [Getting Started](/wiki/guides/getting-started)
Your first steps on the alien planet. Covers gathering resources, crafting basic tools, building shelter, and preparing for your first underground expedition.

### [Boss Strategies](/wiki/guides/boss-strategies)
Detailed strategies for all 6 main bosses and the Void Lord true final boss. Includes recommended gear, attack pattern breakdowns, and arena setup tips.

### [Void Dimension](/wiki/guides/void-dimension)
Everything you need to know about the post-game Void Dimension. How to craft the Super Portal, navigating the 4 void biomes, farming void materials, and reaching the Void Lord.

### [Controls & Hotkeys](/wiki/guides/controls-and-hotkeys)
Full reference for all keyboard and mouse controls, including movement, combat, inventory management, and multiplayer chat.

### [Inventory Management](/wiki/guides/inventory-management)
Tips for organizing your inventory, using the hotbar effectively, equipping armor and accessories, and managing stack sizes.

### [Enchanting](/wiki/guides/enchanting)
A walkthrough of the enchanting system, including how to unlock it, available enchantments, material costs, and recommended enchantment builds for different playstyles.
