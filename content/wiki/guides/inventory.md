# Inventory Guide

Effective inventory management is critical in Starfall. This guide covers every aspect of the inventory system, from basic hotbar usage to armor equipping, chest storage, and the Cloud City shop.

## Hotbar

The **hotbar** is the row of 10 slots always visible at the bottom of the screen. It holds your active items -- the ones you can use immediately.

- Press **1-9** or **0** to select a specific slot (0 = slot 10).
- Use the **scroll wheel** to cycle through slots.
- The highlighted slot is your "active" item. This determines what happens when you:
  - **LMB**: Mine (if tool) or attack (if weapon) or mine bare-handed (if block/other)
  - **RMB**: Place block (if block item selected)
  - **I**: Use/consume item (if consumable)

### Recommended Hotbar Layout

A well-organized hotbar saves lives. Here is a suggested layout:

| Slot | Item Type | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Pickaxe | Primary tool, always accessible |
| 2 | Sword / Melee weapon | Quick switch for close combat |
| 3 | Bow / Ranged weapon | For enemies at a distance |
| 4 | Magic weapon / Summon | Alternative damage source |
| 5-6 | Building blocks (dirt, stone) | For building, bridging, plugging holes |
| 7 | Torches | Light placement underground |
| 8 | Healing Herb / Cooked Meat | Emergency healing (press 8 then I) |
| 9 | Potion (Ironskin, etc.) | Buff before boss fights (press 9 then I) |
| 10 | Special item (compass, etc.) | Situational utility |

## Full Inventory Panel

Press **E** to open the full inventory. The panel shows:

- **Item grid**: Your main storage area. All carried items that are not in the hotbar.
- **Armor slots** (right side): 4 slots for helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots.
- **Accessory slots**: 3 slots below the armor slots.

Click an item to pick it up, then click a destination slot to place it. Click an empty area to cancel.

## Stacking Rules

Different item types have different maximum stack sizes:

| Item Type | Stack Limit | Examples |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Blocks | 99 | Dirt, Stone, Wood, Sand, Obsidian |
| Processed materials | 99 | Iron Bar, Diamond, Titanium Bar, Carbon Plate |
| Shards | 99 | Ember Shard, Frost Shard, Void Shard |
| Plant Fiber, Glass | 99 | Crafting intermediates |
| Healing Herb | 30 | Basic healing item |
| Cooked Meat | 20 | Better healing item |
| Potions | 10 | Ironskin, Swiftness, Rage, etc. |
| Chant Orbs | 10 | Inferno Chant, Glacial Chant, etc. |
| Silver Coins | 999 | Currency |
| Tools | 1 | Pickaxes |
| Weapons | 1 | Swords, Bows, Staves, etc. |
| Armor | 1 | Helmets, Chestplates, etc. |
| Accessories | 1 | Cloud Boots, Lucky Charm, etc. |
| Stations | 1 | Workbench, Furnace, Anvil, etc. |
| Chaos Shards | 50 | Void material (lower stack) |
| Dimensional Fabric | 30 | Void material (lower stack) |

When you pick up an item and already have a partial stack, the items merge up to the stack limit. Overflow goes to the next empty slot.

## Armor

Starfall has 6 armor tiers, each with 4 pieces:

| Tier | Set | Total Defense | Crafting Station |
|------|-----|---------------|------------------|
| 0 | Wood | 5 (1+2+1+1) | Workbench |
| 1 | Stone | 8 (2+3+2+1) | Workbench Mk2 |
| 2 | Iron | 13 (3+5+3+2) | Anvil |
| 3 | Diamond | 19 (4+7+5+3) | Tech Bench |
| 4 | Titanium | 27 (6+10+7+4) | Fusion Station |
| 5 | Void | 82 (18+28+22+14) | Void Forge |

To equip armor:
1. Press **E** to open inventory.
2. Click the armor piece.
3. Click the matching armor slot on the right side of the panel.

Defense reduces incoming damage from all sources. Higher defense = less damage taken per hit.

## Accessories

You have **3 accessory slots** below the armor slots. Accessories provide passive bonuses that are always active while equipped. Equip them the same way as armor -- click the accessory, then click an accessory slot.

### Available Accessories (from Sky Merchant)

| Accessory | Price | Effect |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Cloud Boots | 50 | No fall damage, +15% move speed |
| Star Compass | 80 | Reveals ores on minimap, +50 max mana |
| Gravity Belt | 120 | -40% jetpack fuel use, +20% flight speed |
| Miner's Lantern | 200 | +40% mining speed, +1 mining range |
| Lucky Charm | 150 | +25% double drop chance, better loot |
| Celestial Cape | 300 | +30 max HP, +30 max mana, magnetic item pickup |
| Explorer's Belt | 200 | +10 inventory slots (extra row) |

## Chest Storage

Chests provide extra storage outside your inventory. They are essential for organizing your base.

### Crafting a Chest

Craft at a **Workbench**: **15 Wood** = 1 Chest.

### Using Chests

1. Place the Chest with **RMB**.
2. Click the Chest to open it.
3. Move items between your inventory and the Chest by clicking.

### Organization Tips

- Dedicate chests to categories: "Ores & Bars", "Weapons", "Potions", "Boss Materials"
- Keep a chest near each crafting station with its relevant materials
- Store excess blocks you mine while digging (dirt, stone) rather than dropping them -- you will need building blocks later for boss arenas

## Dropping Items

Press **Q** to drop the currently selected hotbar item. The item appears on the ground near your character. Dropped items will despawn after some time, so only drop things you truly do not want.

There is no auto-delete or auto-sort system. You manually manage everything.

## Shopping

### Getting to the Shop

The **Sky Merchant** NPC is located in **Cloud City**, a floating island near the center of the world at approximately y=60 tiles. You need the jetpack (or creative building/grappling) to reach it.

### Using the Shop

1. Stand near the NPC shopkeeper.
2. Press **F** to open the shop interface.
3. Two tabs: **Buy** and **Sell**.

### Buying

The Buy tab shows all items the NPC sells with their Silver Coin prices. Click an item to purchase it (coins are deducted automatically).

### Selling

The Sell tab lets you sell items from your inventory for Silver Coins. Sell prices vary by item:

| Item Category | Sell Price Range |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Common blocks (dirt, grass, sand) | 1 coin each |
| Coral, mushroom, ice | 2 coins each |
| Iron Ore | 3 coins |
| Obsidian, Crystal | 5 coins |
| Diamond Ore | 8 coins |
| Titanium Ore | 10 coins |
| Carbon Fiber | 15 coins |
| Iron Bar | 5 coins |
| Diamond | 12 coins |
| Titanium Bar | 15 coins |
| Carbon Plate | 20 coins |
| Elemental Shards | 4-10 coins |
| Void materials | 10-100 coins |

### Currency

**Silver Coins** (stack to 999) are the game's currency. Earn them by:
- Selling items to the NPC
- Enemy drops (some enemies drop coins)
- Mining valuable ore and selling it

## Tips

- Keep at least one stack of healing items in your hotbar at all times.
- Before a boss fight, reorganize your hotbar: weapon in slot 1-2, healing in slot 8-9, potions in slot 7.
- The Explorer's Belt (+10 inventory slots) is a game-changing accessory for long mining trips. Buy it early if you can afford it (200 coins).
- Use chests liberally. A well-organized base saves time when crafting.
- Sell excess common blocks (dirt, stone, sand) to the NPC for Silver Coins. You will accumulate hundreds of these while mining.
- Always carry a few blocks (50+ dirt or stone) for emergency bridging, plugging lava, or building boss arenas.
