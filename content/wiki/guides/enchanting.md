# Enchanting Guide

The enchanting system lets you add elemental effects to your weapons, increasing their damage and adding special effects. This guide covers the full enchanting pipeline from finding crystals to applying the ultimate Eternal Chant.

## Overview

The enchanting chain works like this:

```
Mine Elemental Crystal blocks underground
  -> Receive Elemental Shards
    -> Craft Arcane Anvil (at Anvil station)
      -> Combine Shards + Arcane Dust = Chant Orbs (at Arcane Anvil)
        -> Apply Chant Orbs to weapons for enchantment effects
```

## Step 1: Find Elemental Crystals

Five types of elemental crystal blocks generate naturally underground:

| Crystal Block | Shard Dropped | Color | Tier | Typical Depth |
|---------------|---------------|-------|------|---------------|
| Ember Crystal | Ember Shard | Orange-red | 2 | Underground to Deep |
| Frost Crystal | Frost Shard | Light blue | 2 | Underground to Deep |
| Storm Crystal | Storm Shard | Yellow | 2 | Underground to Deep |
| Void Crystal | Void Shard | Purple | 3 | Deep Underground and below |
| Life Crystal | Life Shard | Green | 1 | Underground |

Crystals appear in clusters underground. They are relatively rare, so explore thoroughly. The **Spelunker Potion** (highlights ores through walls) and **Mystical Compass** (points toward nearby shard deposits) help locate them.

### Arcane Dust

**Arcane Dust** is a crafting ingredient found throughout the underground. You need it for almost every enchanting recipe. It drops when mining Crystal blocks and other rare formations. Stock up on it -- you will need a lot (at least 20-30 for a basic enchanting setup).

## Step 2: Craft the Arcane Anvil

The **Arcane Anvil** is your enchanting station. Craft it at a regular **Anvil**:

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Iron Bar | 8 |
| Diamond | 4 |
| Arcane Dust | 5 |

Place the Arcane Anvil in your base. All chant orb recipes and the Mystical Compass are crafted here.

## Step 3: Craft Chant Orbs

Stand near the Arcane Anvil and press **C** to open crafting. Each elemental chant orb requires 5 of the corresponding shard plus 3 Arcane Dust:

| Chant Orb | Recipe | Enchantment Name |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Inferno Chant | 5 Ember Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | Inferno |
| Glacial Chant | 5 Frost Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | Glacial |
| Tempest Chant | 5 Storm Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | Tempest |
| Abyssal Chant | 5 Void Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | Abyssal |
| Verdant Chant | 5 Life Shard + 3 Arcane Dust | Verdant |

Chant orbs stack to 10, so you can stockpile them.

## Step 4: Apply Enchantments

Use a Chant Orb on a weapon to enchant it. The enchantment adds bonus elemental damage and a visual effect to the weapon.

## The 6 Enchantment Types

### Inferno (Ember)
- **Bonus**: Fire damage over time
- **Effect**: Enemies hit by the weapon burn, taking additional damage per second
- **Best for**: Sustained damage, melee weapons
- **Color**: Orange-red glow

### Glacial (Frost)
- **Bonus**: Ice slow effect
- **Effect**: Enemies hit are slowed, reducing their movement speed
- **Best for**: Kiting enemies, ranged weapons
- **Color**: Light blue glow

### Tempest (Storm)
- **Bonus**: Lightning damage
- **Effect**: Bonus lightning damage on hit
- **Best for**: Burst damage, fast weapons
- **Color**: Yellow glow

### Abyssal (Void)
- **Bonus**: Void damage
- **Effect**: Strong bonus damage, especially effective against void enemies
- **Best for**: Endgame content, void dimension
- **Color**: Purple glow

### Verdant (Life)
- **Bonus**: Life steal / healing
- **Effect**: Heals the player for a portion of damage dealt
- **Best for**: Sustain in long fights, melee weapons
- **Color**: Green glow

### Eternal (All Combined)
- **Bonus**: All elemental effects combined
- **Effect**: The ultimate enchantment -- fire, ice, lightning, void, and life effects all at once
- **Best for**: Your absolute best weapon
- **Color**: Gold glow

## The Eternal Chant

The **Eternal Chant** is the most powerful enchantment, combining all 5 elements. It is crafted at the Arcane Anvil by combining all 5 chant orbs:

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Inferno Chant | 1 |
| Glacial Chant | 1 |
| Tempest Chant | 1 |
| Abyssal Chant | 1 |
| Verdant Chant | 1 |

This means you need at minimum: 25 total shards (5 of each type) + 15 Arcane Dust (3 per chant) + 5 additional chant orbs.

Total materials for one Eternal Chant:
- 5 Ember Shards
- 5 Frost Shards
- 5 Storm Shards
- 5 Void Shards
- 5 Life Shards
- 15 Arcane Dust (for the 5 chants)

## Utility Items

### Mystical Compass

Craft at the **Arcane Anvil**:

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Arcane Dust | 8 |
| Diamond | 3 |
| Iron Bar | 5 |

The Mystical Compass points toward the nearest shard deposit when held, making it much easier to locate crystal clusters underground.

### Shard Compasses

After accessing the Void Dimension, you can craft **Shard Compasses** at the **Void Forge**. These are element-specific compasses that point to a single shard type:

| Compass | Recipe (at Void Forge) |
|---------|------------------------|
| Ember Shard Compass | 10 Ember Shard + 5 Hellfire Core + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Frost Shard Compass | 10 Frost Shard + 5 Soul Fragment + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Storm Shard Compass | 10 Storm Shard + 3 Chaos Shard + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Void Shard Compass | 10 Void Shard + 2 Dimensional Fabric + 3 Abyssal Ingot |
| Life Shard Compass | 10 Life Shard + 5 Void Essence + 2 Abyssal Ingot |

## Tips

- Start collecting Arcane Dust early. Even before you can craft the Arcane Anvil, stockpile it whenever you see it underground.
- Life Crystals (green) are the most common and spawn at shallow depths. Ember and Frost Crystals are medium rarity. Void Crystals are the rarest and spawn deepest.
- Enchant your best weapon first. If you are using a Titanium Sword as your main weapon, put the Eternal Chant on that.
- The Glacial (slow) enchantment is extremely useful for boss fights where kiting is your strategy.
- The Verdant (life steal) enchantment on a melee weapon provides excellent sustain, reducing your reliance on healing items.
- Save your first Eternal Chant for a weapon you will use for a long time. Do not waste it on a mid-tier weapon you will replace soon.
- The Spelunker Potion makes finding crystal clusters much easier -- ores and crystals glow through walls for 90 seconds.
