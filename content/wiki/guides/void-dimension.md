# Void Dimension Guide

The Void Dimension is Starfall's endgame content. It is a separate, enclosed world filled with powerful enemies, new materials, and the resources needed to face the true final boss. This guide covers everything from entry to the final confrontation.

## Prerequisites

Before you can enter the Void Dimension, you must:

1. **Defeat all 6 main bosses** and collect their jetpack components.
2. **Assemble the Jetpack** at a Fusion Station.
3. **Escape the planet** (fly to y < 32 to trigger the ending).
4. **Return to your world** after the ending plays.

Only after completing the main game does the Super Portal recipe become available.

## Crafting the Super Portal

Craft the **Super Portal** at a **Fusion Station**:

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Obsidian | 99 |
| Arcane Dust | 40 |
| Titanium Bar | 30 |

Obsidian is found in the Deep Underground and Core layers. Arcane Dust drops from mining Crystal blocks underground. Titanium Bar is smelted from Titanium Ore at a Tech Bench.

## Entering the Void

1. Place the **Super Portal** in the world with RMB.
2. Stand near it and press **F** to activate.
3. A cutscene plays (~20 seconds) showing the portal activation sequence.
4. You are transported to the Void Dimension.

## Void Dimension Layout

The Void Dimension is an enclosed world of **3000 x 800 tiles** (smaller than the overworld). It is divided into 4 biome zones, each with distinct terrain and enemies:

### Ashen Wastes
The entry zone. Barren, rocky terrain with ash-covered ground. Lower-tier void enemies spawn here. Good for initial material gathering.

### Hellfire Caverns
Lava lakes, brimstone, and extreme heat. **Hellfire Imps** and **Nether Golems** patrol here. This is where you find **Hellfire Core** and **Brimstone** to craft the Void Forge.

### Void Forest
Twisted dark trees and glowing void crystals. **Void Wraiths**, **Soul Eaters**, and **Void Serpents** roam the forests. Rich source of **Void Essence** and **Soul Fragments**.

### Dark Citadel
The deepest, most dangerous zone. Nether brick structures house **Dark Knights**, **Shadow Stalkers**, and **Chaos Elementals**. This is where you find **Chaos Shards** and **Dimensional Fabric** materials.

## The Void Forge

| Ingredient | Amount |

Place the Void Forge in either dimension to use it. All void weapons, armor, and the Void Lord Summon Token are crafted here.

## Returning to the Overworld

Use the void portal to return to the overworld. **Warning:** After returning from the Void Dimension, the overworld enemy spawn rate increases by **20x**. The world becomes much more dangerous, so be prepared.

## Artifacts

**Artifacts** are special accessories purchased and upgraded at the **Sky Merchant** in Cloud City. There are 6 artifacts, each with 3 upgrade tiers. The base item is purchased with Silver Coins, and upgrades cost additional coins.

| Artifact | Base Price | Effect (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3) |
|----------|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| Warp Crystal | 500 | Teleport dash (5s cd) / Longer dash (3.5s cd) / Damaging dash (2s cd, 30 dmg) |
| Soul Lantern | 500 | 5% lifesteal / 10% lifesteal / 15% lifesteal + heal allies |
| Chaos Heart | 750 | +100 HP + thorns / +150 HP + stronger thorns / +200 HP + ranged thorns |
| Void Eye | 750 | See through walls + reveal enemies / +15% crit vs revealed / Auto-mark enemies |
| Temporal Shard | 600 | 15% CDR + 10% AS / 25% CDR + 20% AS / 35% CDR + 30% AS + slow enemies |
| Dimensional Anchor | 1000 | Knockback immune + 25% dmg / +40% dmg + 15 armor / +60% dmg + 30 armor |

Upgrade costs: Tier 2 costs 300-500 coins, Tier 3 costs 600-1000 coins (varies by artifact).

## The True Final Boss: The Void Lord

After visiting the Void Dimension and gathering materials, you can face the ultimate challenge.

### Crafting the Summon Token

At the **Void Forge**, craft the **Void Lord Summon Token**:

| Ingredient | Amount |
|------------|--------|
| Chaos Shard | 10 |
| Dimensional Fabric | 3 |
| Abyssal Ingot | 5 |

### Summoning

Return to the **overworld** with the token. Select it and press **F** to summon **The Void Lord**. The boss spawns at your location.

### The Fight

The Void Lord has **5,000 HP** and 4 phases with escalating damage (80 to 150) and speed (100 to 160). It uses void beams, dimensional rifts, minion summoning, and teleportation. See the [Boss Strategy Guide](/wiki/guides/boss-strategies) for detailed tactics.

### True Ending

Defeating the Void Lord triggers the **True Ending** sequence (~35 seconds), unlocking the final story conclusion. The Trophy of the Void Lord item serves as proof of your victory.

## Recommended Progression in the Void

1. Enter the Void with Titanium gear and plenty of potions.
2. Farm Void Essence and Hellfire Core in the Ashen Wastes and Hellfire Caverns.
3. Craft the Void Forge (20 Brimstone + 10 Hellfire Core at Fusion Station).
4. Craft Abyssal Ingots and start building Void Armor.
5. Push into the Void Forest for Soul Fragments.
6. Reach the Dark Citadel for Chaos Shards and Dimensional Fabric.
7. Craft Void weapons and complete your Void Armor set.
8. Return to the overworld and buy/upgrade artifacts at the Sky Merchant.
9. Invest skill points in Ascension skills (requires 2 super trees completed).
10. Craft the Void Lord Summon Token and face the true final boss.
