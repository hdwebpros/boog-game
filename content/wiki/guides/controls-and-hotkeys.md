# Controls & Hotkeys

A complete reference for every control and hotkey in Starfall. Print this page or keep it open in a tab until the controls become second nature.

## Movement Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **A** or **Left Arrow** | Move left |
| **D** or **Right Arrow** | Move right |
| **W** or **Up Arrow** | Climb ladders / Move up |
| **S** or **Down Arrow** | Descend ladders / Move down |
| **Space** | Jump |
| **Space** (hold while airborne) | Activate jetpack (when equipped) |

**Notes:**
- You can also press **W** to jump.
- Double Jump is available after unlocking the Double Jump skill in the Mobility branch.
- Jetpack flight consumes fuel. The fuel bar recharges when you are standing on the ground.

## Combat Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **LMB (Left Click)** | Attack with equipped weapon / Mine blocks |
| **RMB (Right Click)** | Place blocks / Use secondary item action |

**Notes:**
- Hold LMB to continuously mine a block. Mining speed depends on your tool tier and any mining speed bonuses.
- The attack type depends on your equipped weapon style:
  - **Melee**: Swing arc in front of you (126 degree arc, ~4.5 tile range)
  - **Ranged**: Fires a projectile toward the cursor
  - **Magic**: Fires a magic projectile toward the cursor (costs mana)
  - **Summon**: Spawns a minion that fights for you (costs mana)
- Switch to a weapon before approaching enemies. Getting caught with a pickaxe equipped is a common mistake.

## Building Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **RMB (Right Click)** | Place selected block at cursor position |
| **LMB (Left Click)** | Mine / break block at cursor position |

**Notes:**
- You can only place blocks from your hotbar if you have a block-type item selected.
- Blocks must be placed adjacent to existing blocks (you cannot place blocks in mid-air with no neighbors).
- Crafting stations (Workbench, Furnace, etc.) are placed the same way as blocks.

## Inventory Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **1** through **9** | Select hotbar slot 1-9 |
| **0** | Select hotbar slot 10 |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Cycle through hotbar slots |
| **E** | Open / close full inventory panel |
| **Q** | Drop the currently selected item |
| **I** | Use / consume the selected item (eat food, drink potions) |

**Notes:**
- In the inventory panel, click items to pick them up and click a slot to place them.
- Armor is equipped by clicking the armor piece, then clicking the corresponding armor slot (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots).
- Accessories go in the 3 accessory slots below the armor slots.
- The inventory stays open while the game continues -- be careful in dangerous areas.

## Crafting Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **C** | Open / close crafting menu |

**Notes:**
- You must be standing near a crafting station to see its recipes.
- Hand-craft recipes (Workbench, Torch) are available anywhere.
- The crafting menu shows which recipes you have the materials for.
- Click a recipe to craft it.

## Map Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **N** | Toggle minimap overlay |
| **M** | Open world map |
| **[ / ]** | Zoom map in / out |

**Notes:**
- The minimap shows a small overhead view of your surroundings in the corner of the screen.
- The world map shows a larger view. Use **[** and **]** to zoom in and out.

## Menu Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **ESC** | Open pause menu (save game, settings, quit) |
| **TAB** | Open skill tree |

**Notes:**
- The game pauses when you open the pause menu (single player).
- In multiplayer, the pause menu shows the room code and current difficulty.
- The skill tree shows all available branches: Combat, Survival, Mining, Magic, Mobility, and Ascension.
- Click a skill to spend skill points on it. Skill points are earned by leveling up (XP from killing enemies).

## Interaction Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **F** | Context-sensitive interaction |

The **F** key does different things depending on what you are near:
- **Boss Altar**: Summon the boss (consumes ingredients from inventory)
- **NPC Shopkeeper**: Open the shop interface (buy/sell tabs)
- **Portal / Super Portal**: Activate and teleport
- **Void Lord Summon Token** (selected): Summon the Void Lord boss

## Multiplayer Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **T** | Open chat overlay |

**Notes:**
- Type your message and press Enter to send.
- Press Escape or T again to close chat.
- Multiplayer uses a room code system. The host creates a room, and others join by entering the room code.

## Tips & Tricks

- **Hold LMB** to continuously mine. You do not need to click repeatedly.
- **Switch to your weapon** before exploring. Getting caught with a pickaxe or block selected when an enemy appears wastes precious reaction time.
- **Keep healing items in hotbar slots 8-9** so you can quickly press the number key, then **I** to heal during combat.
- **Press Q to drop unwanted items** and free up inventory space while mining deep underground.
- **Use the scroll wheel** for quick hotbar switching during combat -- it is faster than reaching for number keys.
- **Press C near different stations** to see different recipe lists. Each station unlocks its own set of recipes.
- **Place Torches** (RMB with torch selected) liberally underground. Light helps you spot ores and enemies.
- **Build platform arenas** before summoning bosses. Vertical mobility through platforms gives you a huge advantage.
- **F near a shop NPC** opens the shop. Sell excess materials for Silver Coins to buy accessories.
