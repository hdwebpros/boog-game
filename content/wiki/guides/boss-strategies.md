# Boss Strategy Guide

Starfall has **6 main bosses**, each dropping a unique jetpack component. Defeat all six, assemble the Jetpack at a Fusion Station, and fly to escape altitude (y < 32) to complete the game. After that, the **Void Lord** awaits as the true final boss.

## How Boss Summoning Works

Bosses are summoned at **Altars** scattered throughout the world. Each boss has a specific altar type that spawns in the appropriate depth range. To summon a boss:

1. Find the boss's altar (look for colored stone structures at the correct depth).
2. Gather the required summoning ingredients.
3. Stand near the altar with the ingredients in your inventory.
4. Press **F** to summon the boss.

The boss spawns immediately. Make sure you are prepared before summoning.

## Boss Progression Order

Fight bosses in this order, matching the gear tier you should have by that point:

---

### 1. Vine Guardian

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 300 |
| Damage | 15 (Phase 1), 20 (Phase 2) |
| Location | Surface / Near-surface |
| Drop | Fuel Cell Casing |

**Altar Ingredients:** 10 Wood + 20 Leaves + 5 Plant Fiber

**Recommended Gear:** Iron Sword (22 dmg) or better, Iron Armor (13 defense), Healing Herbs

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-50% HP): Ground movement, vine melee attacks, summons Space Slugs. Attacks every 2 seconds.
- Phase 2 (below 50% HP): Faster movement (80 speed), stronger hits (20 dmg), attacks every 1.5 seconds.

**Strategy:** This is the easiest boss. Keep your distance and use a ranged weapon (Iron Bow) or circle-strafe with a melee weapon. Kill the summoned Space Slugs quickly so they don't overwhelm you. Bring 5-10 Healing Herbs.

---

### 2. Deep Sea Leviathan

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 500 |
| Damage | 20 (Phase 1), 30 (Phase 2) |
| Location | Surface to Underground (ocean/lake areas) |
| Drop | Thrust Regulator |

**Altar Ingredients:** 15 Coral + 20 Sand + 8 Plant Fiber

**Recommended Gear:** Iron gear, Rebreather (for underwater breathing), Healing Herbs or Cooked Meat

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-50% HP): Swimming movement, charge attacks + bubble projectiles. 2.5 second attack interval.
- Phase 2 (below 50% HP): Much faster (100 speed), 30 damage charges, 1.8 second attacks.

**Strategy:** The Leviathan fights in water, so craft a **Rebreather** (30 Coral + 20 Sand + 5 Glass + 3 Iron Bar at Anvil) to survive underwater. Use a ranged weapon to chip away at it between charges. The charge attack is telegraphed -- move perpendicular to dodge it. Water Walking Potion helps if you want to fight from the surface.

---

### 3. Crystal Golem

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 800 |
| Damage | 30 / 40 / 50 (3 phases) |
| Location | Underground to Deep Underground |
| Drop | Pressure Valve |

**Altar Ingredients:** 6 Iron Bar + 4 Diamond Ore + 3 Glass

**Recommended Gear:** Diamond Sword (35 dmg), Iron or Diamond Armor, Ironskin Potion, Regeneration Potion

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-60% HP): Slow ground movement (40 speed), slam attacks, occasional crystal rain. 3 second interval.
- Phase 2 (60-30% HP): Faster (55 speed), 40 damage, crystal rain intensifies. 2.2 second interval.
- Phase 3 (below 30% HP): Fast (70 speed), 50 damage, relentless attacks every 1.5 seconds.

**Strategy:** The Golem is tanky and hits hard but is slow in Phase 1. Build a small arena with platforms to dodge the crystal rain. Rush damage early with melee, and switch to ranged kiting when Phase 3 starts. Ironskin Potion (+10 defense) is very helpful here. Bring an Apprentice Staff if you have one -- magic weapons let you attack from safety.

---

### 4. Magma Wyrm

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 1000 |
| Damage | 35 / 45 / 55 (3 phases) |
| Location | Deep Underground to Core |
| Drop | Energy Capacitor |

**Altar Ingredients:** 5 Diamond + 3 Titanium Bar + 4 Iron Bar

**Recommended Gear:** Diamond or Titanium weapons, Diamond Armor minimum, Rage Potion, Endurance Potion

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-50% HP): Flying serpent, fire breath + dive attacks. 80 speed, 2 second interval.
- Phase 2 (50-20% HP): 110 speed, 45 damage, 1.5 second attacks.
- Phase 3 (below 20% HP): Enraged -- 140 speed, 55 damage, attacks every second.

**Strategy:** The Wyrm flies and is very fast. Build a multi-level arena with platforms before summoning. The fire breath has a linear hitbox -- dodge by moving vertically. During dive attacks, move sideways at the last moment. Phase 3 is brutal; save your strongest potions (Wrath, Rage) for this phase and focus on burst damage. A Crystal Staff (32 magic dmg) is excellent here.

---

### 5. Core Sentinel

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 1200 |
| Damage | 40 / 50 / 60 (3 phases) |
| Location | Core layer |
| Drop | Ignition Core |

**Altar Ingredients:** 5 Carbon Plate + 4 Titanium Bar + 3 Diamond

**Recommended Gear:** Titanium weapons, Titanium Armor (27 defense), multiple potion types, Forcefield Potion for emergencies

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-60% HP): Flying, laser beam attacks + energy shield phases. 60 speed, 2.5 second interval.
- Phase 2 (60-25% HP): 80 speed, 50 damage, more frequent shield phases. 1.8 second interval.
- Phase 3 (below 25% HP): 100 speed, 60 damage, near-constant attacks every 1.2 seconds.

**Strategy:** The Sentinel alternates between attacking and shielding. During shield phases, you cannot deal damage, so use that time to heal and reposition. The laser attack has a brief charge-up animation -- when you see it, move immediately. Keep the Forcefield Potion (8 seconds of immunity) ready for Phase 3 emergencies. The Laser Gun (45 dmg ranged) is ideal for this fight.

---

### 6. Mothership

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 1500 |
| Damage | 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 (4 phases) |
| Location | Near surface |
| Drop | Navigation Module |

**Altar Ingredients:** 10 Carbon Plate + 5 Titanium Bar + 5 Diamond

**Recommended Gear:** Best gear available (Titanium weapons minimum), full Titanium Armor, all buff potions, Forcefield Potions

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-70% HP): Flying, spawns attack drones, occasional beam. 50 speed, 3 second interval.
- Phase 2 (70-40% HP): More drones, stronger beam. 60 speed, 60 damage, 2.5 second interval.
- Phase 3 (40-15% HP): Intense drone swarms + beam attacks. 75 speed, 70 damage, 1.8 second interval.
- Phase 4 (below 15% HP): Desperation mode. 90 speed, 80 damage, attacks every 1.2 seconds.

**Strategy:** The Mothership is the final main boss and is a war of attrition. The spawned drones must be dealt with or they overwhelm you. Use a Swarm Beacon (summon weapon) to have minions fight drones while you focus the Mothership. Build a large arena with cover blocks to break line of sight from the beam. Stack every buff you can: Ironskin, Rage, Wrath, Endurance, Regeneration. Save Forcefield Potions for Phase 4. This fight will take a while -- bring plenty of healing items.

---

## After All 6 Bosses

Once you have all 6 jetpack components:

1. **Fuel Cell Casing** (from Vine Guardian)
2. **Thrust Regulator** (from Deep Sea Leviathan)
3. **Pressure Valve** (from Crystal Golem)
4. **Energy Capacitor** (from Magma Wyrm)
5. **Ignition Core** (from Core Sentinel)
6. **Navigation Module** (from Mothership)

Go to a **Fusion Station** and craft the **Jetpack**. Equip it, then fly upward by holding **Space** while airborne. When you reach altitude y < 32, the ending sequence triggers and you escape the planet.

---

## True Final Boss: The Void Lord

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| HP | 5,000 |
| Damage | 80 / 100 / 120 / 150 (4 phases) |
| Size | 128 x 192 pixels (massive) |
| Location | Summoned in the overworld |
| Drop | Trophy of the Void Lord |

**Prerequisites:** Complete the main game (escape once), visit the Void Dimension, and craft the Void Lord Summon Token.

**Summon Token Recipe (Void Forge):** 10 Chaos Shard + 3 Dimensional Fabric + 5 Abyssal Ingot

**Recommended Gear:** Full Void Armor (82 defense), Void or Chaos-tier weapons, all artifacts upgraded, Ascension skills unlocked, every buff potion available.

**Attack Patterns:**
- Phase 1 (100-75% HP): Void beams, teleportation, dimensional rifts. 100 speed, 2 second interval.
- Phase 2 (75-50% HP): Summons void minions, stronger beams. 120 speed, 100 damage, 1.6 second interval.
- Phase 3 (50-25% HP): Minion summoning intensifies, teleportation more frequent. 140 speed, 120 damage, 1.2 second interval.
- Phase 4 (below 25% HP): Enrage. Maximum speed (160), 150 damage, attacks every 0.8 seconds. All attack types used simultaneously.

**Strategy:** This is the hardest fight in the game. You need Void-tier gear, fully upgraded artifacts, and ideally Ascension skills. The Void Lord teleports frequently, so ranged weapons are essential -- the Dimensional Rifle (120 dmg) or Arcane Annihilator (130 magic dmg) are ideal. The Chaos Edge (150 melee dmg) works if you can keep up with the teleportation. Kill summoned minions quickly. Use the Dimensional Anchor artifact for knockback immunity and +60% damage. Stack Wrath Potion (+25% all damage) with the Chaos Mastery skill (+50% all damage) for maximum burst. Save Forcefield Potions for Phase 4 emergencies. Defeating the Void Lord triggers the true ending.

See the [Void Dimension Guide](/wiki/guides/void-dimension) for full details on accessing and surviving the void.
