The **Wood Pickaxe** is the first tool a player should craft after landing. With 1.5x mining speed and the ability to mine tier 0 blocks, it allows you to gather basic resources like dirt, sand, and wood much faster than mining by hand. It is essential for early-game progression.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood     | 8        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value |
|--------------|-------|
| Mining Speed | 1.5x  |
| Mines Tier   | 0     |
| Tier         | 0     |

## Tips

- Craft this immediately after placing your Workbench. Mining without a pickaxe is painfully slow.
- The Wood Pickaxe can only mine tier 0 blocks. You will need a Stone Pickaxe to mine stone and other tier 1 materials.
- Keep this in your hotbar at all times during the early game. Switch to it with the number keys before mining.

## Controls

Equip the pickaxe and press **LMB** on a block to mine it. Higher-tier blocks cannot be mined with this pickaxe. Use **RMB** to place blocks.
