The **Stone Pickaxe** is a tier 1 tool that unlocks mining of stone and other tier 1 blocks. With 2.0x mining speed, it mines noticeably faster than the Wood Pickaxe. This is a critical upgrade that opens up access to underground resources.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench Mk2**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Stone    | 12       |
| Wood     | 4        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value |
|--------------|-------|
| Mining Speed | 2.0x  |
| Mines Tier   | 1     |
| Tier         | 1     |

## Tips

- The Stone Pickaxe is your gateway to the underground. Prioritize crafting it so you can mine iron ore and other valuable resources.
- At 2.0x speed, mining feels significantly faster than the Wood Pickaxe, saving you time on large excavations.
- You will need to upgrade to an Iron Pickaxe to mine tier 2 blocks like diamond ore.

## Controls

Equip the pickaxe and press **LMB** on a block to mine it. Blocks at tier 2 or higher cannot be mined with this pickaxe. Use **RMB** to place blocks.
