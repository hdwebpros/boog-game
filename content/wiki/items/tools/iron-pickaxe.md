The **Iron Pickaxe** is a tier 2 tool forged at an Anvil. With 3.0x mining speed and the ability to mine tier 2 blocks, it is the workhorse of mid-game mining. This pickaxe lets you harvest diamond ore and other valuable deep underground resources.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 8        |
| Wood     | 4        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value |
|--------------|-------|
| Mining Speed | 3.0x  |
| Mines Tier   | 2     |
| Tier         | 2     |

## Tips

- The Iron Pickaxe is a major upgrade in mining speed. Use it to carve out large areas quickly when exploring the underground.
- This pickaxe can mine diamond ore, which is essential for crafting tier 3 equipment.
- Iron bars require smelting iron ore at a furnace, so set up your smelting infrastructure first.

## Controls

Equip the pickaxe and press **LMB** on a block to mine it. Blocks at tier 3 or higher cannot be mined with this pickaxe. Use **RMB** to place blocks.
