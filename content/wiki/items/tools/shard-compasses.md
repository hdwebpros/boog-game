# Shard Compasses

**Shard Compasses** are a set of 5 special items crafted at the Void Forge. Each compass corresponds to one of the crystal shard types and helps you locate deposits of that shard in the world.

## All Shard Compasses

| Compass | ID | Tier | Color | Crafting Cost |
|---------|----|------|-------|---------------|
| Ember Shard Compass | 385 | 3 | Orange | 10 Ember Shard + 5 Hellfire Core + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Frost Shard Compass | 386 | 3 | Blue | 10 Frost Shard + 5 Hellfire Core + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Storm Shard Compass | 387 | 3 | Yellow | 10 Storm Shard + 5 Soul Fragment + 2 Abyssal Ingot |
| Void Shard Compass | 388 | 4 | Purple | 10 Void Shard + 2 Dimensional Fabric + 3 Abyssal Ingot |
| Life Shard Compass | 389 | 3 | Green | 10 Life Shard + 5 Void Essence + 2 Abyssal Ingot |

## Crafting Station

All Shard Compasses are crafted at the **Void Forge**, which itself requires endgame materials. These are post-game items intended for players who have already visited the Void Dimension.

## Tips

- Shard Compasses do not stack (stack size 1 each).
- The Void Shard Compass is tier 4, one tier higher than the others, reflecting the rarity of Void Shards.
- These compasses are useful for farming specific crystal shards needed for enchanting and artifact upgrades.
