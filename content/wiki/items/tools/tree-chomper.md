# Tree Chomper

The **Tree Chomper** is a specialized harvesting tool designed for quickly clearing trees and plant blocks. It mines wood, leaves, grass, and other soft blocks at 3x speed -- faster than a Stone Pickaxe but limited to tier 0 blocks.

## What It Can Mine

The Tree Chomper has a mining tier of 0, meaning it can only break blocks at the lowest hardness level:

- Wood
- Leaves
- Grass
- Dirt
- Sand
- Other tier 0 blocks

It **cannot** mine stone, ores, or any block requiring a pickaxe of tier 1 or higher.

## Tips

- The Tree Chomper is not a replacement for your pickaxe -- it is a companion tool for surface gathering.
- Its 3x mining speed makes clearing forests for wood extremely fast compared to using a pickaxe.
- Keep it in your hotbar alongside your main pickaxe for quick switching between tree harvesting and ore mining.
- Especially useful when you need large quantities of wood for building or crafting.
