The **Diamond Pickaxe** is a tier 3 tool crafted at the Tech Bench. At 4.0x mining speed, it tears through blocks rapidly and can mine tier 3 materials including titanium ore. This pickaxe is essential for late-game resource gathering.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Tech Bench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Diamond  | 6        |
| Iron Bar | 3        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value |
|--------------|-------|
| Mining Speed | 4.0x  |
| Mines Tier   | 3     |
| Tier         | 3     |

## Tips

- The Diamond Pickaxe unlocks titanium ore mining, which is needed for tier 4 equipment and the Fusion Station recipes.
- At 4.0x speed, mining becomes very fast. Use this to quickly carve tunnels through the Deep Underground.
- Diamonds are found in the Deep Underground layer. You need an Iron Pickaxe or better to mine them.

## Controls

Equip the pickaxe and press **LMB** on a block to mine it. Blocks at tier 4 or higher cannot be mined with this pickaxe. Use **RMB** to place blocks.
