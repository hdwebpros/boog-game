# Boring Drill

The **Boring Drill** is an automated mining machine that, once placed, drills straight down through the entire world. It harvests every tile in its path and drops the loot for you to collect.

## Stats

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Item ID | 420 |
| Category | Special |
| Tier | 2 |
| Stack Size | 5 |
| Drill Width | 2 tiles |
| Drill Speed | 12 rows/second |

## How It Works

1. **Place** the Boring Drill on the ground (RMB).
2. The drill activates immediately and begins drilling downward.
3. It processes **2 tiles wide** directly below where it was placed, at a rate of **12 rows per second**.
4. Every block it destroys drops its corresponding item, which accumulates and can be picked up.
5. The drill skips Air, Water, Lava, and Portal blocks.
6. When the drill reaches the bottom of the world, it **explodes** with a flash effect.

## Special Drops

- **Crystal tiles** (Ember, Frost, Storm, Void, Life crystals) drop their corresponding shards.
- Crystal tiles also have a **50% chance** to drop bonus Arcane Dust alongside the shard.

## Tips

- The Boring Drill is excellent for quickly accessing deep layers without manually digging.
- Place it near your base and collect the drops when it finishes -- it accumulates a huge pile of materials.
- Stack up to 5 drills and place them side by side for wider excavation.
- The drill cannot be recovered once placed -- it drills until it hits the bottom and explodes.
- Great for farming crystal shards and the bonus Arcane Dust drops.
