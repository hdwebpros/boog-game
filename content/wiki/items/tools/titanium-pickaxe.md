The **Titanium Pickaxe** is the most powerful mining tool in Starfall. With 5.0x mining speed and the ability to mine tier 4 blocks, it can break through any block in the game. Crafted at the Fusion Station from titanium bars and carbon plates, it represents the pinnacle of mining technology.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Titanium Bar | 8        |
| Carbon Plate | 2        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value |
|--------------|-------|
| Mining Speed | 5.0x  |
| Mines Tier   | 4     |
| Tier         | 4     |

## Tips

- The Titanium Pickaxe can mine every block in the game, including void stone and other tier 4 materials.
- At 5.0x speed, blocks break almost instantly. This is invaluable for exploring the Void Dimension quickly.
- This is the last pickaxe you will ever need. Once crafted, you can mine anything in both the overworld and the Void Dimension.

## Controls

Equip the pickaxe and press **LMB** on a block to mine it. This pickaxe can mine all block types. Use **RMB** to place blocks.
