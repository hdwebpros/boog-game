**Nether Brick** is a dark, structured block that forms the walls, floors, and towers of the Dark Citadel zone in the Void Dimension. It has a built or constructed appearance, suggesting an ancient civilization once inhabited the void.

## Where to Find

Nether Brick is found exclusively in the **Dark Citadel** biome zone within the Void Dimension. The Dark Citadel contains pre-built structures made from these bricks, including towers, walls, and corridors.

## Uses

- **Building**: A dark, fortress-style building material with a structured appearance.
- **Terrain**: Forms the structures of the Dark Citadel zone.

## Tips

- The Dark Citadel is the most dangerous zone in the Void Dimension, home to Chaos Elementals and Dark Knights that drop Chaos Shards.
- Nether Brick structures can provide natural cover during fights with Dark Citadel enemies.
- Mining through Nether Brick walls can open shortcuts through the citadel, but be careful not to break into rooms full of enemies.
- If you want a dark, gothic aesthetic for your base, Nether Brick is one of the best decorative blocks available.
