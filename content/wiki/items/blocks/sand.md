**Sand** is a soft surface block found primarily in Desert biomes and along the shores of Lake biomes. Its main value is as a crafting ingredient: it can be smelted into Glass at a Furnace.

## Where to Find

- **Desert biomes**: Large expanses of sand on the surface, easy to mine in bulk.
- **Lake biomes**: Found along lakeshores and underwater.

## Properties

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Tile ID | 11 |
| Tier | 0 |
| Hardness | Very low |
| Tool Required | None (bare hands work) |

## Uses

- **Glass** (4 Sand smelted at a Furnace)
- **Building**: Can be used as a building block, though it is soft.

## Tips

- Collect sand from Desert biomes for the most efficient harvesting. The surface is entirely sand.
- Glass is needed for the Tech Bench crafting station, so gather sand early and smelt it when you build your Furnace.
- Sand mines extremely quickly, so large quantities can be gathered in a short time.
