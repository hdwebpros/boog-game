**Brimstone** is a reddish, heat-radiating block found in the Hellfire Caverns zone of the Void Dimension. It replaces Void Stone in areas with intense volcanic activity.

## Where to Find

Brimstone is concentrated in the **Hellfire Caverns** biome zone within the Void Dimension. This zone is characterized by lava lakes and volcanic terrain, and Brimstone forms the walls and floors of these fiery caverns.

## Uses

- **Building**: A distinctive red-toned building material.
- **Terrain**: The signature block of the Hellfire Caverns zone.

## Tips

- Brimstone is a visual indicator that you are in the Hellfire Caverns zone, where Hellfire Imps spawn. This is where you farm Hellfire Cores.
- Like Void Stone, Brimstone requires a Titanium-tier or better pickaxe to mine.
- Watch for lava lakes when mining in areas with Brimstone. The Hellfire Caverns zone has frequent lava hazards.
- Brimstone's red color makes it a unique decorative block if you bring it back to the main world.
