**Obsidian** is a dense, dark block found near the core layer where lava meets stone. It is one of the hardest blocks to mine and is a key ingredient in crafting the Super Portal to access the Void Dimension.

## Where to Find

Obsidian forms naturally near the core layer (around depth 1320), typically at the edges of lava pools where lava contacts stone or other cooler blocks. It can also appear scattered in the deep underground.

## Uses

- **Super Portal** (99 Obsidian + 40 Arcane Dust + 30 Titanium Bars, crafted at Fusion Station)
- **Building**: The most durable building material in the main world.

## Tips

- You need a Diamond-tier or better pickaxe to mine obsidian. Lower-tier tools cannot break it.
- The Super Portal requires 99 obsidian, which is a substantial amount. Plan dedicated mining trips to the core layer.
- Obsidian mining is slow even with the right tools. Be patient and clear enemies from the area first.
- Bring plenty of blocks to bridge over lava while mining obsidian near lava pools. Falling in lava is a common hazard.
- Obsidian makes an excellent building material for fortified structures due to its high hardness.
