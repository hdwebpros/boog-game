**Stone** is the dominant block of the underground layers, forming the bulk of the terrain below the dirt layer. It is harder than dirt but still mineable with basic tools, and is a key crafting ingredient for Stone-tier equipment.

## Where to Find

Stone begins appearing a few blocks below the surface dirt layer and extends throughout the entire underground, deep underground, and core layers. It is the most common block in the game by volume.

## Uses

- **Stone Armor** (45 stone for the full set)
- **Stone weapons** (Stone Sword, etc.)
- **Workbench Mk2** crafting station
- **Furnace** crafting station
- **Building**: A sturdy building material, stronger than wood or dirt.

## Tips

- Stone is available as soon as you mine through the surface dirt layer, typically 5-10 blocks down.
- Prioritize collecting stone for a Furnace and Workbench Mk2, which unlock the next tier of crafting.
- Stone is the first block that takes noticeably longer to mine than dirt. A Wood Pickaxe speeds up the process.
- Keep a stockpile of stone for building. It is the most practical mid-tier building material.
