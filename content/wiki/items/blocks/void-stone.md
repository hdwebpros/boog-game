**Void Stone** is the dominant terrain block of the Void Dimension, serving the same role that stone plays in the main world. It is dark in color and significantly harder to mine than regular stone.

## Where to Find

Void Stone makes up the bulk of the terrain in the **Void Dimension** (3000x800 tiles). It appears in all four biome zones: Ashen Wastes, Hellfire Caverns, Void Forest, and Dark Citadel.

## Uses

- **Building**: A very durable dark-themed building material.
- **Terrain**: The foundation block of the Void Dimension.

## Tips

- You need at least a Titanium-tier pickaxe to mine Void Stone efficiently. Bring one before entering the Void Dimension.
- Void Stone is abundant in the Void Dimension but has no crafting uses. Its main value is as a building material.
- The enclosed nature of the Void Dimension (800 tiles tall with solid boundaries) means you will be mining through a lot of Void Stone to navigate between zones.
- The dark color of Void Stone makes it hard to see in the dim Void Dimension. Bring plenty of torches.
