**Cloud Block** is a unique block found exclusively in the Cloud City, a floating island high above the world near the horizontal center of the map. Cloud blocks are soft and mine quickly, but the Cloud City itself is more valuable intact as it houses the NPC shopkeeper.

## Where to Find

Cloud blocks form the structure of the **Cloud City**, a floating island located at approximately y=60 tiles, near the center of the map (around spawn x-center). The Cloud City is accessible once you have the jetpack or by building upward.

## Uses

- **Building**: A decorative white building material.
- **Cloud City structure**: Forms the foundation of the Cloud City where the NPC shopkeeper resides.

## Tips

- Do not mine the Cloud City floor. Falling through will send you plummeting to the surface, which can be fatal without the jetpack.
- Cloud blocks are mostly useful as a decorative building material. Their white color is unique and cannot be obtained elsewhere.
- The Cloud City contains the NPC shopkeeper who sells accessories for Silver Coins. Reaching it is a milestone in progression.
- You can reach the Cloud City by building a tall column of blocks upward, but the jetpack makes access much easier.
