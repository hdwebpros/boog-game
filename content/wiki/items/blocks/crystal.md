**Crystal** is a luminous block found in crystal caverns scattered throughout the underground and deep underground layers. Crystal caverns are distinctive open areas filled with glowing crystal formations and are often rich in diamond ore.

## Where to Find

Crystal blocks generate in **crystal caverns**, which are natural cave formations in the underground layer (below depth 180) and deep underground layer (below depth 640). These caverns are identifiable by their distinctive glow.

| Property | Value |

## Uses

- **Light source**: Crystal blocks emit a natural glow, making crystal caverns easy to spot.
- **Building**: An attractive decorative building material due to its glow.
- **Landmark**: Crystal caverns often contain nearby diamond ore deposits. Finding crystals is a sign you are in a good mining area.

## Tips

- Crystal caverns are the best places to search for diamond ore. When you find crystals, mine the surrounding walls thoroughly.
- An Iron pickaxe or better is needed to mine crystal blocks.
- Crystal blocks can be placed in your base as decorative light sources.
- The glow from crystal caverns can be spotted from a distance in dark underground tunnels, making them natural waypoints during exploration.
