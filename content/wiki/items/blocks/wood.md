**Wood** is the most essential early-game resource in Starfall, harvested from trees found across the surface. It is the primary ingredient in the Workbench, Wood Armor, Wood Sword, and many other fundamental recipes.

## Where to Find

Trees are present in most surface biomes, with Forest and Jungle biomes having the highest density. Mine tree trunk blocks to collect wood.

## Uses

- **Workbench** crafting station (the first station you build)
- **Wood Armor** (36 wood for the full set)
- **Wood weapons** (Wood Sword, Wood Bow, etc.)
- **Secondary ingredient** in Stone and Iron tier recipes
- **Building**: A reliable building material available from the start
- **Plant Fiber** (3 leaves from trees at a Workbench)

## Tips

- Wood is the first resource you should collect after crash-landing. Immediately mine nearby trees.
- Your first priority should be a Workbench, which only requires wood. This unlocks your first crafting recipes.
- Wood remains useful throughout the entire game as a secondary ingredient in higher-tier recipes. Iron Armor still requires 8 wood.
- Forest biomes are the best source for bulk wood gathering. Jungle biomes are also dense but may have more dangerous enemies.
