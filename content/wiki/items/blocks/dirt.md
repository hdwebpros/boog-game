**Dirt** is one of the most abundant blocks in Starfall, found in thick layers between the surface grass and underground stone. It is soft and mines quickly with any tool.

## Where to Find

Dirt forms several layers beneath the grass on the surface and extends into the upper portions of the underground layer. It is present in nearly every biome.

## Uses

- **Building**: A basic building block for quick shelters and bridges.
- **Filler**: Useful for plugging holes, creating paths, or blocking enemy movement during combat.

## Tips

- Dirt mines very quickly, making it ideal for rapid tunneling downward to reach stone and ore layers.
- Keep a stack of dirt in your hotbar. It is invaluable for bridging gaps, blocking lava flows, and creating temporary platforms.
- Dirt has no crafting value, but its abundance and fast mining speed make it the most practical utility block in the game.
