**Grass** is the most common surface block in Starfall, forming the top layer of Plains, Forest, and most other surface biomes. It is one of the softest blocks and can be mined almost instantly.

## Where to Find

Grass covers the surface layer (above depth 100) in most biomes. It is absent in Desert (replaced by Sand), Snow (replaced by snow blocks), and Lake (replaced by water) biomes.

## Uses

- **Dirt source**: Grass blocks drop as dirt when mined.
- **Building**: Can be used as a basic building material, though wood and stone are sturdier.
- **Landmark**: The grass layer indicates you are at or near the surface, useful for navigation when mining underground.

## Tips

- Grass is the first block you will encounter after crash-landing. Mine it to start digging downward.
- Beneath grass you will find dirt, and below that, stone. This layering is consistent across most biomes.
- Grass has no crafting uses, so do not bother stockpiling it unless you need it for building.
