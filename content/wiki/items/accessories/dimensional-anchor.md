The **Dimensional Anchor** is the most expensive and powerful void artifact. It provides knockback immunity, a massive damage bonus, and increasing armor. At Tier 3, you become "unstoppable," immune to crowd control effects.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **1,000 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Damage Bonus | Armor | Extra | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|-------------|-------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | +25% | -- | KB immune | 1,000 silver (buy) | 1,000 |
| 2 | +40% | +15 | KB immune | 500 silver | 1,500 |
| 3 | +60% | +30 | Unstoppable (CC immune) | 1,000 silver | 2,500 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Dimensional Anchor to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Dimensional Anchor into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- At Tier 3, +60% damage is the single largest damage boost from any equipment or accessory in the game. It transforms every weapon into a powerhouse.
- Knockback immunity at all tiers means enemies cannot interrupt your attacks or push you into hazards. This is critical for melee builds.
- The +30 armor at Tier 3 is equivalent to wearing an extra set of iron armor on top of your existing gear.
- "Unstoppable" at Tier 3 prevents all crowd control: stuns, slows, roots, and knockbacks. You become immune to enemy disruption.
- The 1,000-silver buy price and 2,500 total investment make this the most expensive item in the game. Farm with Lucky Charm and sell valuable materials to afford it.
- This is the single best artifact for the Void Lord fight. The damage bonus shortens the fight, the armor helps you survive, and CC immunity prevents the boss from locking you down.
- Pairs with Chaos Heart for an unkillable tank build: massive HP, thorns, armor, knockback immunity, and damage all in two accessory slots.
