The **Soul Lantern** is a void artifact that creates a lifesteal aura, healing you for a percentage of all damage you deal. Higher tiers increase the lifesteal rate, and Tier 3 can heal nearby allies in multiplayer.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **500 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Effect | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | 5% lifesteal | 500 silver (buy) | 500 |
| 2 | 10% lifesteal | 300 silver | 800 |
| 3 | 15% lifesteal + heals nearby allies | 600 silver | 1,400 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Soul Lantern to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Soul Lantern into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- Lifesteal scales with your damage output. The harder you hit, the more you heal. Pair with damage-boosting potions (Wrath, Rage) for maximum sustain.
- At Tier 3, the 15% lifesteal provides substantial healing during combat. A 100-damage hit heals 15 HP, and with fast weapons you can outheal many enemies.
- The ally healing at Tier 3 makes this essential for multiplayer support roles.
- Works with all weapon styles: melee, ranged, magic, and summon damage all trigger the lifesteal.
- Excellent for the Void Dimension where enemies are plentiful and you need sustained healing between encounters.
- Stacks well with Regeneration Potion for passive healing from two sources simultaneously.
