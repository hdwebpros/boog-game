The **Chaos Heart** is a void artifact that massively increases your maximum HP and deals thorns damage to attackers. It is the premier defensive artifact, turning you into a damage-absorbing tank.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **750 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Max HP | Thorns Damage | Extra | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|--------|---------------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | +100 | 10 | -- | 750 silver (buy) | 750 |
| 2 | +150 | 25 | -- | 400 silver | 1,150 |
| 3 | +200 | 50 | Ranged thorns | 800 silver | 1,950 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Chaos Heart to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Chaos Heart into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- At Tier 3, +200 max HP is a game-changing amount of extra health. Combined with Giant Potion (+25 HP) and Celestial Cape (+30 HP), your health pool becomes enormous.
- Tier 3 ranged thorns means enemies take damage when they hit you even from a distance, not just melee attackers.
- The thorns effect stacks with Thorns Potion (25% reflect) for devastating retaliation damage.
- Essential for the Void Lord fight, where the extra HP provides a crucial survival buffer during high-damage phases.
- Pairs naturally with Dimensional Anchor (+armor) for a full tank build, or with Soul Lantern (lifesteal) for a sustain-focused build.
- The 750-silver buy price makes it one of the more expensive artifacts. Farm Silver Coins with Lucky Charm equipped to save up faster.
