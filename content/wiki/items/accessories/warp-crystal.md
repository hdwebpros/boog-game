The **Warp Crystal** is a void artifact that grants a teleport dash ability. It can be upgraded through 3 tiers at the Sky Merchant, reducing the cooldown and eventually adding damage.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **500 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Effect | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Teleport dash, 5s cooldown | 500 silver (buy) | 500 |
| 2 | Teleport dash, 3.5s cooldown | 300 silver | 800 |
| 3 | Teleport dash, 2s cooldown + 30 damage at destination | 600 silver | 1,400 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Warp Crystal equipped or in your inventory to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Warp Crystal into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- The teleport dash is both offensive and defensive. Use it to escape danger or to close distance on ranged enemies.
- At Tier 3, the 30 damage on arrival turns every dash into an attack. Combined with the 2-second cooldown, you can deal consistent bonus damage during fights.
- Extremely useful in the Void Lord fight for dodging dimensional rifts and void beams.
- The teleport moves you in the direction you are facing. Practice the timing and direction in safe areas before relying on it in combat.
- Pairs well with Temporal Shard (CDR) to push the cooldown even lower, though the interaction depends on whether CDR applies to artifact abilities.
