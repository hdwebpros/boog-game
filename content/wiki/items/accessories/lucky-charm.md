The **Lucky Charm** gives a 25% chance for enemies to drop double loot and generally improves the quality of drops you receive. A passive income booster that pays for itself over time.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **150 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Double drop chance | +25% |
| Loot quality | Improved |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Lucky Charm into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. Effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- The 25% double drop chance applies to all enemy drops, including Silver Coins. This means the Lucky Charm effectively pays for itself after enough combat.
- Equip this whenever you are farming enemies for specific drops or grinding Silver Coins for other accessories.
- Keep it equipped during boss fights for a chance at bonus boss drops.
- The improved loot quality means rarer items appear more frequently in drop tables. This is especially valuable when farming Void Dimension enemies for void materials.
- Pair with the Celestial Cape's magnetic pickup to automatically collect all the extra drops.
