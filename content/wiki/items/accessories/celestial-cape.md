The **Celestial Cape** is a premium accessory that boosts both max HP and max mana by 30 while also adding a magnetic pickup effect that automatically collects dropped items within 80 pixels.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **300 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Max HP | +30 |
| Max mana | +30 |
| Item pickup radius | 80 pixels (magnetic) |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Celestial Cape into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. Effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- The most expensive base accessory at 300 silver, but arguably the most well-rounded. The stat boosts benefit every playstyle.
- The magnetic pickup is a massive quality-of-life improvement. Dropped items, coins, and materials fly toward you automatically. No more running over each individual drop.
- Pairs exceptionally well with Lucky Charm: more drops + automatic collection = effortless farming.
- The +30 HP and +30 mana are always useful, making this a safe choice for any of your 3 accessory slots.
- Consider this a high-priority purchase once you have Cloud Boots and one other accessory that suits your playstyle.
