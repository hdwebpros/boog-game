The **Miner's Lantern** boosts mining speed by 40% and extends your mining reach by 1 tile. A must-have for dedicated mining sessions and resource gathering.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **200 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Mining speed | +40% |
| Mining range | +1 tile |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Miner's Lantern into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. Effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- The +40% mining speed stacks with Mining Potion (+25%) for a combined 65% faster block breaking. Hard blocks like obsidian and titanium ore become much more manageable.
- The +1 mining range is subtle but powerful. You can reach blocks that were previously just out of reach, reducing the need to reposition.
- At 200 silver, it is one of the pricier base accessories. Prioritize it if you still need significant resource gathering (titanium, diamond, void materials).
- Makes the Night Owl Potion partially redundant thanks to the lantern's light, though the potion still provides fuller visibility.
- Swap this out for a combat accessory before boss fights. Mining speed does not help in combat.
