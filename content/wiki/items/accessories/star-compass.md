The **Star Compass** reveals nearby ores on your minimap and increases your maximum mana by 50. A dual-purpose accessory that benefits both miners and magic users.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **80 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Ore reveal | Shown on minimap |
| Max mana | +50 |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Star Compass into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. Effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- The ore reveal effect works permanently while equipped, unlike the Spelunker Potion which has a limited duration. However, the Spelunker Potion shows ores directly in the game world, while the Star Compass only shows them on the minimap.
- The +50 max mana is a significant boost for magic weapon users, especially when combined with Mana Surge Potion.
- A great second purchase after Cloud Boots. The ore reveal saves hours of blind digging.
- If you use primarily melee or ranged weapons, the mana bonus is less valuable. Consider Lucky Charm or Explorer's Belt instead.
