# Accessories & Artifacts

Accessories are equippable items that provide passive bonuses. You can equip up to **3 accessories** at a time in the accessory slots below your armor in the inventory panel. All accessories and artifacts are purchased from the **Sky Merchant NPC** in Cloud City.

## How to Access the Sky Merchant

1. Build or obtain a **jetpack** (or use Featherfall Potion + tower building).
2. Fly to **Cloud City**, the floating island near the world's center at y=60.
3. Find the NPC shop building on the island.
4. Press **F** near the Sky Merchant to open the shop UI.
5. Use the **buy tab** to purchase accessories with Silver Coins.

## Currency: Silver Coins

Silver Coins (ID 250) are the currency used at the Sky Merchant. You earn them by:

- Killing enemies (coin drops)
- Selling items at the shop (sell tab)

Silver Coins stack up to 999 per slot.

## Recommended Loadouts

### Early Game (3 slots)
- Cloud Boots + Star Compass + Lucky Charm

### Mid Game / Mining Focus
- Miner's Lantern + Star Compass + Explorer's Belt

### Boss Fighting
- Celestial Cape + Gravity Belt + Lucky Charm

### Void Dimension
- Dimensional Anchor + Chaos Heart + Soul Lantern

### True Final Boss (Void Lord)
- Dimensional Anchor + Chaos Heart + Temporal Shard
