The **Temporal Shard** is a void artifact that reduces cooldowns and increases attack speed. At Tier 3, it also slows nearby enemies, giving you a time-manipulation advantage in combat.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **600 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | CDR | Attack Speed | Extra | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|-----|-------------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | 15% | +10% | -- | 600 silver (buy) | 600 |
| 2 | 25% | +20% | -- | 350 silver | 950 |
| 3 | 35% | +30% | Slow nearby enemies | 700 silver | 1,650 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Temporal Shard to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Temporal Shard into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- Attack speed increases your DPS directly. At Tier 3, +30% attack speed is like having a weapon one full tier above your current one in terms of raw output.
- CDR (cooldown reduction) affects ability cooldowns, making this artifact synergize well with Warp Crystal's teleport dash.
- The Tier 3 enemy slow is a powerful defensive effect. Slower enemies are easier to dodge and kite, reducing incoming damage without needing armor.
- Excellent for fast-attacking melee weapons where the attack speed bonus translates to many extra hits per fight.
- One of the more affordable artifacts at 600 silver base. A good first artifact purchase if you want an offensive boost.
- The enemy slow effect is particularly valuable against the Void Lord, whose fast attacks in the enrage phase become more manageable.
