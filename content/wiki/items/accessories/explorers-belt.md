The **Explorer's Belt** adds 10 extra inventory slots as an additional row in your inventory panel. Invaluable for long exploration trips where inventory space is at a premium.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **200 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Inventory slots | +10 (extra row) |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Explorer's Belt into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. The extra inventory row appears immediately.

## Tips

- The extra 10 slots let you carry more materials, potions, and weapons on each trip. Fewer return visits to your base means faster progression.
- Particularly valuable during mining expeditions, where you accumulate many different ore and stone types quickly.
- If you frequently find yourself dropping items to make space, this accessory solves that problem permanently.
- Less useful in combat situations. Consider swapping to a combat-focused accessory before boss fights.
- Pairs well with Miner's Lantern for dedicated resource-gathering loadouts: mine faster and carry more home.
