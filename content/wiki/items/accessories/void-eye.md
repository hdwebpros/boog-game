The **Void Eye** is a void artifact that lets you see through walls and, at higher tiers, boosts your critical hit chance. It combines exploration utility with offensive power.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **750 Silver Coins** (Tier 1).

## Upgrade Tiers

| Tier | Wall Vision Range | Crit Chance | Extra | Upgrade Cost | Total Invested |
|------|-------------------|-------------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | 5 tiles | -- | -- | 750 silver (buy) | 750 |
| 2 | 8 tiles | +15% | -- | 400 silver | 1,150 |
| 3 | 12 tiles | +30% | Auto-mark enemies | 800 silver | 1,950 |

Return to the Sky Merchant with the Void Eye to upgrade it.

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Void Eye into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor.

## Tips

- Wall vision lets you see enemies, ores, and caverns through solid blocks. Invaluable for navigating the Void Dimension's enclosed corridors.
- The Tier 2 crit bonus (+15%) is a significant damage increase that applies to all weapon styles.
- At Tier 3, auto-marking enemies highlights hostile creatures through walls, giving you advance warning of ambushes.
- +30% crit chance at Tier 3 makes this one of the strongest offensive accessories in the game, rivaling Dimensional Anchor for damage output.
- Pairs well with high-damage weapons where crits are devastating, especially slow melee weapons with large base damage.
- The wall vision partially overlaps with Night Owl Potion and Miner's Lantern, but goes further by revealing what is behind walls, not just illuminating dark areas.
