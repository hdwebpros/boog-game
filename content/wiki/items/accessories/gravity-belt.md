The **Gravity Belt** dramatically improves jetpack performance by reducing fuel consumption by 40% and increasing flight speed by 20%. An essential accessory for extended aerial exploration and the endgame flight to escape the planet.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **120 Silver Coins**.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Jetpack fuel consumption | -40% |
| Flight speed | +20% |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place the Gravity Belt into one of the **3 accessory slots** below your armor. Effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- Only useful once you have assembled the jetpack from 6 boss component drops. Before that, this accessory does nothing.
- The 40% fuel reduction means you can fly roughly 67% longer on a single tank. Combined with ground recharging, you can stay airborne for most of your exploration.
- The +20% flight speed makes reaching Cloud City and high-altitude areas much faster.
- Extremely valuable in the Void Dimension, where flying over hazards is often safer than walking.
- Pair with Cloud Boots for the ultimate mobility loadout: fast flight, fast ground movement, and no fall damage on landing.
