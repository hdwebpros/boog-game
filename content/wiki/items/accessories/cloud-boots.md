**Cloud Boots** are an accessory that eliminates all fall damage and increases movement speed by 15%. They are the cheapest accessory at the Sky Merchant and one of the most impactful early purchases.

## How to Obtain

Purchase from the **Sky Merchant** NPC in Cloud City for **50 Silver Coins**.

1. Fly to Cloud City (floating island near spawn, y=60).
2. Find the NPC shop building.
3. Press **F** to open the shop.
4. Buy Cloud Boots from the accessories section.

## Effects

| Effect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Fall damage | Eliminated |
| Move speed | +15% |

## How to Equip

Open your inventory and place Cloud Boots into one of the **3 accessory slots** (visible below armor slots). The effects are passive and always active while equipped.

## Tips

- The single best quality-of-life accessory in the game. No fall damage means you can drop into any cave without worry.
- The +15% move speed stacks with Swiftness Potion (+30%) for a total of +45% movement speed.
- At only 50 silver, this should be your first accessory purchase. You likely have enough coins from early enemy kills.
- Makes the Featherfall Potion completely redundant, freeing up a hotbar slot and crafting resources.
- Essential for navigating the Mountains biome and any vertical cave systems.
