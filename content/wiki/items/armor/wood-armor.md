**Wood Armor** is the first armor set available in Starfall. It provides a modest 5 total defense, but even this small amount of damage reduction makes a meaningful difference in the early game when enemy hits deal 5-15 damage.

## Armor Pieces

| Piece | Item ID | Defense | Recipe |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| Wood Helmet | 200 | 1 | 8 Wood |
| Wood Chestplate | 201 | 2 | 12 Wood |
| Wood Leggings | 202 | 1 | 10 Wood |
| Wood Boots | 203 | 1 | 6 Wood |
| **Total** | | **5** | **36 Wood** |

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at a **Workbench**. The full set requires 36 wood, which can be gathered by mining trees on the surface. Wood is abundant in Forest and Jungle biomes.

## Tips

- Craft this set as soon as you build your Workbench. Wood is plentiful and the defense helps survive early exploration.
- A full set of Wood Armor combined with a Wood Sword makes surface exploration much safer.
- Upgrade to Stone Armor once you have access to a Workbench Mk2 and sufficient stone.
- Equip armor by opening your inventory panel and placing each piece into its corresponding armor slot (helmet, chest, legs, boots).
