**Titanium Armor** is a tier 4 armor set providing 27 total defense. As the strongest armor available before entering the Void Dimension, it requires both titanium bars and carbon plates, making it a true end-game investment.

## Armor Pieces

| Piece | Item ID | Defense | Recipe |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| Titanium Helmet | 216 | 6 | 6 Titanium Bar, 2 Carbon Plate |
| Titanium Chestplate | 217 | 10 | 10 Titanium Bar, 4 Carbon Plate |
| Titanium Leggings | 218 | 7 | 8 Titanium Bar, 3 Carbon Plate |
| Titanium Boots | 219 | 4 | 5 Titanium Bar, 2 Carbon Plate |
| **Total** | | **27** | **29 Titanium Bar, 11 Carbon Plate** |

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at a **Fusion Station**. Titanium Bars are smelted at a Tech Bench from 4 titanium ore each, and Carbon Plates are processed at a Fusion Station from 4 carbon fiber blocks each. Titanium ore is found in the deep underground and core layers.

## Tips

- Titanium ore is most common near and within the core layer (below depth 1320). Bring lava protection.
- Carbon fiber blocks are also found deep underground. You will need 44 carbon fiber blocks for the 11 Carbon Plates required.
- This armor set is the best you can have for the main game bosses. Equip the full set before attempting the final jetpack component boss fights.
- After completing the jetpack, consider crafting the Super Portal to access the Void Dimension, where Void Armor with dramatically higher defense awaits.
