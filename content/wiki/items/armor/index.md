# Armor

Armor in Starfall reduces incoming damage from enemies and environmental hazards. Each armor set consists of four pieces: **Helmet**, **Chestplate**, **Leggings**, and **Boots**. Equip armor through the inventory panel by placing pieces into the corresponding armor slots.

## How Armor Works

- Each armor piece provides a **defense** value.
- Total defense is subtracted from incoming damage (minimum 1 damage per hit).
- Higher-tier armor requires more advanced crafting stations and rarer materials.
- Only one piece can be equipped per slot at a time.

## Progression Tips

- Start with **Wood Armor** immediately after building your Workbench. Even 5 defense makes a noticeable difference against early enemies.
- **Iron Armor** is a major upgrade and should be prioritized once you reach the underground layer and find iron ore.
- **Diamond Armor** requires deep underground exploration but the jump from 13 to 19 defense is significant for boss fights.
- **Void Armor** is post-game content with dramatically higher defense, designed for surviving the Void Dimension and the Void Lord fight.
