**Iron Armor** is a tier 2 armor set that provides 13 total defense. It represents a major milestone in player progression, nearly doubling the protection of Stone Armor. Crafted at an Anvil using iron bars, this set is the first armor that requires smelted materials.

## Armor Pieces

| Iron Helmet | 208 | 3 | 6 Iron Bar, 2 Wood |
| Iron Chestplate | 209 | 5 | 10 Iron Bar, 3 Wood |
| Iron Leggings | 210 | 3 | 8 Iron Bar, 2 Wood |
| Iron Boots | 211 | 2 | 5 Iron Bar, 1 Wood |
| **Total** | | **13** | **29 Iron Bar, 8 Wood** |

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at an **Anvil**. Each Iron Bar is smelted at a Furnace from 3 iron ore, so the full set requires **87 iron ore** total. Iron ore is found in the underground layer (below depth 180).

## Tips

- Iron ore starts appearing in the underground layer. Bring torches and dig deep to find veins.
- Prioritize the Chestplate first since it gives the biggest single-piece defense boost (5 defense).
- Iron Armor is strong enough to comfortably handle most surface and upper underground enemies, and is recommended before fighting your first boss.
- You need an Anvil to craft this set, which itself requires iron bars. Plan your iron mining trips to gather enough for both the Anvil and the armor.
