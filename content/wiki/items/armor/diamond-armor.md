**Diamond Armor** is a tier 3 armor set providing 19 total defense. Crafted at a Tech Bench using diamonds and iron bars, this is the point where players become durable enough to take on the tougher bosses and explore the deep underground with confidence.

## Armor Pieces

| Piece | Item ID | Defense | Recipe |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| Diamond Helmet | 212 | 4 | 5 Diamond, 2 Iron Bar |
| Diamond Chestplate | 213 | 7 | 8 Diamond, 3 Iron Bar |
| Diamond Leggings | 214 | 5 | 6 Diamond, 2 Iron Bar |
| Diamond Boots | 215 | 3 | 4 Diamond, 1 Iron Bar |
| **Total** | | **19** | **23 Diamond, 8 Iron Bar** |

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at a **Tech Bench**. Each Diamond is smelted at a Furnace from 3 diamond ore. The full set requires **69 diamond ore** and 8 Iron Bars (24 iron ore). Diamond ore is found in the deep underground layer (below depth 640).

## Tips

- Diamond ore is rare and found deep underground. Crystal caverns are good locations to search.
- The Chestplate alone gives 7 defense, making it the single highest-value piece at this tier. Craft it first.
- Diamond Armor is strongly recommended before tackling mid-to-late game bosses that drop jetpack components.
- Keep your Iron Armor as a backup. If you die, you do not want to lose progress hunting for more diamond ore.
