**Stone Armor** is a tier 1 armor set that provides 8 total defense, a solid upgrade from the 5 defense of Wood Armor. It requires a Workbench Mk2 to craft, making it the second set most players will obtain.

## Armor Pieces

| Piece | Item ID | Defense | Recipe |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| Stone Helmet | 204 | 2 | 10 Stone, 3 Wood |
| Stone Chestplate | 205 | 3 | 15 Stone, 4 Wood |
| Stone Leggings | 206 | 2 | 12 Stone, 3 Wood |
| Stone Boots | 207 | 1 | 8 Stone, 2 Wood |
| **Total** | | **8** | **45 Stone, 12 Wood** |

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at a **Workbench Mk2**. Stone is mined from stone blocks found just below the surface layer. The wood component is gathered from trees.

## Tips

- Stone is easy to find once you dig below the grass and dirt layers. Start mining downward near your base.
- The jump from 5 to 8 defense may seem small, but it meaningfully reduces damage from surface and shallow underground enemies.
- This set is a good stepping stone while you search for iron ore in the underground layer.
- If you are struggling with early bosses, make sure you have at least Stone Armor equipped before attempting a summon.
