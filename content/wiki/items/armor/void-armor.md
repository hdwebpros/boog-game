**Void Armor** is the most powerful armor set in Starfall, providing a massive 82 total defense. Crafted at a Void Forge using materials found exclusively in the Void Dimension, this set is designed for surviving the extreme dangers of post-game content including the Void Lord true final boss.

## How to Obtain

Craft all four pieces at a **Void Forge**, which is only available after entering the Void Dimension via the Super Portal.

### Material Sources

- **Abyssal Ingot**: Crafted at the Void Forge from 5 Void Essence + 3 Hellfire Core. The full set needs 29 ingots (145 Void Essence, 87 Hellfire Cores).
- **Soul Fragment**: Dropped by Soul Eaters and other void enemies. You need 17 total.
- **Dimensional Fabric**: Crafted at the Void Forge from 3 Chaos Shard + 2 Abyssal Ingot. The 2 fabric requires 6 Chaos Shards and 4 more Abyssal Ingots.

## Tips

- The jump from Titanium (27 defense) to Void (82 defense) is enormous. This set trivializes most regular enemies.
- Prioritize the Chestplate first for its 28 defense, the highest single-piece defense value in the game.
- Soul Fragments drop from Soul Eaters, which are common in the Ashen Wastes and Void Forest biome zones.
- Dimensional Fabric is the rarest component. Chaos Shards only drop from Chaos Elementals and Dark Knights in the Dark Citadel zone.
- This armor is essential for the Void Lord fight, which deals extremely high damage in its later phases.
- After returning from the Void Dimension, the original world has a 20x enemy spawn rate, making this armor valuable even outside the void.
