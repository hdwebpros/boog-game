**Barbed Armor** is a unique armor set in Starfall that reflects a portion of incoming damage back to attackers. While its base defense is low, the thorns effect makes it a powerful choice for melee-heavy combat situations.

## How It Works

Each piece of Barbed Armor has a **thorns percentage**. When an enemy hits you, a portion of the damage dealt is reflected back to the attacker. With the full set equipped, 25% of all melee damage taken is reflected.

The thorns effect stacks with the Thorns Potion buff for even higher damage reflection.

## How to Obtain

Barbed Armor pieces are loot drops from jungle enemies. Farm **Vine Stranglers** and **Jungle Spiders** in the Jungle biome for a chance to obtain pieces.

## Tips

- The full set only provides 4 defense, so pair it with strong weapons and healing items.
- Barbed Armor is especially effective against swarm enemies that attack frequently.
- Consider wearing it against bosses with rapid melee attacks to add free passive damage.
- Stacks with the Thorns Potion for up to 50%+ damage reflection.
