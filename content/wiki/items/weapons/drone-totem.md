The **Drone Totem** is the first summon weapon available to players. Using it costs 20 mana and summons a drone minion that automatically attacks nearby enemies, dealing 10 damage per hit. The drone fights independently, letting you focus on mining, exploring, or attacking with a different weapon.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 8        |
| Glass    | 4        |

| Stat         | Value  |

| Damage       | 10     |

| Tier         | 2      |

## Tips

- Summon your drone before entering dangerous areas. It will automatically engage enemies, giving you extra DPS without any effort.
- The 20 mana cost is a one-time expenditure. Once summoned, the drone persists and attacks on its own.
- Pair the Drone Totem with a melee or ranged weapon for a hybrid playstyle where you deal damage while your minion adds chip damage.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to summon a drone minion. The drone costs 20 mana to summon and will automatically target and attack nearby enemies. The minion attacks every 1000ms, dealing 10 damage per hit.
