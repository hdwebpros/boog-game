The **Void Blade** is a tier 5 melee weapon forged at the Void Forge using materials from the Void Dimension. With 85 damage and a swift 280ms swing speed, it drastically outperforms the Titanium Sword. The blade crackles with void energy, making it effective against the powerful enemies found in the Void Dimension and the post-void overworld.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material       | Quantity |
|----------------|----------|
| Abyssal Ingot  | 8        |
| Soul Fragment  | 10       |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 85     |
| Attack Speed | 280ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 5      |

## Tips

- The Void Blade is the entry-level void melee weapon. It swings faster than the Abyssal Scythe but deals less damage per hit.
- Soul Fragments drop from void enemies, so fight your way through the Void Dimension to collect enough.
- If you prefer speed over raw power, the Void Blade is the better choice over the Abyssal Scythe.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the blade. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
