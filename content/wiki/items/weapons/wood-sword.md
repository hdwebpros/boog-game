The **Wood Sword** is the first melee weapon a player can craft after landing on the planet. While its damage output is modest at 8 per swing, it provides a critical early-game advantage over punching enemies bare-handed. As a tier 0 weapon, it is quickly outclassed by the Stone Sword once stone becomes available.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Wood     | 7        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 8      |
| Attack Speed | 400ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 0      |

## Tips

- Craft this as soon as you place your first Workbench. Fighting enemies without a weapon is dangerous.
- The Wood Sword is sufficient for handling early surface enemies like Space Slugs and Crystal Beetles.
- Upgrade to a Stone Sword as soon as you can mine stone to significantly boost your damage.

## Controls

Press **LMB** (left mouse button) to swing the sword. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. You can hold LMB for continuous attacks.
