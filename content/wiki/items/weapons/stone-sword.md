The **Stone Sword** is a tier 1 melee weapon that nearly doubles the damage of the Wood Sword while also swinging slightly faster. It requires a Workbench Mk2 to craft, making it the natural next step once you have established a base and begun mining stone.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench Mk2**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Stone    | 10       |
| Wood     | 3        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 14     |
| Attack Speed | 380ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 1      |

## Tips

- The Stone Sword is effective against most surface-level enemies and early underground threats.
- At 380ms cooldown it swings faster than the Wood Sword, giving you better DPS overall.
- Start collecting iron as soon as possible to unlock the Iron Sword at the Anvil.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the sword. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous swinging.
