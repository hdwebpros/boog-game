The **Wood Bow** is the earliest ranged weapon available to players. While its 6 damage per shot is lower than the Wood Sword, the ability to attack from a distance makes it invaluable against enemies with dangerous melee attacks. The 600ms cooldown between shots is relatively slow, so aim carefully.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Wood        | 6        |
| Plant Fiber | 3        |

## Tips

- The Wood Bow is excellent for kiting enemies that are too dangerous to fight up close early on.
- Projectiles travel in a straight line at 400 speed, so lead your shots against moving targets.
- Pair this with a melee weapon in your hotbar so you can switch between ranged and close combat.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to fire a projectile in the direction of your cursor. The arrow travels forward at 400 speed. Hold LMB for continuous firing.
