The **Arcane Annihilator** is the ultimate magic weapon in Starfall. Dealing 130 damage per cast with a 270ms cooldown, it delivers the highest magic DPS in the game. The 35 mana cost per cast is heavy, but the sheer destructive output makes it worth every point of mana spent.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material           | Quantity |
|--------------------|----------|
| Dimensional Fabric | 5        |
| Chaos Shard        | 15       |
| Soul Fragment      | 20       |

## Tips

- At 35 mana per cast, mana management is critical. Equip every mana-boosting accessory you can to sustain your casting.
- The Arcane Annihilator offers the best single-target DPS of any magic weapon, making it ideal for boss fights.
- Consider alternating between this and a melee weapon to conserve mana for critical moments during the Void Lord fight.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to cast an annihilation bolt toward the cursor. Each cast consumes 35 mana. Hold LMB for continuous casting (mana permitting).
