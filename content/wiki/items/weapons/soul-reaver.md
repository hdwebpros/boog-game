The **Soul Reaver** is a tier 5 summon weapon that tears souls from the void to create powerful spectral minions. Each minion deals 60 damage per attack, more than double the Swarm Beacon's output. The 40 mana cost is steep, but the resulting minions are formidable fighters against void enemies.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material           | Quantity |
|--------------------|----------|
| Soul Fragment      | 20       |
| Dimensional Fabric | 3        |

## Stats

| Damage       | 60     |

| Weapon Style | Summon |
| Tier         | 5      |

## Tips

- The Soul Reaver's minions are powerful enough to handle most void enemies on their own, freeing you to mine or explore.
- Dimensional Fabric is one of the rarest void materials. Prioritize collecting it from the Dark Citadel biome.
- At 40 mana per summon, make sure you have a large enough mana pool before using this weapon.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to summon spectral minions. The summon costs 40 mana. Minions automatically target and attack nearby enemies, dealing 60 damage every 800ms each.
