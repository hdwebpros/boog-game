The **Iron Bow** is a tier 2 ranged weapon that triples the damage of the Wood Bow while also firing faster projectiles. The increased projectile speed of 500 makes it much easier to hit moving targets. This bow bridges the gap between early-game ranged combat and the powerful Laser Gun.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Iron Bar    | 4        |
| Wood        | 5        |
| Plant Fiber | 4        |

| Stat             | Value  |

| Damage           | 18     |
| Attack Speed     | 500ms  |
| Projectile Speed | 500    |
| Weapon Style     | Ranged |
| Tier             | 2      |

## Tips

- The Iron Bow is a reliable mid-game ranged option, dealing consistent damage from a safe distance.
- The faster projectile speed compared to the Wood Bow makes hitting enemies much more forgiving.
- Consider alternating between this and a melee weapon depending on the enemy you are facing.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to fire a projectile toward the cursor. The arrow travels at 500 speed. Hold LMB for continuous firing.
