The **Swarm Beacon** is a tier 4 summon weapon that calls multiple minions to fight for you. Each minion deals 28 damage at an 800ms attack interval, providing substantial passive DPS. The swarm overwhelms enemies with numbers while you focus on dodging or dealing additional damage with other weapons.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Titanium Bar | 6        |
| Carbon Plate | 4        |

| Stat         | Value  |

| Damage       | 28     |
| Attack Speed | 800ms  |
| Mana Cost    | 30     |
| Weapon Style | Summon |
| Tier         | 4      |

## Tips

- The Swarm Beacon summons multiple minions, making it far more powerful than the Drone Totem in total DPS output.
- At 30 mana per use, make sure you have a healthy mana pool before summoning. The investment pays off quickly.
- This is one of the best weapons for boss fights since your minions deal damage continuously while you focus entirely on avoiding attacks.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to summon multiple minions. The summon costs 30 mana. Minions automatically target and attack nearby enemies, dealing 28 damage every 800ms each.
