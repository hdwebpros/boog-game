The **Laser Gun** is the pinnacle of pre-void ranged weaponry. With 45 damage, a blazing 300ms fire rate, and 700 projectile speed, it shreds enemies from a distance with ease. The laser projectiles are nearly impossible to dodge for enemies, making this one of the most effective weapons in the base game.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Titanium Bar | 5        |
| Carbon Plate | 3        |
| Diamond      | 3        |

## Tips

- The Laser Gun has the fastest projectile speed of any base-game ranged weapon, making it nearly impossible to miss.
- With 300ms between shots, you can maintain constant pressure on bosses while staying at a safe distance.
- The material cost is steep, so make sure you have explored the Deep Underground thoroughly before attempting to craft this.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to fire a laser projectile toward the cursor. The laser travels at 700 speed. Hold LMB for rapid continuous fire.
