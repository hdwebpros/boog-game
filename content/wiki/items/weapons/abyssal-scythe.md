The **Abyssal Scythe** is a tier 5 melee weapon that trades attack speed for raw power. At 110 damage per swing, it hits harder than any other tier 5 weapon, though its 450ms cooldown means you must time your strikes carefully. The wide scythe arc makes it especially effective against groups of void enemies.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material       | Quantity |
|----------------|----------|
| Abyssal Ingot  | 12       |
| Chaos Shard    | 5        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 110    |
| Attack Speed | 450ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 5      |

## Tips

- The Abyssal Scythe excels in situations where you can land hits safely. Its high damage per swing punishes enemies hard.
- Chaos Shards are rarer than Soul Fragments, so you may craft the Void Blade first and upgrade to this later.
- Compare this to the Void Blade: Scythe deals 110 damage at 450ms, Blade deals 85 at 280ms. Choose based on your combat style.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the scythe. The wide swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
