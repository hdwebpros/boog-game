The **Hellfire Bow** is a tier 5 ranged weapon that fires blazing projectiles forged from hellfire energy. With 70 damage and an extremely fast 250ms fire rate, it delivers outstanding ranged DPS. The bow is crafted from materials found in the Hellfire Caverns biome within the Void Dimension.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Hellfire Core | 10       |
| Abyssal Ingot | 6        |

## Stats

| Stat             | Value  |
|------------------|--------|
| Damage           | 70     |
| Attack Speed     | 250ms  |
| Projectile Speed | 500    |
| Weapon Style     | Ranged |
| Tier             | 5      |

## Tips

- The 250ms fire rate makes this the fastest-firing ranged weapon before tier 6, allowing you to pour on damage from safety.
- Hellfire Cores drop from hellfire imps and are found in the Hellfire Caverns biome of the Void Dimension.
- This bow is excellent for the Void Lord fight, where keeping your distance is often the safest strategy.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to fire a hellfire projectile toward the cursor. The projectile travels at 500 speed. Hold LMB for rapid continuous fire.
