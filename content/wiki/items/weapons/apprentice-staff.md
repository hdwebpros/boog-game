The **Apprentice Staff** is the first magic weapon available to players. It fires arcane projectiles that cost 8 mana per cast. While its damage is moderate at 12 per hit, magic weapons offer a unique playstyle that combines the safety of ranged attacks with the versatility of mana management. Mana regenerates over time, so plan your shots carefully.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Iron Bar    | 5        |
| Diamond Ore | 2        |

## Tips

- Keep an eye on your mana bar. Running out of mana mid-fight leaves you unable to cast until it regenerates.
- The Apprentice Staff pairs well with a melee weapon for when your mana runs low.
- At 8 mana per shot, you can fire many times before depleting your mana pool, making it efficient for sustained combat.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to cast a magic projectile toward the cursor. Each cast consumes 8 mana. The projectile travels at 350 speed. Hold LMB for continuous casting (mana permitting).
