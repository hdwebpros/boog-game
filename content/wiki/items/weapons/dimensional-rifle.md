The **Dimensional Rifle** is the ultimate ranged weapon in Starfall. Firing dimensional energy bolts at 120 damage with an incredible 200ms fire rate and 600 projectile speed, it delivers devastating ranged DPS. This weapon tears through void enemies and is one of the best choices for the Void Lord fight.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material           | Quantity |
|--------------------|----------|
| Dimensional Fabric | 5        |
| Hellfire Core      | 15       |
| Abyssal Ingot      | 10       |

## Stats

| Stat             | Value  |
|------------------|--------|
| Damage           | 120    |
| Attack Speed     | 200ms  |
| Projectile Speed | 600    |
| Weapon Style     | Ranged |
| Tier             | 6      |

## Tips

- The 200ms fire rate is the fastest of any weapon in the game, allowing you to unleash a constant barrage of dimensional bolts.
- At 600 projectile speed, the bolts are fast enough to hit almost any enemy reliably, even teleporting chaos elementals.
- This is arguably the safest weapon for the Void Lord fight since you can maintain maximum distance while still dealing massive damage.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to fire a dimensional bolt toward the cursor. The bolt travels at 600 speed. Hold LMB for rapid continuous fire.
