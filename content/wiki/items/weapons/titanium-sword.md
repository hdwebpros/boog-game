The **Titanium Sword** is the strongest melee weapon available before entering the Void Dimension. At 50 damage with a rapid 300ms attack speed, it offers the best melee DPS in the standard world. Crafted at the Fusion Station, it requires late-game materials including titanium bars and carbon plates.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Titanium Bar | 6        |
| Carbon Plate | 2        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 50     |
| Attack Speed | 300ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 4      |

## Tips

- This is the best melee weapon for tackling the final bosses and collecting jetpack components.
- The 300ms attack speed means you can get hits in during very tight windows between boss attack patterns.
- Once you have access to the Void Dimension, the Void Blade and Abyssal Scythe outclass this weapon.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the sword. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
