# Weapons

Starfall features four distinct weapon styles, each offering a unique combat experience. Weapons are organized into tiers from 0 (basic) to 6 (void-tier), with higher tiers dealing more damage and attacking faster.

All weapons are used with **LMB** (left mouse button) to attack. Use **RMB** to place blocks.

Melee weapons swing in an arc in front of the player, hitting all enemies within range. They require no ammunition or mana.

## Weapon Progression

The recommended weapon progression follows the material tiers:

1. **Tier 0** -- Wood weapons from the Workbench
2. **Tier 1** -- Stone and basic magic from Workbench Mk2 and Anvil
3. **Tier 2** -- Iron weapons and first summon from the Anvil
4. **Tier 3** -- Diamond and crystal weapons from the Tech Bench
5. **Tier 4** -- Titanium and advanced weapons from the Fusion Station
6. **Tier 5** -- Void weapons from the Void Forge (requires Void Dimension access)
7. **Tier 6** -- Ultimate weapons from the Void Forge (requires dimensional fabric)
