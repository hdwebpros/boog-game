The **Diamond Sword** is a tier 3 melee weapon crafted at the Tech Bench. With 35 damage and a swift 320ms attack speed, it delivers formidable DPS that makes it effective against deep underground enemies and mid-tier bosses. The diamond edge provides both durability and sharpness.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Tech Bench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Diamond  | 5        |
| Iron Bar | 2        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 35     |
| Attack Speed | 320ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 3      |

## Tips

- The Diamond Sword is strong enough to take on most bosses if you have good dodging skills.
- Diamonds are found in the Deep Underground layer, so bring a good pickaxe and plenty of torches.
- Pair this with the Crystal Staff for a versatile melee/magic loadout.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the sword. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
