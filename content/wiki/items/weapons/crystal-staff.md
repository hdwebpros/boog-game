The **Crystal Staff** is a tier 3 magic weapon that fires powerful crystal bolts at 32 damage per hit. With faster attack speed and higher projectile speed than the Apprentice Staff, it delivers a major upgrade in magic DPS. However, the 15 mana cost per cast means you will burn through mana quickly during extended fights.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Tech Bench**:

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Diamond      | 8        |
| Titanium Bar | 2        |

## Tips

- The Crystal Staff is mana-hungry at 15 per cast. Consider equipping mana-boosting accessories to sustain longer fights.
- Its 450 projectile speed is fast enough to reliably hit most enemies, including agile underground types.
- This staff is strong enough for boss fights, but always carry a backup melee weapon in case you run dry on mana.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to cast a crystal bolt toward the cursor. Each cast consumes 15 mana. The bolt travels at 450 speed. Hold LMB for continuous casting (mana permitting).
