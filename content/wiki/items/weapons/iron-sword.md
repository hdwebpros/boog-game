The **Iron Sword** is a tier 2 melee weapon forged at an Anvil. With 22 damage and a 350ms swing speed, it represents a significant power spike over stone-tier equipment. This weapon is well-suited for exploring the underground layers and taking on tougher enemies.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 6        |
| Wood     | 3        |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 22     |
| Attack Speed | 350ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 2      |

## Tips

- The Iron Sword is the workhorse of mid-game combat. Keep one on hand even after unlocking ranged or magic weapons.
- This tier unlocks alongside the Iron Bow and Drone Totem, so consider which weapon style suits your playstyle.
- Iron bars require smelting iron ore, so make sure you have a furnace set up.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the sword. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
