The **Void Staff** is a tier 5 magic weapon that channels raw void energy into devastating bolts. At 95 damage per cast, it is the most powerful magic weapon before tier 6. The 25 mana cost per cast is significant, so mana management becomes critical during extended fights.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Chaos Shard   | 8        |
| Soul Fragment | 15       |

## Stats

| Stat             | Value  |
|------------------|--------|
| Damage           | 95     |
| Attack Speed     | 320ms  |
| Mana Cost        | 25     |
| Weapon Style     | Magic  |
| Tier             | 5      |

## Tips

- At 25 mana per cast, you will drain mana quickly. Equip mana-boosting accessories or keep a melee backup weapon ready.
- The Void Staff deals the highest damage per hit of any tier 5 weapon when you factor in its attack speed.
- Chaos Shards are found in the more dangerous parts of the Void Dimension, so be prepared for tough fights while gathering materials.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to cast a void bolt toward the cursor. Each cast consumes 25 mana. Hold LMB for continuous casting (mana permitting).
