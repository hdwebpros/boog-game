The **Chaos Edge** is the most powerful melee weapon in Starfall. At 150 damage with a blistering 250ms attack speed, it delivers unmatched melee DPS. Forged from dimensional fabric and chaos shards, this sword represents the pinnacle of void-tier craftsmanship and is designed to challenge the Void Lord himself.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material           | Quantity |
|--------------------|----------|
| Dimensional Fabric | 5        |
| Chaos Shard        | 10       |
| Abyssal Ingot      | 15       |

## Stats

| Stat         | Value  |
|--------------|--------|
| Damage       | 150    |
| Attack Speed | 250ms  |
| Weapon Style | Melee  |
| Tier         | 6      |

## Tips

- The Chaos Edge is the strongest melee weapon in the game. If you have the materials, there is no reason not to craft it.
- Its 250ms attack speed combined with 150 damage gives it the highest melee DPS by a wide margin.
- This weapon trivializes most enemies in the overworld and is essential for the Void Lord fight if you prefer melee combat.

## Controls

Press **LMB** to swing the blade. The swing arc hits enemies in front of the player. Hold LMB for continuous attacks.
