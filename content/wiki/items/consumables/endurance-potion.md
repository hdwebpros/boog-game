The **Endurance Potion** reduces all incoming damage by 15% for 45 seconds. Unlike Ironskin Potion (flat defense), this is a percentage reduction that scales with enemy damage.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Frost Crystal | 2        |
| Obsidian      | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Endurance Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. All incoming damage is reduced by 15% for 45 seconds.

## Tips

- Percentage reduction becomes more valuable against harder-hitting enemies. Against a 100-damage boss attack, this saves 15 HP per hit.
- Stacks with Ironskin Potion for maximum damage reduction: flat defense applies first, then the 15% reduction on the remainder.
- Frost crystals are found in cold underground areas and the Snow biome. Obsidian is near the core layer.
- One of the best defensive potions for the Void Lord fight, where incoming damage is extremely high and flat defense has diminishing returns.
- The 45-second duration is shorter than Ironskin (60s), so time it carefully during boss fights.
