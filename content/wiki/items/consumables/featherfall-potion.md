The **Featherfall Potion** eliminates all fall damage for 90 seconds. Perfect for diving into deep caves or descending from Cloud City without worrying about landing.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Cloud Block | 5        |
| Leaves      | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Featherfall Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. You take no fall damage for 90 seconds, regardless of height.

## Tips

- Drink one before dropping into a deep underground shaft. It saves a lot of HP and avoids an embarrassing death.
- Cloud blocks are found at Cloud City (floating island at y=60). Mine some while you are up there shopping.
- Once you obtain Cloud Boots (which permanently negate fall damage), this potion becomes redundant. It is a good budget option before you can afford them.
- Useful in the Mountains biome where the terrain has sharp elevation changes.
