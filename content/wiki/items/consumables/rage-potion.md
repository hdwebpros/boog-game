The **Rage Potion** boosts all melee weapon damage by 15% for 45 seconds. A strong offensive buff for sword and melee weapon users.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Ember Crystal | 2        |
| Iron Bar      | 2        |

## How to Use

1. Place Rage Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. All melee weapon damage increases by 15% for 45 seconds.

## Tips

- The shorter 45-second duration means timing matters. Drink right before engaging a boss, not while exploring.
- Stacks with Wrath Potion (+25% ALL damage) for a combined melee damage boost that melts bosses.
- Ember crystals are found in hot underground areas, often near lava. Bring extra when you go mining there.
- Melee weapons put you in close range, so always pair Rage Potion with a defensive buff like Ironskin or Endurance Potion.
