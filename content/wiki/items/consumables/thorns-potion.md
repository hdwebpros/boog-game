The **Thorns Potion** reflects 25% of all damage you receive back to the attacker for 45 seconds. This turns incoming hits into a source of passive damage.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Cactus   | 5        |
| Iron Bar | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Thorns Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. For 45 seconds, enemies that hit you take 25% of the damage they dealt reflected back at them.

## Tips

- Most effective against enemies that attack frequently with smaller hits, since each hit triggers the reflection.
- Pairs naturally with high-defense builds. The less damage you take, the longer you survive while still dealing reflected damage.
- Stacks with the Chaos Heart artifact's thorns effect for even more reflected damage.
- Less useful against bosses with slow, powerful attacks. Consider swapping for an offensive potion in those situations.
- Cactus is easy to farm in the Desert biome, making this a cheap potion to keep stocked.
