The **Water Walking Potion** lets you walk across water surfaces as if they were solid ground for 120 seconds. Useful for crossing lakes and ocean areas without swimming.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Coral    | 5        |
| Ice      | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Water Walking Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. You can walk on water surfaces for 120 seconds.

## Tips

- Essential for exploring the Lake biome and ocean areas efficiently.
- The long 120-second duration gives plenty of time to cross even large bodies of water.
- Coral is found in ocean and lake areas. Ice is found in the Snow biome. Plan a gathering trip that hits both biomes.
- Useful for accessing areas that are otherwise difficult to reach without the jetpack.
