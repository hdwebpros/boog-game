The **Healing Herb** is the most basic healing consumable in Starfall. It restores 25 HP instantly when used, making it an essential early-game survival tool. Healing Herbs stack up to 30, so you can carry a generous supply without eating too many inventory slots.

## How to Obtain

### Crafting

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Leaves      | 5        |
| Plant Fiber | 2        |

### Enemy Drops

Many enemies across all biomes have a chance to drop Healing Herbs, making them a reliable pickup during exploration.

## How to Use

1. Place Healing Herbs in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot so Healing Herbs are your active item.
3. Press **I** to consume one, instantly restoring 25 HP.

## Tips

- Craft a full stack of 30 before your first boss fight. Even though 25 HP per use is modest, rapid use between attacks adds up.
- Healing Herbs are your only healing option before you unlock the Brewing Stand. Stock up early.
- Since they drop from many enemies, you will accumulate these passively during exploration. Keep a stack on your hotbar at all times.
- Later in the game, Cooked Meat (50 HP) and potions like Giant Potion or Regeneration Potion outclass Healing Herbs, but they remain useful as a cheap supplemental heal.
