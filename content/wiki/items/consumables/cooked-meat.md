**Cooked Meat** is a healing consumable that restores 50 HP instantly. It cannot be crafted and is only obtained as an enemy drop, making it a valuable find during exploration.

## How to Obtain

Dropped by the following enemies:

| Enemy | Biome |
|-------|-------|
| Frost Wolf | Snow |
| Sand Scorpion | Desert |
| Lava Serpent | Core / Deep Underground |
| Mountain Hawk | Mountains |

## How to Use

1. Place Cooked Meat in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot so it is your active item.
3. Press **I** to consume one, instantly restoring 50 HP.

## Tips

- Cooked Meat heals double what a Healing Herb does, so prioritize using Healing Herbs first and save Cooked Meat for emergencies.
- Farm Frost Wolves in the Snow biome or Sand Scorpions in the Desert for a steady supply. These enemies respawn frequently.
- With a stack size of 20, Cooked Meat provides up to 1000 total HP in a single inventory slot, making it excellent for extended boss fights.
- Pair with Regeneration Potion for both burst and sustained healing during tough encounters.
