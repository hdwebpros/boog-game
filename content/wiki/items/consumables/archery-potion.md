The **Archery Potion** boosts all ranged weapon damage by 20% for 60 seconds. A must-have for bow and gun users heading into boss fights.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material     | Quantity |
|--------------|----------|
| Cactus       | 3        |
| Jungle Grass | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Archery Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. All ranged weapon damage increases by 20% for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- The ranged equivalent of Rage Potion (melee) and Magic Power Potion (magic). Choose the one that matches your primary weapon style.
- Ranged weapons already benefit from safe distance. Adding 20% damage on top makes boss fights significantly faster.
- Cactus and jungle grass are both surface materials, making this potion easy to farm once you locate the Desert and Jungle biomes.
- For maximum ranged damage, combine with Wrath Potion (+25% ALL damage). The bonuses stack for devastating output.
