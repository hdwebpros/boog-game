The **Forcefield Potion** grants 8 seconds of complete damage immunity. Unlike most potions, it is crafted at an **Anvil** rather than a Brewing Stand. Its short but powerful effect makes it a lifesaver during boss fights and dangerous situations.

## How to Obtain

Craft at an **Anvil**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 3        |
| Glass    | 2        |
| Leaves   | 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place Forcefield Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot so it is your active item.
3. Press **I** to activate. You become immune to all damage for 8 seconds.

## Tips

- Save these for boss fights. Pop a Forcefield Potion during a boss's most dangerous phase or when your HP is critically low and you need time to heal.
- The 8-second window is enough to use several Healing Herbs or reposition safely.
- Particularly effective against the Void Lord's enrage phase, where damage output is extremely high.
- With a stack size of only 10, bring extras if you expect a long fight. Craft a batch before each major boss encounter.
- Pairs well with Regeneration Potion: activate the forcefield, and your regen ticks heal you without risk of taking damage.
