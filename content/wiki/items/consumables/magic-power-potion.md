The **Magic Power Potion** increases all magic weapon damage by 20% for 60 seconds. The premier offensive buff for magic weapon users.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Storm Crystal | 2        |
| Crystal       | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Magic Power Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. All magic weapon damage increases by 20% for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- Pair with Mana Surge Potion (+50% mana regen) for a full magic combat kit: more damage and more mana to fuel it.
- Storm crystals can be tricky to find. Look in the deep underground layer during exploration.
- The 60-second duration gives more uptime than Rage Potion (45s), making magic builds slightly more efficient over long fights.
- Stacks with Wrath Potion (+25% ALL damage) for devastating magic output. Use both for the final phase of tough bosses.
