The **Mana Surge Potion** boosts your mana regeneration rate by 50% for 60 seconds. Essential for magic weapon users who burn through mana quickly during combat.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material       | Quantity |
|----------------|----------|
| Crystal        | 2        |
| Mushroom Block | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Mana Surge Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Your mana regeneration rate increases by 50% for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- Critical for magic weapon builds. Magic weapons consume mana per attack, and running dry mid-fight leaves you vulnerable.
- Pair with Magic Power Potion (+20% magic damage) for a full magic combat loadout.
- The Star Compass accessory (+50 max mana) synergizes well, giving you a larger mana pool to work with alongside faster regen.
- Crystals are found in underground crystal caverns. They are easy to spot due to their distinctive appearance.
