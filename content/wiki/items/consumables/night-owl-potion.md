The **Night Owl Potion** lets you see clearly in dark areas for 120 seconds. It is especially useful when exploring underground layers before you obtain a Miner's Lantern accessory.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material       | Quantity |
|----------------|----------|
| Mushroom Block | 3        |
| Plant Fiber    | 2        |

## How to Use

1. Place Night Owl Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Dark areas become fully visible for 120 seconds.

## Tips

- Essential for early underground exploration. Without this potion or a Miner's Lantern, navigating caves is extremely difficult.
- The 120-second duration is generous. One potion is usually enough for a full cave expedition.
- Mushroom blocks are found in the Mushroom biome on the surface and in underground mushroom patches.
- Once you buy the Miner's Lantern accessory from the Sky Merchant, this potion becomes less necessary, but it remains a budget alternative if you want to use a different accessory slot.
