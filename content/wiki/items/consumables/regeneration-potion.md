The **Regeneration Potion** heals you for 2 HP every second over 60 seconds, for a total of 120 HP restored. Unlike instant heals, this provides steady recovery that works while you fight.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Life Crystal | 2        |
| Plant Fiber  | 3        |

## How to Use

1. Place Regeneration Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. You regenerate 2 HP per second for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- The total 120 HP healed over 60 seconds makes this one of the best healing options in the game, but it requires you to survive long enough to benefit.
- Pair with defensive potions (Ironskin, Endurance) so the regen can outpace incoming damage.
- Works during Forcefield Potion immunity, letting you heal safely during the 8-second window.
- Life Crystals are found in the underground and deep underground layers. Mine extras specifically for potion crafting.
- Drink at the start of a boss fight rather than waiting until you are low. The sooner you activate it, the more total healing you receive.
