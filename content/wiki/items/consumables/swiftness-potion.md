The **Swiftness Potion** increases your movement speed by 30% for 60 seconds. It is one of the easiest potions to craft, requiring only basic desert and surface materials.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material    | Quantity |
|-------------|----------|
| Cactus      | 3        |
| Plant Fiber | 2        |

## How to Use

1. Place Swiftness Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Your movement speed is boosted by 30% for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- A great general-purpose buff for exploration. The speed boost helps you cover ground faster when searching for biomes or ores.
- Useful in boss fights for dodging projectile attacks and repositioning.
- Cactus is abundant in the Desert biome. Harvest a large supply early to keep Swiftness Potions stocked.
- Stacks well with Cloud Boots (+15% move speed accessory) for a total of +45% movement speed.
- One of the cheapest potions to mass-produce. Keep a stack handy at all times.
