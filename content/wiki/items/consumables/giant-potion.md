The **Giant Potion** temporarily increases your maximum HP by 25 for 60 seconds. The extra health is added immediately, effectively healing you while also raising your ceiling.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material       | Quantity |
|----------------|----------|
| Life Crystal   | 3        |
| Mushroom Block | 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place Giant Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Your maximum HP increases by 25 for 60 seconds. Current HP also increases by 25.

## Tips

- Effectively a heal and a buff in one. Drink when at full HP to enter a fight with an HP advantage.
- The +25 max HP stacks with the Chaos Heart artifact (+100/150/200 HP) for a massive health pool.
- Life Crystals are the bottleneck ingredient. Farm them during deep underground exploration trips.
- Useful as a safety buffer before boss summons. The extra 25 HP can be the difference between surviving a big hit and going down.
