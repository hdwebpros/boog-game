The **Mining Potion** increases your mining speed by 25% for 90 seconds, letting you break blocks faster during excavation runs.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Ore | 3        |
| Moss     | 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place Mining Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. All blocks break 25% faster for 90 seconds.

## Tips

- Combine with a Spelunker Potion for the ultimate mining run: see all ores, then break them faster.
- The Miner's Lantern accessory (+40% mining speed) stacks with this potion for extremely fast block breaking.
- Moss is found on underground stone surfaces. Iron ore is abundant in the underground layer.
- Particularly valuable when mining hard blocks like obsidian, diamond ore, or titanium ore, where every percentage of speed boost saves noticeable time.
- The 90-second duration is generous enough for a focused mining session. Drink one when you find a rich vein.
