The **Spelunker Potion** highlights all nearby ores through solid walls for 90 seconds. This makes mining expeditions far more efficient by revealing exactly where to dig.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Ember Crystal | 1        |
| Frost Crystal | 1        |
| Glass         | 2        |

## How to Use

1. Place Spelunker Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Ores in your vicinity glow through walls for 90 seconds, visible as highlighted blocks.

## Tips

- The best way to find rare ores like diamond and titanium. Without this potion, you may walk past valuable deposits hidden behind a single layer of stone.
- The 90-second duration gives you plenty of time to scan an area. Move through caves systematically while the effect is active.
- Requires both ember and frost crystals, which are found in different underground areas. Stock up on both during exploration trips.
- Pairs perfectly with Mining Potion (+25% mining speed) for maximum efficiency during dedicated mining runs.
- The Star Compass accessory provides a similar ore-revealing effect permanently via the minimap, but the Spelunker Potion shows ores directly in the world view, which some players find more useful.
