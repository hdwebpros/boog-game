# Consumables & Potions

Consumables are single-use items that restore health or grant temporary buffs. Select a consumable on your hotbar and press **I** to use it. Potions grant timed buffs that appear as icons near your health bar.

## How to Use

1. Place the consumable in your **hotbar** (number keys 1-9).
2. Select it so it is the active item.
3. Press **I** to consume it.
4. Buffs are active for their listed duration. You cannot stack the same buff.

## Boss Fight Loadout

For tough boss encounters, consider bringing:

- **Ironskin Potion** and **Endurance Potion** for survivability
- **Rage/Magic Power/Archery Potion** matching your weapon style
- **Regeneration Potion** for sustained healing
- **Healing Herbs** for burst healing between phases
- **Wrath Potion** for the final push (short duration, save for low HP phases)
