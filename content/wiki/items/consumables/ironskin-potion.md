The **Ironskin Potion** boosts your defense by 10 for 60 seconds. Defense directly reduces incoming damage, making this one of the most universally useful potions for any combat situation.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Bar | 2        |
| Stone    | 5        |

## How to Use

1. Place Ironskin Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. Your defense increases by 10 for 60 seconds.

## Tips

- A staple for boss fights. +10 defense is equivalent to upgrading from Wood Armor to Iron Armor temporarily.
- Combine with Endurance Potion (-15% damage taken) for maximum damage reduction. The two effects stack multiplicatively for excellent survivability.
- Iron bars and stone are easy to come by, making this one of the most cost-effective tier 1 potions to mass-produce.
- Always drink one before summoning a boss. The 60-second duration covers most of a boss fight, and you can drink another if the fight runs long.
