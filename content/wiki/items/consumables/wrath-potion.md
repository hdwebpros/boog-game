The **Wrath Potion** is the most powerful offensive potion in Starfall, boosting ALL damage output by 25% for 30 seconds. It works with every weapon style: melee, ranged, magic, and summon. The short duration and expensive recipe make it a strategic burst tool.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Brewing Stand** (produces 3):

| Material      | Quantity |
|---------------|----------|
| Void Crystal  | 2        |
| Ember Crystal | 2        |
| Diamond       | 1        |

## How to Use

1. Place Wrath Potions in your **hotbar**.
2. Select the slot and press **I** to drink.
3. ALL weapon damage increases by 25% for 30 seconds.

## Tips

- The 30-second duration is the shortest of any potion. Do not waste it on exploration; save it exclusively for boss fights.
- Pop it when a boss enters a vulnerable phase or when its HP is low enough to finish off quickly.
- Stacks with weapon-specific potions (Rage, Archery, Magic Power) for massive combined damage bonuses. A Rage + Wrath combo gives +15% + 25% melee damage.
- Void crystals are found in the Void Dimension, making this a post-game potion. You will not have access to it for the base 6 bosses.
- Diamond is a rare deep underground ore. Bring a Spelunker Potion to find diamond veins more easily.
- Excellent for speed-killing the Void Lord during its brief vulnerability windows. Coordinate with Forcefield Potion for a safe damage burst.
