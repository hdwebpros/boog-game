The **Soul Fragment** is a tier 5 material dropped primarily by Soul Eaters in the Void Dimension. These ethereal shards are used directly in Void Armor recipes alongside Abyssal Ingots.

## How to Obtain

Dropped by **Soul Eaters** and other void enemies. Soul Eaters are found throughout the Void Dimension, particularly in the Ashen Wastes and Void Forest zones.

## Used In

- **Void Helmet** (5 Soul Fragment)
- **Void Leggings** (8 Soul Fragment)
- **Void Boots** (4 Soul Fragment)
- Total for Void Armor set: **17 Soul Fragment**

## Tips

- Soul Eaters are the primary source of Soul Fragments. They appear in multiple zones, making them fairly common encounters.
- You need 17 Soul Fragments for the full Void Armor set (excluding the Chestplate, which uses Dimensional Fabric instead).
- Soul Eaters can be dangerous in groups. Engage them one at a time when possible.
- Soul Fragments are used directly in armor recipes rather than being processed into ingots first, so keep them in your inventory.
