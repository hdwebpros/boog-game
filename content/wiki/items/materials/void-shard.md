The **Void Shard** is a tier 3 enchanting material mined from Void Crystal blocks in the deep underground. It is rarer than the elemental shards and is used in advanced enchanting recipes.

## How to Obtain

Mine **Void Crystal blocks** found in the deep underground layer (below depth 640). These blocks emit a dark purple glow and are the rarest of the crystal types.

## Used In

- Advanced enchantments
- High-tier enchanting recipes requiring void energy

## Tips

- Void Crystal blocks are significantly rarer than Ember, Frost, or Storm crystals. Mark their locations if you plan to return for more.
- These are found primarily in the deep underground, so come prepared with good equipment and lighting.
- Do not confuse Void Shards with Void Essence, which is a separate tier 5 material from the Void Dimension. Void Shards are found in the main world.
