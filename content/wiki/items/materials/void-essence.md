**Void Essence** is the basic void-tier material, dropped by nearly all enemies in the Void Dimension. It serves as the foundation for crafting Abyssal Ingots, which are used in all void equipment recipes.

## How to Obtain

Dropped by **void enemies** in the Void Dimension. Most void enemy types have a chance to drop Void Essence, including Void Wraiths, Shadow Stalkers, and others.

## Used In

- **Abyssal Ingot** -- crafted at Void Forge (5 Void Essence + 3 Hellfire Core per ingot)

## Tips

- Void Essence is the most common drop in the Void Dimension. You will accumulate large amounts through normal exploration and combat.
- A full set of Void Armor requires approximately 145 Void Essence (through Abyssal Ingot crafting), so do not discard these.
- The Ashen Wastes biome zone has high enemy density and is a good farming location for Void Essence.
- Combined with Hellfire Cores at a Void Forge, Void Essence becomes Abyssal Ingots, the key metal for all void equipment.
