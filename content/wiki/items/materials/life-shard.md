The **Life Shard** is a tier 1 enchanting material mined from Life Crystal blocks found underground. It glows with restorative energy and is used in healing and life-related enchanting recipes.

## How to Obtain

Mine **Life Crystal blocks** found in the underground layer (below depth 180). These blocks have a bright green or pink glow.

## Used In

- Healing enchantments
- Life-related enchanting recipes

## Tips

- Life Crystal blocks start appearing in the upper parts of the underground layer, making them one of the earliest shards you can find.
- As a tier 1 material, Life Shards are relatively common compared to higher-tier shards.
- Healing enchantments are useful for extended exploration and boss fights, so collect these early and often.
