**Glass** is a basic material smelted from sand at a Furnace. Despite being tier 0, it is used in several important recipes including the Tech Bench crafting station and various potion recipes.

## How to Obtain

Smelt at a **Furnace**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Sand | 4 |

Sand is found abundantly in Desert biomes and along lakeshores. It can be mined with any pickaxe.

## Used In

- **Tech Bench** crafting station
- **Potion recipes**
- Various utility items

## Tips

- Desert biomes are the easiest source of large amounts of sand. Look for the sandy-colored surface areas.
- Smelt a good stock of glass early on, as the Tech Bench is a critical crafting station needed for diamond-tier equipment.
- Sand is also found underwater in Lake biomes, but Desert biomes are easier to harvest from.
