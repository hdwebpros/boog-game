**Dimensional Fabric** is the rarest crafting material in Starfall, crafted at the Void Forge from Chaos Shards and Abyssal Ingots. It is used exclusively in the Void Chestplate, the single most protective armor piece in the game.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Chaos Shard | 3 |
| Abyssal Ingot | 2 |

## Used In

- **Void Chestplate** (2 Dimensional Fabric required)

## Tips

- You only need 2 Dimensional Fabric for the Void Chestplate, but gathering the materials is the hardest part of making Void Armor.
- Each fabric requires 3 Chaos Shards (from Chaos Elementals and Dark Knights in the Dark Citadel) and 2 Abyssal Ingots.
- Total raw materials for 2 fabric: 6 Chaos Shards + 4 Abyssal Ingots (20 Void Essence + 12 Hellfire Cores).
- The Void Chestplate provides 28 defense on its own, making the effort to craft Dimensional Fabric well worth it.
- This is the final bottleneck in completing the Void Armor set. Plan a focused expedition to the Dark Citadel zone to farm Chaos Shards.
