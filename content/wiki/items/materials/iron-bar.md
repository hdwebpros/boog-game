The **Iron Bar** is one of the most important materials in Starfall. Smelted from iron ore at a Furnace, it is used in a wide range of mid-tier recipes including Iron Armor, Iron weapons, and the Anvil crafting station itself.

## How to Obtain

Smelt at a **Furnace**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Iron Ore | 3 |

Iron ore is found in the underground layer (below depth 180). Look for metallic-colored blocks embedded in stone. Iron veins are relatively common and appear throughout the entire underground.

## Used In

- **Iron Armor** (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots) -- 29 bars total for the full set
- **Iron weapons** (Sword, Bow, Staff, etc.)
- **Anvil** crafting station
- **Diamond Armor** recipes (as a secondary material)
- Various other mid-tier recipes

## Tips

- Iron ore becomes available as soon as you dig past the surface layer. Bring plenty of torches.
- Smelt iron ore in bulk. You will need dozens of Iron Bars for armor, weapons, and the Anvil.
- The Anvil requires Iron Bars to craft and unlocks Iron-tier equipment recipes, so prioritize building one early.
- Iron Bars remain useful into late game as a secondary ingredient in Diamond Armor recipes.
