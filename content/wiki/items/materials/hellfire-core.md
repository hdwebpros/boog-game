The **Hellfire Core** is a tier 5 material dropped by Hellfire Imps in the Void Dimension. It burns with an intense internal flame and is a key ingredient in crafting Abyssal Ingots.

## How to Obtain

Dropped by **Hellfire Imps** in the Void Dimension. Hellfire Imps are most common in the Hellfire Caverns biome zone.

## Used In

- **Abyssal Ingot** -- crafted at Void Forge (5 Void Essence + 3 Hellfire Core per ingot)

## Tips

- Hellfire Imps are concentrated in the Hellfire Caverns zone of the Void Dimension. Head there for focused farming.
- You need 3 Hellfire Cores per Abyssal Ingot, making this one of the most in-demand void materials. A full Void Armor set requires approximately 87 Hellfire Cores.
- Hellfire Imps are ranged attackers that throw fire projectiles. Approach with good armor and dodge their attacks.
- Hellfire Cores are more targeted to farm than Void Essence since they only drop from one enemy type. Plan dedicated farming runs in the Hellfire Caverns.
