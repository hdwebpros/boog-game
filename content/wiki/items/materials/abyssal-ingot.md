The **Abyssal Ingot** is a tier 5 metal crafted at the Void Forge from Void Essence and Hellfire Cores. It is the primary metal used in all void-tier weapons and armor, making it the most important crafted material in the Void Dimension.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Void Forge**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Void Essence | 5 |
| Hellfire Core | 3 |

## Used In

- **Void Armor** (all 4 pieces) -- 29 Abyssal Ingots for the full set
- **Dimensional Fabric** (2 Abyssal Ingot per craft)
- **Void weapons** (Sword, Bow, Staff, etc.)
- Various void-tier equipment

## Tips

- The full Void Armor set requires 29 Abyssal Ingots directly, plus 4 more for the 2 Dimensional Fabric needed for the Chestplate. That is 33 ingots total (165 Void Essence + 99 Hellfire Cores).
- Batch your crafting. Farm large quantities of Void Essence and Hellfire Cores before returning to the Void Forge.
- Abyssal Ingots serve the same role in the Void Dimension that Iron Bars serve in the main world: the essential refined metal for everything.
- Prioritize crafting enough ingots for the Void Chestplate first, as it provides the highest single-piece defense (28).
