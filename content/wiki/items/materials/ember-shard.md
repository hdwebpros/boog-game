The **Ember Shard** is a tier 2 enchanting material mined directly from Ember Crystal blocks found underground. It radiates heat and is used in fire-themed enchanting recipes.

## How to Obtain

Mine **Ember Crystal blocks** found in the underground and deep underground layers. These blocks have a distinctive warm glow and are often found near lava pockets.

## Used In

- Fire enchantments for weapons
- Various enchanting recipes

## Tips

- Ember Crystal blocks are most common near the boundary between underground and deep underground layers.
- Look for warm-colored glowing blocks in cave systems. They tend to cluster near lava.
- Collect shards whenever you see them, as enchanting recipes can require several at a time.
