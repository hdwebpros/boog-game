**Diamond** is a tier 2 material smelted from diamond ore at a Furnace. It is the primary ingredient in Diamond Armor and Diamond-tier weapons, representing a significant power spike in the mid-to-late game.

## How to Obtain

Smelt at a **Furnace**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Diamond Ore | 3 |

Diamond ore is found in the deep underground layer (below depth 640). It is rarer than iron ore and often appears near crystal caverns.

## Used In

- **Diamond Armor** (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots) -- 23 diamonds for the full set
- **Diamond weapons** (Sword, Bow, Staff, etc.)
- **Tech Bench** crafting station
- Various tier 3 recipes

## Tips

- Crystal caverns in the deep underground are the best locations to find diamond ore. The glowing crystals nearby make them easier to spot.
- Each diamond requires 3 ore, so plan on collecting at least 69 diamond ore for a full armor set.
- Diamond gear is a major power upgrade and is recommended before tackling the later bosses.
- Always carry a return strategy when mining deep for diamonds. The core layer below is filled with dangerous enemies and lava.
