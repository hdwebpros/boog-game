**Arcane Dust** is a tier 1 material obtained from enemy drops and found scattered in the underground. It is a versatile enchanting ingredient and a key component in crafting the Super Portal.

## How to Obtain

- **Enemy drops**: Many enemies have a chance to drop Arcane Dust on defeat.
- **Underground deposits**: Small amounts can be found embedded in underground cave walls.

## Used In

- Various enchanting recipes
- **Super Portal** (requires 40 Arcane Dust alongside 99 Obsidian and 30 Titanium Bars)

## Tips

- Arcane Dust accumulates naturally as you fight enemies during exploration. Do not sell it.
- The Super Portal requires 40 Arcane Dust, which is a significant amount. Start saving early if you plan to access the Void Dimension.
- Since it drops from many enemy types, farming any enemy-dense area will help you stockpile this material.
- Underground cave exploration often yields Arcane Dust alongside other valuable ores, making it efficient to collect passively.
