**Plant Fiber** is a basic tier 0 material crafted from leaves at a Workbench. It is used in early-game recipes for basic tools and equipment.

## How to Obtain

Craft at a **Workbench**:

| Material | Quantity | Produces |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Leaves | 3 | 2 Plant Fiber |

Leaves are obtained by mining tree canopy blocks in Forest, Jungle, and Plains biomes.

## Used In

- Early-game tool and equipment recipes
- Basic rope and binding materials

## Tips

- Forest and Jungle biomes have dense tree coverage, making them the best sources for leaves.
- The recipe produces 2 fibers per craft, so 3 leaves gives you 2 plant fibers. Gather leaves in bulk to be efficient.
- Plant Fiber is only needed for early-game items. Once you progress to iron tier and beyond, you will not need more.
