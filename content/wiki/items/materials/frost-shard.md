The **Frost Shard** is a tier 2 enchanting material mined from Frost Crystal blocks found underground. It pulses with cold energy and is used in ice-themed enchanting recipes.

## How to Obtain

Mine **Frost Crystal blocks** found in the underground and deep underground layers. These blocks have a cool blue glow and are often found in clusters near Snow biome underground sections.

## Used In

- Ice enchantments for weapons
- Various enchanting recipes

## Tips

- Frost Crystal blocks are more common beneath Snow biomes, but can appear anywhere in the underground.
- The blue glow makes them easy to spot in dark cave systems.
- Stock up on Frost Shards alongside Ember Shards, as some advanced recipes require multiple shard types.
