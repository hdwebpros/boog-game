**Titanium Bar** is a tier 3 material smelted from titanium ore at a Tech Bench. It is required for crafting Titanium Armor and end-game weapons, making it one of the most valuable metals in the game.

## How to Obtain

Smelt at a **Tech Bench**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Titanium Ore | 4 |

Titanium ore is found in the deep underground and core layers. It is significantly rarer than iron or diamond ore.

## Used In

- **Titanium Armor** (Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots) -- 29 bars for the full set
- **Titanium weapons**
- **Fusion Station** crafting station
- **Super Portal** (30 Titanium Bars required)
- Various tier 4 recipes

## Tips

- Titanium ore is most commonly found near the core layer boundary (around depth 1320). Be prepared for lava and dangerous enemies.
- Each bar requires 4 ore, so the full armor set needs 116 titanium ore. Plan multiple mining expeditions.
- Building a Fusion Station requires Titanium Bars, and the Fusion Station unlocks Carbon Plate processing, so getting titanium is a gateway to the final pre-void tier of equipment.
- Titanium Bars are also needed for the Super Portal to access the Void Dimension (30 bars), so stockpile extra.
