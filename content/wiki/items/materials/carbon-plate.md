**Carbon Plate** is a tier 4 material processed from carbon fiber blocks at a Fusion Station. It is the highest-tier crafting material available in the main game, used alongside titanium bars to create end-game equipment.

## How to Obtain

Process at a **Fusion Station**:

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Carbon Fiber Block | 4 |

Carbon fiber blocks are found in the deep underground and core layers, often near titanium ore deposits.

## Used In

- **Titanium Armor** (as a secondary material) -- 11 plates for the full set
- **End-game weapons and tools**
- Various tier 4 recipes

## Tips

- You need a Fusion Station to process Carbon Plates, which itself requires titanium bars to build. This material is late in the progression chain.
- The full Titanium Armor set requires 11 Carbon Plates, meaning you need 44 carbon fiber blocks total.
- Carbon fiber blocks are found in the same depth range as titanium ore. Mine both resources during the same expeditions to save time.
- Carbon Plates are not used in Void Dimension recipes, which have their own material chain.
