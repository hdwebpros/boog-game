The **Storm Shard** is a tier 2 enchanting material mined from Storm Crystal blocks found underground. It crackles with electrical energy and is used in lightning-themed enchanting recipes.

## How to Obtain

Mine **Storm Crystal blocks** found in the underground and deep underground layers. These blocks have a flickering yellow-white glow.

## Used In

- Lightning enchantments for weapons
- Various enchanting recipes

## Tips

- Storm Crystal blocks can appear throughout the underground but are somewhat rarer than Ember and Frost varieties.
- Their flickering glow distinguishes them from other crystal types.
- Lightning enchantments tend to be powerful, so Storm Shards are worth collecting whenever you find them.
