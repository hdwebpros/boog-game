The **Chaos Shard** is a rare tier 5 material dropped by Chaos Elementals and Dark Knights in the Void Dimension. It is the rarest enemy-dropped void material and is required to craft Dimensional Fabric.

## How to Obtain

Dropped by **Chaos Elementals** and **Dark Knights** in the Void Dimension. Both enemy types are found primarily in the Dark Citadel biome zone.

## Used In

- **Dimensional Fabric** -- crafted at Void Forge (3 Chaos Shard + 2 Abyssal Ingot per fabric)

## Tips

- The Dark Citadel is the most dangerous zone in the Void Dimension. Come fully prepared with the best available armor and weapons.
- Chaos Elementals and Dark Knights are tough enemies. Expect challenging fights when farming for these shards.
- You need at least 6 Chaos Shards for the 2 Dimensional Fabric required by the Void Chestplate.
- Since Chaos Shards only drop from two enemy types in one zone, farming can be slow. Be patient and methodical.
