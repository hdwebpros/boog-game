The Soul Eater is a swift flying predator of the Void Dimension that swoops at players from above. With 150 HP, a speed of 160, and a swooping attack pattern, it is the void's answer to the Cave Bat -- but exponentially more dangerous. It dives relentlessly, giving players little time to recover between attacks.

## Behavior

The Soul Eater uses a **swoop** AI pattern. It flies at high speed above the player and dives repeatedly, dealing contact damage on each pass. At speed 160, its dives are extremely fast and difficult to dodge in the void's often cramped environments. It circles quickly between dives, maintaining constant pressure.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Soul Eaters are moderately common throughout all four biome zones of the Void Dimension.

## Strategy

The Soul Eater's high speed makes it a difficult target. Time your attacks for the moment it reaches the bottom of its dive arc, where it briefly slows before pulling back up. Wide-arc melee weapons or area-effect magic spells are most effective for catching it during the dive. Ranged weapons can work during the circling phase if you can lead your shots. At 150 HP, the Soul Eater takes several hits to bring down, so consistency is more important than burst damage. The high Soul Fragment drop rate (70% for 4 fragments) makes Soul Eaters the best source of this crafting material.

## Special Traits

- **Best Soul Fragment Source:** With a 70% chance to drop 4 Soul Fragments, the Soul Eater is by far the most efficient way to farm this void crafting material.
