The Chaos Elemental is a supremely dangerous ranged attacker found in the Void Dimension. With 250 HP, 55 damage, and a speed of 150, it combines high durability with devastating ranged attacks and extreme mobility. It is arguably the most frustrating enemy to fight in the void due to its ability to deal massive damage while staying out of melee range.

## Behavior

The Chaos Elemental uses a **ranged** AI pattern. It flies at high speed, maintaining distance from the player while bombarding them with chaos projectiles. At speed 150, it easily outpaces players and kites relentlessly. Each projectile deals 55 damage, which can kill poorly-armored players in just two or three hits. The elemental aggressively repositions to maintain its preferred attack range.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Chaos Elementals are moderately rare but appear in all four void biome zones. Encountering one alongside other void enemies creates an extremely dangerous situation.

## Strategy

The Chaos Elemental is the most dangerous ranged enemy in the game. Use terrain as cover to block its projectiles while closing the distance or returning fire. Ranged weapons are the most practical option since you cannot outrun it. If you can corner it in a tight space, melee becomes viable, but in open areas you will be kited indefinitely. Magic weapons with tracking projectiles are highly effective. Prioritize killing it before other enemies in mixed encounters, as its ranged damage output is unsustainable. The Chaos Shard and Dimensional Fabric drops are both valuable void crafting materials.

## Special Traits

- **Highest Ranged Damage:** At 55 damage per projectile, the Chaos Elemental deals the most ranged damage of any regular enemy in the game.
- **Dimensional Fabric Source:** One of only a few enemies that can drop the rare Dimensional Fabric crafting material (8% chance).
