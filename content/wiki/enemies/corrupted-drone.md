The Corrupted Drone is a ranged enemy found exclusively in the core layer of the world. These damaged machines fly erratically and fire projectiles at players from a distance, making them a persistent nuisance in the deepest parts of the world. Their combination of flight, speed, and ranged attacks makes them one of the most annoying enemies to deal with in tight core tunnels.

## Behavior

The Corrupted Drone uses a **ranged** AI pattern. It flies through the air, maintaining distance from the player while periodically firing projectiles. The drone attempts to keep a gap between itself and the player, retreating if approached and shooting if the player stays in range. Its projectiles travel in a straight line toward the player's position at the time of firing.

## Location

- **Layers:** Core
- **Biomes:** All (core layer)
- **Spawn conditions:** Any time (core has no day/night cycle)

Corrupted Drones are found only in the core layer, the deepest part of the overworld. They are relatively common in this zone and often appear alongside Rock Golems and other core enemies.

## Strategy

Closing the distance is key. The Corrupted Drone wants to maintain range, so aggressive melee rushes can catch it off guard. Dodge its projectiles by moving perpendicular to the line of fire rather than trying to outrun them. Ranged weapons work well for trading fire, but melee is often faster if you can corner the drone against a wall. In open caverns, try to use terrain as cover to block its shots while you close in. The Carbon Fiber and Titanium Bar drops make these enemies worth farming at the core layer.

## Special Traits

- **Projectile Attack:** The Corrupted Drone fires projectiles from range, one of the few overworld enemies with a ranged attack.
