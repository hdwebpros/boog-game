The Shadow Stalker is one of the most dangerous ambush predators in the Void Dimension. With 200 HP, a devastating 50 damage per hit, and 50% knockback resistance, it is a top-tier threat that can kill unprepared players in just a few hits. It hides in the shadows of the void, striking without warning and pursuing relentlessly once revealed.

## Behavior

The Shadow Stalker uses an **ambush** AI pattern. It remains hidden in the dark corners of the Void Dimension, invisible to the player until they wander within trigger range. Once activated, it reveals itself and attacks aggressively, pursuing the player at a speed of 140. Its 50 damage per hit makes the initial ambush strike potentially fatal if the player is not at full health.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones, most common in Dark Citadel
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Shadow Stalkers lurk throughout the Void Dimension but are especially common in the Dark Citadel zone where the low visibility plays to their ambush strengths.

## Strategy

Always explore the Void Dimension with full health and your best armor equipped. The Shadow Stalker's ambush deals 50 damage, so you need to survive the initial hit. Once revealed, it becomes a straightforward but extremely dangerous melee fight. Its 50% knockback resistance means you cannot push it away, so focus on kiting with ranged weapons or timing hit-and-retreat with melee. Void-tier weapons are almost essential for dealing with its 200 HP pool efficiently. The rare Chaos Shard drop makes Shadow Stalkers a valuable but risky farming target.

## Special Traits

- **Shadow Ambush:** The Shadow Stalker is invisible until a player enters its trigger range, delivering a devastating surprise attack.
- **50% Knockback Resistance:** Resists half of all knockback, making it impossible to create distance through melee hits alone.
- **High Damage:** At 50 damage per hit, the Shadow Stalker has one of the highest damage values among regular (non-boss) enemies.
