The Mountain Hawk is the fastest surface enemy in Starfall, clocking a speed of 100 as it dives from the peaks of the Mountains biome. Its extreme speed makes it the most difficult swooping enemy to dodge on the surface, though its moderate HP and damage keep it from being overwhelming. The mountainous terrain adds extra challenge, as uneven ground makes dodging the hawk's dives trickier.

## Behavior

The Mountain Hawk uses a **swoop** AI pattern. It soars high above the mountain peaks and dives at the player at extreme speed. At speed 100, it is the fastest swooping enemy on the surface, making its dives very difficult to react to. After diving, it pulls up and circles quickly for another pass. The frequency of its attacks is high due to its speed.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Mountains biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Mountain Hawks are exclusive to the Mountains biome surface. The uneven, elevated terrain of the mountains makes their swooping dives especially dangerous since there is often nowhere flat to dodge.

## Strategy

The Mountain Hawk's extreme speed makes timing essential. Watch for the dive and dodge at the last possible moment, as dodging too early lets the hawk adjust its trajectory. Melee weapons with a wide swing arc give you the best chance of connecting during the brief window when the hawk passes by. Ranged weapons are effective during the circling phase between dives. Try to fight on the flattest ground you can find in the Mountains biome to give yourself room to dodge. At 30 HP, the hawk dies quickly once you land hits.

## Special Traits

- **Highest Surface Speed:** At speed 100, the Mountain Hawk has the fastest movement speed of any surface enemy in the game, making it the most challenging swooper to dodge.
