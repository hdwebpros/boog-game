Sporelings are small, fast creatures that only appear when a Fungal Shambler is killed. Three Sporelings burst from the Shambler's remains and scatter in different directions at high speed. While individually weak, their sudden appearance and speed can catch players off guard, especially in confined spaces. They despawn automatically after 6 seconds.

## Behavior

Sporelings move rapidly in random directions upon spawning. They deal contact damage and are difficult to track due to their small size and high speed. They do not use any particular AI pattern beyond fast movement. They cannot be encountered through natural spawning -- they only appear from dying Fungal Shamblers.

## Location

- **Layers:** Underground, Deep Underground (wherever Fungal Shamblers spawn)
- **Biomes:** All (underground)
- **Spawn conditions:** Only spawned by a dying Fungal Shambler, never spawns naturally

Sporelings exist only as a byproduct of killing Fungal Shamblers. You will never encounter one without first encountering its parent.

## Drops

| Item | Quantity | Chance |

## Strategy

Sporelings are fragile with only 10 HP, so any weapon kills them in one or two hits. The challenge is their speed and the fact that three appear simultaneously. Wide-arc melee swings are ideal for catching multiple Sporelings at once. Alternatively, simply retreat for 6 seconds and they will despawn on their own. Do not waste resources chasing them unless you need the XP or Plant Fiber.

## Special Traits

- **Spawned Only:** Sporelings never spawn naturally. They are created exclusively by the death of a Fungal Shambler.
- **Timed Despawn:** Sporelings automatically despawn after 6 seconds, making them a temporary threat you can simply outlast.
