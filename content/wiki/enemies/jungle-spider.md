The Jungle Spider is a stealthy predator found exclusively in the Jungle biome. It lies in wait, camouflaged among the dense vegetation, and springs at players who wander too close. Its ambush attack deals solid damage and its moderate speed makes it difficult to escape once triggered. The thick jungle terrain compounds the danger by limiting escape routes.

## Behavior

The Jungle Spider uses an **ambush** AI pattern. It remains hidden and motionless among the jungle foliage, nearly invisible to the player. When a player enters its trigger range, the spider springs to life and attacks aggressively. Once revealed, it pursues the player at a speed of 65, making it a persistent threat in the dense jungle environment.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Jungle biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Jungle Spiders are exclusive to the Jungle biome surface. They are moderately common and their camouflage makes them difficult to spot before triggering. They often share territory with Vine Stranglers.

## Strategy

Move carefully through the Jungle biome with your weapon ready. Since Jungle Spiders are ambush predators, the first hit often belongs to them. Keep your health topped up while exploring to survive the initial strike. Once triggered, the spider is a straightforward melee fight at 35 HP. A good mid-tier weapon dispatches it quickly. The generous Plant Fiber and Healing Herb drops make them a productive enemy to farm if you can handle the ambush consistently.

## Special Traits

- **Camouflage Ambush:** The Jungle Spider is hidden among jungle vegetation until a player triggers its ambush range. Unlike the Mimic which disguises as a chest, the spider simply blends into the natural environment.
