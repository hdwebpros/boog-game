The Hellfire Imp is the fastest enemy in the entire game at speed 200. This small demonic creature zips through the Void Dimension at blinding speed, hurling fire projectiles at the player from a distance. While its 80 HP is relatively low for a void enemy, its extreme speed and ranged attacks make it incredibly difficult to pin down and eliminate.

## Behavior

The Hellfire Imp uses a **ranged** AI pattern. It flies at extreme speed, maintaining maximum distance from the player while firing fire projectiles. At speed 200, it is the single fastest creature in Starfall, easily outrunning any player. It kites aggressively, constantly repositioning to maintain its preferred attack range.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones, most common in Hellfire Caverns
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Hellfire Imps are found throughout the Void Dimension and are especially prevalent in the Hellfire Caverns zone.

## Strategy

Chasing a Hellfire Imp is futile -- at speed 200, it will always outrun you. The best approach is to use ranged weapons and trade fire from cover, using void terrain as shields against its projectiles. Alternatively, corner it in tight cavern spaces where its speed advantage is negated. Magic weapons with tracking can be effective. Its relatively low 80 HP means it dies quickly once you consistently land hits. The Hellfire Core drop is needed for void crafting, making these a necessary farming target.

## Special Traits

- **Fastest Enemy:** At speed 200, the Hellfire Imp is the fastest creature in the game. No player can outrun it.
- **Fire Projectiles:** Shoots fire at the player from range, dealing 30 damage per hit. Combined with its speed, this creates a frustrating kiting dynamic.
