The Dark Knight is the single most dangerous regular enemy in Starfall. With 350 HP, a staggering 70 damage per hit, 60% knockback resistance, and the richest drop table of any void enemy, it is the ultimate test of combat skill. These armored void warriors patrol the darkest reaches of the Void Dimension, and encountering one unprepared is almost certainly fatal.

## Behavior

The Dark Knight uses a **patrol** AI pattern. It marches back and forth through the void with deliberate purpose, dealing devastating contact damage to anything in its path. At speed 100, it moves at a brisk pace that keeps pressure on players. Its patrol pattern may seem simple, but the Dark Knight's raw stats turn even a basic patrol enemy into a lethal threat. At 70 damage, a single touch can take most of a player's health.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones, most common in Dark Citadel
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Dark Knights are relatively rare but appear throughout the Void Dimension. They are most frequently found in the Dark Citadel zone, where multiple knights may patrol the nether brick corridors simultaneously.

## Strategy

Never engage a Dark Knight without end-game equipment. At 70 damage per hit, even void-tier armor will struggle to keep you alive through multiple contacts. Ranged combat is the safest approach -- keep maximum distance and whittle down its 350 HP over time. If using melee, treat it like a slower but deadlier Nether Golem: jump over it, hit from behind, and retreat before it turns. Its 60% knockback resistance prevents effective melee control. The fight will be long and resource-intensive regardless of strategy. The Dark Knight has the best overall drop table in the Void Dimension, with a 10% Dimensional Fabric chance and consistent Abyssal Ingot and Chaos Shard drops, making it the premier farming target for players with the gear to handle it.

## Special Traits

- **Highest Damage Regular Enemy:** At 70 damage per hit, the Dark Knight deals more contact damage than any other non-boss enemy in the game.
- **60% Knockback Resistance:** Resists the majority of knockback, making melee positioning extremely difficult.
- **Best Void Drop Table:** The Dark Knight is the only regular enemy that drops Abyssal Ingots, Chaos Shards, and Dimensional Fabric simultaneously, making it the most rewarding enemy to farm.
- **Highest XP:** At 220 XP, the Dark Knight grants more experience than any other regular enemy.
