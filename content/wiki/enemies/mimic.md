The Mimic is a cunning predator that disguises itself as a treasure chest in underground and deep underground tunnels. Players who approach expecting loot are instead met with a vicious surprise attack dealing 30 damage -- one of the highest contact damage values among non-void enemies. The Mimic is a classic dungeon-crawling trap and a strong incentive to approach underground chests with caution.

## Behavior

The Mimic uses an **ambush** AI pattern. It sits completely motionless, appearing as an ordinary treasure chest. When a player approaches within close range, the Mimic springs to life, revealing its true form and immediately attacking. Once revealed, it moves aggressively toward the player at moderate speed. The initial ambush strike is the most dangerous moment, as it often catches players completely off guard.

## Location

- **Layers:** Underground, Deep Underground
- **Biomes:** All (underground)
- **Spawn conditions:** Any time

Mimics are relatively rare but can appear anywhere in the underground and deep underground. Their disguise makes them impossible to spot until you are already within ambush range.

## Strategy

There is no reliable way to identify a Mimic before triggering it. Approach all underground chests with full health and your weapon ready. The moment the Mimic springs, immediately dodge backward to avoid the initial hit, then counter-attack. With 60 HP, the Mimic is durable but not excessively so -- a few solid hits with a mid-tier weapon will bring it down. Ranged weapons let you safely kite it after the initial reveal. The generous drop table (Iron Bars, Diamonds, and Healing Herbs) makes Mimics rewarding to kill despite the ambush danger.

## Special Traits

- **Chest Disguise:** The Mimic is visually identical to a treasure chest until a player enters its ambush range. There is no way to distinguish it from a real chest before triggering the ambush.
- **High Initial Damage:** At 30 damage, the Mimic's attack is among the strongest of any non-void, non-boss enemy, making the ambush particularly punishing.
