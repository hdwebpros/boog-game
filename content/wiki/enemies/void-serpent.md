The Void Serpent is the void counterpart of the Lava Serpent, but vastly more powerful. With 300 HP and 45 damage, this colossal serpent emerges from the void's terrain to deliver devastating strikes. Its emerge attacks are nearly impossible to anticipate, and its high HP makes each encounter a prolonged and dangerous fight.

## Behavior

The Void Serpent uses an **emerge** AI pattern. It hides beneath the void terrain, completely invisible to the player. When a player walks near its hiding spot, it erupts from the ground, dealing heavy contact damage before retreating back below the surface. At speed 120, its emergence is extremely fast, giving almost no reaction time. Unlike the Lava Serpent which is limited to lava pools, the Void Serpent can emerge from any void terrain.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones, most common in Void Forest
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Void Serpents are found throughout the Void Dimension and are especially common in the Void Forest zone where dense terrain provides ample hiding spots.

## Strategy

Constant movement is your best defense. The Void Serpent's emerge attack is nearly impossible to predict, so never stand still in the Void Dimension. When a serpent emerges, immediately attack with your strongest weapon while it is above ground -- it will retreat back below the surface quickly. Ranged weapons allow you to get more hits in during each emergence window. Its 300 HP means you will need multiple emergence cycles to kill it, requiring patience. The high Void Essence drop rate (80% for 5) makes Void Serpents the best Void Essence farm in the game, and the rare 5% Dimensional Fabric drop is a valuable bonus.

## Special Traits

- **Terrain Emergence:** Unlike the Lava Serpent which is confined to lava, the Void Serpent can emerge from any void terrain, making it impossible to predict where it will strike.
- **Best Void Essence Source:** With an 80% chance to drop 5 Void Essence, the Void Serpent is the single best source of this critical crafting material.
- **Rare Dimensional Fabric:** Has a 5% chance to drop the extremely rare Dimensional Fabric material.
