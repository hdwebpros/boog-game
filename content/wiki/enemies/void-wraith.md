The Void Wraith is the void counterpart of the Phantom Wraith, but far more dangerous. With 120 HP, 35 damage, and a blistering speed of 180, it is a terrifying presence in the Void Dimension. Like its overworld cousin, it alternates between tangible and intangible states, but its massively increased stats make each encounter far more punishing.

## Behavior

The Void Wraith uses a **phase** AI pattern. It flies at extreme speed, alternating between tangible and intangible states. When tangible, it can deal and receive damage. When intangible, it is completely immune to damage but continues moving and can still overlap with the player. Its speed of 180 means it closes distances almost instantly, giving players very little time to react when it becomes tangible and attackable.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Void Wraiths appear throughout the Void Dimension and are one of the more common void enemies. They can appear in any of the four void biome zones.

## Strategy

The same patience required for Phantom Wraiths applies here, but the margin for error is razor-thin. Only attack during tangible phases, and use the highest-damage weapon you have to minimize the number of vulnerability windows you need. Ranged weapons are strongly recommended due to the wraith's extreme speed making melee timing very difficult. Void-tier weapons are highly effective. Keep moving constantly to avoid being caught stationary when it phases in on top of you. The Void Essence drops are essential for void crafting.

## Special Traits

- **Phase Shifting:** Alternates between tangible and intangible states. Immune to all damage while intangible. This is the same mechanic as the Phantom Wraith but at drastically higher stats.
- **Extreme Speed:** At 180 speed, the Void Wraith is one of the fastest enemies in the game, making its tangible-phase attacks very difficult to dodge.
