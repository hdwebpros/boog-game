The Space Slug is one of the first enemies you will encounter in Starfall. These sluggish alien creatures crawl across the surface and into shallow underground tunnels, posing a minimal threat to even freshly crash-landed players. While not particularly dangerous on their own, they can catch you off guard if you are low on health or distracted by mining.

## Behavior

The Space Slug uses a simple **patrol** AI pattern. It moves back and forth along the ground in a fixed area, reversing direction when it hits a wall or reaches the edge of a platform. It does not actively pursue the player and will only deal damage on direct contact.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface, Underground
- **Biomes:** All surface biomes
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Space Slugs are extremely common across the entire surface and upper underground areas, making them one of the most frequently encountered enemies in the game.

## Drops

| Item | Quantity | Chance |

## Strategy

Space Slugs are the easiest enemy in the game. Their low speed and patrol-only AI means you can simply walk up and hit them with any weapon. Even a basic wooden sword will dispatch them in a few hits. They make good practice targets for learning combat timing. Since they drop Plant Fiber, farming them early on can help you stock up on basic crafting materials.

## Special Traits

The Space Slug has no special traits. It is a straightforward enemy with no quirks or surprises.
