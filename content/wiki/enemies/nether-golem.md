The Nether Golem is the tankiest regular enemy in Starfall. With a massive 400 HP, 60 damage per hit, and 80% knockback resistance, it is an almost immovable wall of void stone that charges through anything in its path. Only the Void Dimension's bosses exceed its raw stats. Fighting a Nether Golem is a war of attrition that demands top-tier equipment and patience.

## Behavior

The Nether Golem uses a **charge** AI pattern. It lumbers forward slowly until it detects the player, then charges with surprising force. While its speed of 80 is not the fastest in the void, its enormous HP pool and devastating damage make each charge life-threatening. Its 80% knockback resistance means it barely flinches when hit, plowing through attacks to reach its target.

## Location

- **Layers:** Void Dimension (all areas)
- **Biomes:** All Void Dimension biome zones, most common in Ashen Wastes
- **Spawn conditions:** Void Dimension only

Nether Golems are found throughout the Void Dimension but are most frequently encountered in the Ashen Wastes zone where open terrain gives them room to charge.

## Strategy

Do not try to tank a Nether Golem's hits -- at 60 damage, even void-tier armor will not protect you for long. The key is mobility. Jump over its charges and attack from behind while it recovers. Ranged weapons are the safest option, allowing you to chip away at its 400 HP from a distance. Magic weapons with high DPS are ideal for burning through its massive health pool. Its 80% knockback resistance makes melee combat extremely dangerous, as you cannot push it back at all. The fight will be long regardless of your approach, so bring plenty of healing. Abyssal Ingot drops make this one of the most rewarding regular enemies to farm.

## Special Traits

- **80% Knockback Resistance:** The Nether Golem resists nearly all knockback, making it essentially immovable. Melee fighters cannot control its positioning.
- **Highest HP Regular Enemy:** At 400 HP, the Nether Golem has more health than any other non-boss enemy in the game.
- **Devastating Damage:** 60 damage per hit is the second-highest among regular enemies, behind only the Dark Knight.
