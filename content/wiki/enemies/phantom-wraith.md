The Phantom Wraith is a ghostly enemy found in the deep underground and core layers. Its defining trait is the ability to phase between tangible and intangible states, making it immune to damage part of the time. This phasing mechanic forces players to time their attacks carefully, turning each encounter into a test of patience and timing.

## Behavior

The Phantom Wraith uses a **phase** AI pattern. It flies through the air, alternating between two states: tangible and intangible. When tangible, it glows brightly and can both deal and receive damage. When intangible, it becomes translucent and is completely immune to all damage while still moving toward the player. The wraith switches states on a regular timer, creating windows of vulnerability that players must exploit.

## Location

- **Layers:** Deep Underground, Core
- **Biomes:** All (deep underground)
- **Spawn conditions:** Any time

Phantom Wraiths are moderately rare but appear consistently throughout the deep underground and core layers. They often appear in open caverns where their flight and phasing make them particularly difficult to corner.

## Strategy

Patience is essential. Watch the Wraith's visual state closely -- attack only when it is tangible (brightly glowing). Hitting it while intangible wastes time and positions you dangerously close. Ranged weapons are ideal because you can stay at a safe distance and fire during tangible windows. For melee, approach during tangible phases, land your hits, then back off before it becomes intangible and potentially passes through you. The 25% Diamond drop rate makes Phantom Wraiths one of the better sources of Diamonds in the game.

## Special Traits

- **Phase Shifting:** The Phantom Wraith alternates between tangible and intangible states. While intangible, it is completely immune to damage but can still move and overlap with the player. This is the only overworld enemy with damage immunity phases.
