The Cave Bat is a fast-moving aerial enemy found in underground and deep underground tunnels. Despite its low health, its high speed and swooping attack pattern make it a nuisance, especially when you are focused on mining or navigating tight cave systems. They often attack in groups, compounding the threat.

## Behavior

The Cave Bat uses a **swoop** AI pattern. It flies overhead, then rapidly dives toward the player at an angle before pulling back up and circling for another pass. The dive is fast and can be difficult to dodge in narrow tunnels where movement is restricted.

## Location

- **Layers:** Underground, Deep Underground
- **Biomes:** All (underground)
- **Spawn conditions:** Any time (underground has no day/night cycle)

Cave Bats become more common the deeper you go, and they are a staple enemy throughout underground exploration.

## Strategy

The Cave Bat's low HP means any weapon will kill it quickly -- the challenge is landing a hit. Melee weapons with a wide swing arc work well since the bat moves fast. Ranged weapons are effective if you can lead your shot. Try to fight in open caverns rather than narrow tunnels where dodging is harder. A well-timed melee swing as the bat dives toward you is the most reliable approach.

## Special Traits

The Cave Bat has no special traits. It is a standard swooping enemy with high speed but fragile health.
