The Vampire is a fast-flying nocturnal enemy that appears on the surface only at night. With high speed and a swooping attack, it is significantly more dangerous than daytime surface enemies. Vampires add real danger to nighttime exploration and are a strong incentive to build shelter before darkness falls, especially for newer players.

## Behavior

The Vampire uses a **swoop** AI pattern. It flies high above the player, then dives sharply downward at high speed to deal contact damage. After the dive, it pulls back up and circles for another pass. Its speed of 90 makes it one of the fastest swooping enemies, and the dives come quickly and frequently.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** All surface biomes
- **Spawn conditions:** Night only

Vampires spawn exclusively at night on the surface. They despawn or stop spawning once daylight returns. Multiple vampires can appear simultaneously, making nighttime surface travel hazardous.

## Strategy

Vampires are fast but predictable. Watch for the dive pattern and dodge sideways just before impact. A well-timed melee swing during the dive can catch the Vampire as it passes. Ranged weapons are effective for picking them off before they begin their dive. If overwhelmed by multiple vampires, retreat underground or into a shelter until dawn. The generous Healing Herb drops make Vampires a useful source of healing items if you can handle them.

## Special Traits

- **Nocturnal:** Vampires only appear at night, making them the primary nighttime surface threat.
