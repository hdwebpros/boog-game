The Fish is a completely passive ocean creature that poses no threat whatsoever. With 0 damage and only 8 HP, it is the weakest creature in Starfall. Fish swim aimlessly through ocean waters and serve primarily as ambient life, though they can be killed for a small amount of Sand and XP.

## Behavior

The Fish uses a **lure** AI pattern but with 0 damage, making it functionally passive. It floats through the water in gentle patterns, never actively attacking the player. It may drift toward the player but contact deals no damage.

## Location

- **Layers:** Ocean
- **Biomes:** Ocean / Lake (underwater areas)
- **Spawn conditions:** Ocean tiles only, passive

Fish are found exclusively in ocean and lake water, swimming peacefully among the more dangerous Anglerfish.

## Drops

| Item | Quantity | Chance |
|------|----------|--------|
| Sand | 1 | 60% |

## Strategy

No strategy is needed. Fish cannot hurt you. Simply swing any weapon to dispatch them if you want the Sand drop or the 2 XP. They can be useful for brand-new players looking to gain their very first experience points safely.

## Special Traits

- **Passive:** The Fish deals 0 damage and will never attack the player, making it the only truly harmless creature in the game.
