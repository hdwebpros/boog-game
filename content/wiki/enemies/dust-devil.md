The Dust Devil is a fast-moving ranged enemy found exclusively in the Desert biome. It whirls across the sand at high speed while firing projectiles at the player from a distance. Its combination of speed and ranged attacks makes it one of the more annoying biome-specific enemies to deal with, constantly kiting away from approaching players.

## Behavior

The Dust Devil uses a **ranged** AI pattern. It flies erratically across the desert, maintaining distance from the player while periodically firing projectiles. Its speed of 90 makes it very difficult to chase down, and it actively retreats when the player approaches. The projectiles travel in a straight line toward the player's position.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Desert biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Dust Devils are exclusive to the Desert biome surface and are moderately common there. They frequently appear alongside Sand Scorpions.

## Strategy

Ranged weapons are the best counter to the Dust Devil's kiting behavior. Trading projectiles eliminates the need to chase it down. If using melee, try to corner it against terrain or a wall where it cannot retreat. Its low HP of 30 means it dies quickly once you land hits. In mixed encounters with Sand Scorpions, prioritize the Dust Devil first since it can damage you from range while you fight the scorpion. Dodge its projectiles by moving perpendicular to the line of fire.

## Special Traits

- **Projectile Attack:** The Dust Devil fires ranged projectiles, making it one of the few surface biome enemies with a ranged attack.
