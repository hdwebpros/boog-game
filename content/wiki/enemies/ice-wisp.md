The Ice Wisp is a fast-flying elemental spirit found in the Snow biome. It swoops at players from above with high speed, though its low HP and damage make it more of an annoyance than a serious threat. Ice Wisps often appear alongside Frost Wolves, creating a combined ground-and-air assault in the frozen tundra.

## Behavior

The Ice Wisp uses a **swoop** AI pattern. It floats high above the snow-covered terrain, then dives rapidly toward the player. Its speed of 85 makes it one of the faster swooping enemies, but its low damage of 10 means each hit is not particularly punishing. The wisp circles after each dive and quickly lines up another attack.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Snow biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Ice Wisps are exclusive to the Snow biome surface and are fairly common there. They frequently appear alongside Frost Wolves.

## Strategy

With only 25 HP, Ice Wisps die quickly to any weapon. The challenge is landing a hit on such a fast target. Time your melee swing for when the wisp reaches the bottom of its dive arc. Ranged weapons can pick them off during the circling phase between dives. In mixed encounters with Frost Wolves, deal with the wolves first since they hit harder, then clean up the wisps. The Ice drops are useful if you need that material for crafting.

## Special Traits

The Ice Wisp has no special traits. It is a standard swooping enemy with high speed but low health and damage, exclusive to the Snow biome.
