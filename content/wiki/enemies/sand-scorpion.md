The Sand Scorpion is a durable ground predator found exclusively in the Desert biome. With 45 HP and 18 damage, it is one of the stronger biome-specific surface enemies. Its charge attack is moderately fast and hits hard, making Desert exploration dangerous for players without decent armor or weapons.

## Behavior

The Sand Scorpion uses a **charge** AI pattern. It patrols the desert surface and charges at the player when they come within detection range. Its charge speed of 55 is moderate, giving players a reasonable window to dodge or prepare a counterattack. The scorpion is sturdy enough to survive several hits, so sustained combat is typical.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Desert biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Sand Scorpions are exclusive to the Desert biome surface. They are fairly common and often appear alongside Dust Devils, creating a mixed melee-and-ranged threat.

## Strategy

Sand Scorpions are straightforward melee enemies. Jump over their charge and attack from behind, or meet them head-on with a strong weapon. Their moderate speed gives you time to react to the charge. In mixed encounters with Dust Devils, close the gap to the Dust Devil first (since it shoots projectiles) while dodging the scorpion's charge. The Sand drops in quantity make scorpions a decent farming target if you need Sand for crafting.

## Special Traits

The Sand Scorpion has no special traits. It is a solid, conventional charge enemy exclusive to the Desert biome.
