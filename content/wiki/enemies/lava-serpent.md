The Lava Serpent is a deadly creature that lurks beneath lava pools in the deep underground and core layers. It erupts from the molten rock to strike at passing players, dealing heavy damage before retreating back into the lava. Its emerge-and-retreat pattern makes it one of the more unpredictable enemies in the game's lower layers.

## Behavior

The Lava Serpent uses an **emerge** AI pattern. It hides beneath the surface of lava pools, completely invisible to the player. When a player walks near the edge of a lava pool or crosses over one, the serpent bursts upward, dealing contact damage before sinking back down. The emerge is fast and difficult to react to if you are not expecting it.

## Location

- **Layers:** Deep Underground, Core (down to world bottom)
- **Biomes:** All (deep underground)
- **Spawn conditions:** Requires nearby lava tiles

Lava Serpents are tied to lava pools, so they are most common in the deep underground and core layers where lava is abundant. You will never encounter one away from lava.

## Strategy

The best defense against Lava Serpents is awareness. Avoid walking directly along the edges of lava pools, and keep moving when crossing near lava. If you see a serpent emerge, quickly back away and attack with a ranged weapon while it is above the surface. Melee combat is risky because the serpent retreats back into the lava quickly, and standing near lava puts you at risk of falling in. The Titanium Ore drops make Lava Serpents a valuable target for players farming high-tier materials.

## Special Traits

- **Lava Emergence:** The Lava Serpent hides in lava pools and is invisible until it attacks, making it impossible to spot before it strikes for the first time.
