The Vine Strangler is a slow-moving but durable plant creature found in the Jungle biome. With 50 HP and 50% knockback resistance, it is a tanky presence on the jungle floor that is difficult to push around. While its damage and speed are modest, its durability and knockback resistance make it a persistent obstacle during Jungle exploration.

## Behavior

The Vine Strangler uses a **patrol** AI pattern. It moves slowly back and forth along the jungle floor, dealing contact damage to players it touches. It does not actively chase or pursue, but its tanky nature means it takes a while to kill and cannot be easily shoved out of the way with melee knockback.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Jungle biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Vine Stranglers are exclusive to the Jungle biome surface and are moderately common. They often block pathways in the dense jungle, forcing players to either fight through or find an alternate route.

## Strategy

Vine Stranglers are slow enough that you can often simply walk past them if you are in a hurry. When you do engage, be aware that its 50% knockback resistance means melee attacks will not push it back effectively. Ranged weapons are the cleanest option, allowing you to whittle it down from a safe distance. For melee, hit and retreat repeatedly rather than standing in contact range. The high drop rates on Wood and Plant Fiber make Vine Stranglers excellent for farming basic materials.

## Special Traits

- **50% Knockback Resistance:** The Vine Strangler resists half of all knockback, making it difficult to control its position with melee attacks. You must move around it rather than pushing it away.
