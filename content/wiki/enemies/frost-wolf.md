The Frost Wolf is a fast, aggressive predator found exclusively in the Snow biome. It charges at players with high speed, making it one of the more threatening surface enemies. Frost Wolves are often encountered in pairs or small groups, which can quickly overwhelm an unprepared traveler in the frozen tundra.

## Behavior

The Frost Wolf uses a **charge** AI pattern. It patrols the snowy surface and, upon detecting the player, rushes forward at high speed to deal contact damage. Its charge speed of 70 makes it significantly faster than the Rock Golem's charge, giving players less time to react. After charging, it briefly resets before potentially charging again.

## Location

- **Layers:** Surface
- **Biomes:** Snow biome only
- **Spawn conditions:** Day and night

Frost Wolves are exclusive to the Snow biome surface. They do not appear underground or in any other biome.

## Strategy

Frost Wolves are fast but not especially durable at 40 HP. A strong melee weapon can kill them in a few hits if you time your swings to connect as they charge in. Jump over the charge and attack from behind for a safe approach. Ranged weapons work well for picking them off before they close the gap. When facing multiple wolves, try to line them up so they charge in sequence rather than surrounding you. The Cooked Meat drop is a useful food source during Snow biome exploration.

## Special Traits

The Frost Wolf has no special traits beyond its Snow biome exclusivity and fast charge speed.
