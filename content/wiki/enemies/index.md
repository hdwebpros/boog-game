# Bestiary

The world of Starfall is teeming with hostile creatures. From the surface down to the planet's core, and even into the Void Dimension itself, dangers lurk around every corner. This bestiary catalogs every known creature, their behaviors, and how to deal with them.

## Surface Enemies

These creatures roam the surface world and are generally the first threats you will encounter.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_space_slug.png){width=32} [Space Slug](/wiki/enemies/space-slug) -- A slow, lumbering creature that patrols the surface and shallow underground.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_vampire.png){width=32} [Vampire](/wiki/enemies/vampire) -- A fast nocturnal predator that swoops down on unsuspecting players at night.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_gloom_moth.png){width=32} [Gloom Moth](/wiki/enemies/gloom-moth) -- A winged insect that is passive during daylight but turns aggressive after dark.

## Underground Enemies

Found beneath the surface, these enemies pose a greater challenge to spelunkers.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_cave_bat.png){width=32} [Cave Bat](/wiki/enemies/cave-bat) -- A quick flyer that dives at players in underground tunnels.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_rock_golem.png){width=32} [Rock Golem](/wiki/enemies/rock-golem) -- A heavily armored stone creature that charges at intruders.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_fungal_shambler.png){width=32} [Fungal Shambler](/wiki/enemies/fungal-shambler) -- A mushroom creature that splits into Sporelings on death.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_sporeling.png){width=32} [Sporeling](/wiki/enemies/sporeling) -- Tiny spore creatures spawned by a dying Fungal Shambler.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_mimic.png){width=32} [Mimic](/wiki/enemies/mimic) -- A deceptive creature disguised as a treasure chest.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_shock_beetle.png){width=32} [Shock Beetle](/wiki/enemies/shock-beetle) -- An armored beetle that retaliates with electric projectiles when struck.

## Ocean Enemies

These creatures inhabit the ocean depths.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_anglerfish.png){width=32} [Anglerfish](/wiki/enemies/anglerfish) -- A deep-sea predator that lures prey with its bioluminescent light.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_fish.png){width=32} [Fish](/wiki/enemies/fish) -- A harmless passive creature found swimming in ocean waters.

## Deep Underground Enemies

The deeper caverns hold far more dangerous foes.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_phantom_wraith.png){width=32} [Phantom Wraith](/wiki/enemies/phantom-wraith) -- A spectral entity that phases between tangible and intangible states.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_lava_serpent.png){width=32} [Lava Serpent](/wiki/enemies/lava-serpent) -- A fiery snake that emerges from lava pools to strike.

## Core Enemies

At the planet's core, only the most powerful surface-world enemies survive.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_corrupted_drone.png){width=32} [Corrupted Drone](/wiki/enemies/corrupted-drone) -- A malfunctioning machine that fires projectiles from a distance.

## Biome-Specific Enemies

These creatures are adapted to specific surface biomes.

### Snow Biome
- ![](/sprites/enemy_frost_wolf.png){width=32} [Frost Wolf](/wiki/enemies/frost-wolf) -- A swift predator that charges through the frozen tundra.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_ice_wisp.png){width=32} [Ice Wisp](/wiki/enemies/ice-wisp) -- A floating spirit of cold that swoops at travelers.

### Desert Biome
- ![](/sprites/enemy_sand_scorpion.png){width=32} [Sand Scorpion](/wiki/enemies/sand-scorpion) -- A burrowing arachnid that charges from the desert sands.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_dust_devil.png){width=32} [Dust Devil](/wiki/enemies/dust-devil) -- A whirling sand spirit that attacks from range.

### Jungle Biome
- ![](/sprites/enemy_jungle_spider.png){width=32} [Jungle Spider](/wiki/enemies/jungle-spider) -- A camouflaged predator that ambushes from the canopy.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_vine_strangler.png){width=32} [Vine Strangler](/wiki/enemies/vine-strangler) -- A thick, plant-like creature that patrols the jungle floor.

### Mountains Biome
- ![](/sprites/enemy_mountain_hawk.png){width=32} [Mountain Hawk](/wiki/enemies/mountain-hawk) -- The fastest flying enemy on the surface, diving from mountain peaks.

## Void Dimension Enemies

These otherworldly horrors inhabit the Void Dimension. They are drastically more powerful than any surface creature.

- ![](/sprites/enemy_void_wraith.png){width=32} [Void Wraith](/wiki/enemies/void-wraith) -- A spectral terror that phases through solid matter in the void.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_shadow_stalker.png){width=32} [Shadow Stalker](/wiki/enemies/shadow-stalker) -- A massive ambush predator lurking in void shadows.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_hellfire_imp.png){width=32} [Hellfire Imp](/wiki/enemies/hellfire-imp) -- A swift demonic creature that hurls fire from a distance.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_nether_golem.png){width=32} [Nether Golem](/wiki/enemies/nether-golem) -- An immense construct of void stone, nearly immovable.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_soul_eater.png){width=32} [Soul Eater](/wiki/enemies/soul-eater) -- A flying void creature that feeds on life force.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_void_serpent.png){width=32} [Void Serpent](/wiki/enemies/void-serpent) -- A colossal serpent that emerges from the void's depths.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_chaos_elemental.png){width=32} [Chaos Elemental](/wiki/enemies/chaos-elemental) -- A being of pure chaotic energy that attacks from range.
- ![](/sprites/enemy_dark_knight.png){width=32} [Dark Knight](/wiki/enemies/dark-knight) -- An armored warrior of the void, the most dangerous regular enemy in the game.
